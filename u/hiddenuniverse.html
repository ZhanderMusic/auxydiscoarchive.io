Greetings from Singapore, a lil’tropical island in Asia

An aimless weekend afternoon exploring random music apps awakened a dormant passion with music making. Being able to create something that resonates with my senses became therapeutical, uplifting and enriching.

While exploring Auxy, it showed me how infinite music making could be and I have never stopped using Auxy ever since as a platform to practice and hopefully meliorate my music to another level.

Most importantly, to produce music that is of deserving quality to be shared with anyone who appreciates my style - which is somewhat organic and experimental, at times random or rather, in a wtf sorta way!

Do drop by my soundcloud, hear how it’s like. Hope it resonates with you somehow. I look forward to your feedback, especially in areas that I need improvement with.

Be frank, be honest, just be you - just as I am, who I am

Or if you have simliar experiences that started your path into an awakening, creating music, and so on to share, let’s chat on.

Let’s all create music to heal and connect with as many people as possible; to bridge the gaps thats dividing us from each other.
