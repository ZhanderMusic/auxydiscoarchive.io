Matik

Hey! My name’s Valerie, also known as Matik for my producer name and vivesdotpng as my artists name. I’m based in Melbourne, Australia, and I’m currently studying a Bachelors in Animation at RMIT University!
As of 2020, I’ve left the Auxy scene and have moved on to work on bigger projects such as my art career and creating my portfolio in hopes of getting into an animation company as a Storyboard Artist.
I’ve been composing on Auxy and interacting with the community since 2017, and I’ve had great fun getting to connect with other likeminded producers, however I do not see myself focusing on music production as a career in the future, which in doing so, has led to me losing touch with this amazing community.
Albeit, I hope that you’ll continue to support me on my art journey! You can find my insta here and here! To whom all I’ve met during my time with Auxy, I hope you are all doing well! I will still be making music here and there, but I will no longer be active on this forum nor my Soundcloud and Instagram for MatikAuxy.

‘Till next time!
