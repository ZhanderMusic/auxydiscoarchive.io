Auxy Disco

WarpNode

Starting out as a guitar player when I was 14, I immediately started writing songs as a way to practice guitar. Since then I’ve written dozens of songs and realised how much I enjoy the art of composing. It wasn’t until I discovered the Auxy app however that I could fully express and implement the ideas that always seemed to evade my imagination. Now I am hooked to explore the vast world of electronic music and create my songs in different expressions of creativity.
