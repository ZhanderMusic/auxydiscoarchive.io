Are you looking for in terms of the vocal chops in Auxy, or just in general?

If you’re looking for in general, I’d recommend this guy’s tutorial. He’s the same guy who did those “How to [Genre] in 3 minutes or less!” videos on Youtube, and it’s quite helpful.

In terms of what Auxy provides… good luck. I’ve tried to do it but it just ends up too frustrating for me, due to the division of the samples being already hard enough to work with sometimes. But if you’re just going for something simple and not more lyrical, you’re probably best just studying the Intonations demo from Phluze. It really gives a good overview of how to get freaky with them in Auxy.

iammane June 8, 2019, 4:52pm #3

Yeah these are not vocals. They aren’t meant to be vocals. They’re just chops.

I stumbled across this same pack on loopmasters I think it was, was just poking around. There’s actually stems for twelve full songs in the full pack, but obviously working with full length stems in Auxy is kind of a nightmare (not just Auxy, this is a common problem in any groovebox style app that doesn’t use a linear sequencer. Gadget is in the same exact boat).

Anywho, just my two pence. They will be largely unsatisfying if you’re trying to use them as vocals.
