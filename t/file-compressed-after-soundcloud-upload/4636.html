blakkaz November 30, 2017, 5:35am #4

There are some good tips here on improving sound quality of lossy conversions.

As a starting point, I’d pull back the levels on your mix.

Auxy puts a limiter on your final mix to try and match a mastered track’s volume.
It works pretty well when exporting lossless WAVs, but can often sound pretty poor when converted to mp3.

Perhaps consider doing two mixes. One for sale, promo, DJing etc… and one for uploading to social media channels.
