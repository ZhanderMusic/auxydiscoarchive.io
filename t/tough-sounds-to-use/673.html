I find Owl actually good for backing up Climb. To get a good EDM lead, use Climb and Cube. Those two are powerful together. Cube is one of my favorites. Gravel and Pepper can be hard to use, but they are powerful once you find a good combination of leads and bass. Joker is hard to use, but with the pitch bend update, it may be useful for risers!
