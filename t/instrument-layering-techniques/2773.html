Use low pass and high pass to filter unnecessary parts of a sound, eg. filtering the bass out of a piano to allow a fat bass line to really shine. Conversely- take the top end out of a low sound to allow room for a high pitch sound to come through. This is a basic mixing technique but it really adds a lot by freeing up ‚Äúaudio realestate‚Äù- there‚Äôs only so much audio you can jam in.
