Hey Auxy-Crew,

It’s time for another Auxy Forum Unicorn Challenge.

Write a minimalistic track for any genre, using limited sounds, silence and few layers. Keep it simple. It’s more about what you don’t include, than what you do. Less can be more. What can you reduce your track down to?

Submissions close: End of November 2019.

Rules:

  • One entry per person
  • Don’t enter something you released earlier.
  • No remixes or collabs.
  • Entries must include Soundcloud links. No project links.
  • Anyone who enters an empty track or something with one note is instantly disqualified .
  • Entries can also be entered into Track of the Week.

Prize: Limited Edition Unicorn icon by your forum name for the top three entries.

Judge: MisterMaster
