Hey guys! Mr. Serpent here, and I’m just letting y’all know that I’m planning on starting something up called the ‘Auxy Radio Show’!

Here’s some stuff about it:

  1. If you’ve ever heard of auxcasts (something similar to this where we uploaded podcasts to soundcloud), this isn’t gonna be like it. Podcasts in auxcasts were permanent, but this is gonna keep a sense of raw-ness and have vc discussions where people can listen in on the hosts talking about several topics relating to auxy and having an all-around good time just discussing stuff and playing little games. Your average radio show, stuffed in to discord and about a specific wonderful app

  2. No hosts will be permanent. We’ll likely have temporary hosts per week, and out of those selected for a week not every host has to participate in every VC. In order to become a host for a week, you have to fill out a form and coordinate what times you’re able to get onto the VC. Check number 3 for further information regarding this.

  3. We have yet to create a solid schedule, but as of now VCs are planned to be hosted at 14:00 PDT (17:00 EDT) on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. There’s also an idea for nighttime VCs (for those night owls out there) hosted at 23:30 or 0:00 on Friday and Saturday nights, however this hasn’t been set into stone.

TL;DR - The Auxy Radio Show is a place where you can sign up to host discord VCs that happen on select coordinated days of the week. If you’re interested in hosting a specific block, dm me on the disco or send me a discord pm and we’ll sort things out. Thanks for your support!

We’ve already got quite a few people on the server, including Kro, SanaB, and aUstin Haga | “Official.” but we’re open to anyone who cares to participate! More info will be in the discord server.

Here’s the link: https://discord.gg/TxwdYm8
