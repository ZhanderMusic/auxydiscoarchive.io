Time to Beat the Clock!

This week’s host is @s0phont who will present the challenge below and pick his favorites on Monday.

If you’re new to Beat the Clock, here’s how it works:

  • Download the project template below.
  • Be creative and make some musics based on the rules presented by the host.
  • Upload your track to SoundCloud and post a link to it in this thread.
  • The host will listen to all submissions, so there’s no advantage in posting early.
  • On Monday, the host will pick his/her favorites, so it’s a good idea to post your submission on Sunday.

Go go go!

Hey all, I’m happy to be hosting this week’s challenge.

Not sure how many of you guys were here for the second Beat the Clock challenge, but it featured a supplied drum beat with the challenge to build a track around that. I loved that challenge as a creator, and I want to bring that kind of enthusiasm back to you guys.

I’ve put together a nice trap beat to build off of, and I’m curious to see where people take it. It features two of the original drum kits, each of which has a four bar loop that combine to make your beat. You don’t have to use the beat the whole time, in fact, I suggest a short build and a heavy drop.

I’d love if you could keep it under 30 seconds, but if your overflowing ideas cant be contained in such a short amount of time, I’m not going to count it against you. Just, don’t go too crazy. I personally think the beat sounds good at this tempo, but if you can pull some magic and morph it into something else, more power to you.

Here’s the file:
