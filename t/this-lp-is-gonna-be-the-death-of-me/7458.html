This LP is gonna be the death of me

Production Talk
DontPartyWithGuns February 10, 2018, 5:06pm #1

So some backstory, I sent my LP to this label a while back and they said they would take it if I worked on it more. I spent the last 2 1/2 months on this thing. I sent it to a couple of the submission reviewers at the label (I know them personally) and they said it was really good. As in, they would take it. SO, I sent the revised LP to the label and I got an email back yesterday. (I didnt see it until today cause I dont check my email very often :P)

drum roll please…

drrrrrrrrrrrrrrrr

They denied it.
Now usually Im not mad at labels for denying my stuff, it happens all the time, I move on and get better at creating. But what REALLY pissed me off was this;

Whoever wrote this is a prick and honestly I wanna kill them.
I spoke to the majority of the freaking submission staff and they said they would take it. THE MAJORITY SAID IT WAS OK.

Like come on man, at least give me feedback. Not giving me feedback is like saying that your music is trash and you should delete all of it and give up hope of all your dreams.

Im guessing what Im trying to say is, give people feedback, even if you think its trash.

Oh and uh, one last thing.

I cant be asked to send this to any more labels so Im self-releasing the LP next weekend.

See you then

~Noah // DPWG
