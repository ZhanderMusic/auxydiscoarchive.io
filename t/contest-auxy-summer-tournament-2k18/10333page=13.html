Mr_Mooo June 16, 2018, 10:24pm #257

https://soundcloud.com/user-460338301/golden-waves
This is about my fondest summer memory from my 8th summer. Keep in mind that I am an 8 year old for this story.

So the beginning is about waking up to a cloudless day. The first build is the car journey, and the first chorus(or drop idk) is when we (me, my twin, my mum, my dad, my cousins, their parents) first arrived, excited as an 8 year old on summer holiday. We were renting beach houses at this awesome beach. It was as tropical and beachy as England could get basically. So the second drop represents the time I caught a crab when we went crab fishing (you can note this as boring if you like but I was EIGHT and I had never been crab fishing and I CAUGHT A CRAB) so my 8 year old mind was EXCITED!! The part after that with tiny mixed with pebble and a soft guitar represents the stars. That evening our families (mine and my cousins) went to a restaurant and we got our own KIDS TABLE (oh yes). The stars part really comes in now. We walked out of the restaurant at pitch black night time and the stars were out and there were thousands. The third drop represents the bike ride back to the beach house we did. We only had 2 headlights and it was a ride through a dark forest and that was one of my greatest experiences.

The end yay.
