Reggae is not Reggaeton. It’s very different.
Reggaeton and Moombahton are similar, but Reggae is a completely different party animal.

Reggaeton is Latin (Hispanic) Pop music, made with the same beat, at 100 bpm.
Moombahton is the cheesy American version of Reggaeton.
