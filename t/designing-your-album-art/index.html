Hey Auxy artists!

I’ve received quite a few questions about my album art and I wanted to share with you my go-to place: Canva.com!

This is a very easy-to-use graphic design site with a simple interface and lots of versatile features that, with some time and effort, can really make your album art stand out! The program offers lots of tools and presets to get you started, but you can go the extra mile and do some advanced layering with your own uploads.

If you’re not looking to spend the money on an advanced design program, I highly recommend Canva. It’s totally free (unless you decide on a membership, but it’s not necessary unless you’re designing for an established business).

Have any other suggestions for where to design cool album art? Let the community know in this thread!
