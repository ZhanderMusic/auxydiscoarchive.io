iammane:

if the artwork is compelling I’m gonna click it. If it says drum and bass, I’m gonna click it. If it says dubstep, I’m gonna click it. These truths are universal and guaranteed

And the counterpoint to that is…

…if the artwork (as well as the artist name and ‘logo’) says un/non-professional or ‘just starting out’, then it’s likely to reduce the likelihood of people clicking that play button.

 
For anyone who’s serious about moving forward with their music, it’s important that you don’t let the visuals accompanying your music create the impression that you’re not serious.
