sn.ow.y July 7, 2020, 1:31pm #7

The sounds that I know might sound like a nice piano include: kiss, coin, bo, bright house piano, everything in signature pianos (especially noir in a high octave), and radiant if you really modified the sound. Highpassing them or raising the notes an octave helps in reducing the volume slightly and making the melody sound like it’s being played by a pluck as well.
