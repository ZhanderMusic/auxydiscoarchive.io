So my latest track ‘Immersion’ has done extremely well on my YouTube (when compared to my other tracks) so I wanna open the project up and allow others to Remix it.

Details:

  • Depending on how many remixes I receive, I’m looking for maybe 3 winners.

  • The more creative you are with the Remix (example: changing the genre or mood of the track) the more likely it will be chosen.

  • Winning remixes will be uploaded to my channel and full credit will be given to winners with whatever links/socials they want promoted.

  • Deadline will be November 30th

  • You may upload your version to Soundcloud as long as you make it clear it is a remix.

Hope to see some creative remixes!
