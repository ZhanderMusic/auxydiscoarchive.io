akabillposters June 18, 2018, 2:25pm #3

I’ve never done any serious promotion, but was invited to release one of my tracks on a sub-label after submitting it for feedback to a prominent(-ish) music producer YouTuber/vlogger. I declined because it wasn’t a good deal (for me).

I’ve done a fair bit of research since then and would probably formally create my own label, unless I got interest from a label big enough to be genuinely beneficial.

Simply being on a ‘label’ is relatively easy these days. Being on a proper/worthwhile label - that pro-actively works to develop promotion contacts and channels to promote and maximises interest and revenue for the track/artist - is not so easy.

I think it’s safe to say that no-one here is on that kind of label - yet.
