DontPartyWithGuns November 21, 2017, 3:10am #41

I live near Boston and my town is just greattttt (half sarcasm, half truth). Near the beach is were the posh rich people live in their million dollar houses and more west in the forest is where the poor(er) people live (I’m more middle class ;P). Our weather is just the best. Hot summers, cold winters, sharp and cold autumns, and crisp spring. The worst thing about living here is how unpredictable the temperature is. A couple weeks ago we had a storm which caused it to be 70 degrees F on Wednesday and 36 degrees F on Thursday.

Oh, did I mention the storms? Hurricanes, at least one every two years. Noreaster’s, frequently hitting my area. Blizzards, caused by unpredictable storms and weather changes. And sometimes the occasional tornado. EDIT: A tornado has never (I think) been reported in my county but Massachusetts does get tornadoes. We usually get around 5 EF0’s a year (state-wide) which basically have the same force as a big storm and don’t really cause much damage.

And yeah that’s it really lol. I’ve lived in the same town all my life, and I don’t regret it cause my town is cool even if the weather sucks. Oh well, at least we get a few snow days each year!
