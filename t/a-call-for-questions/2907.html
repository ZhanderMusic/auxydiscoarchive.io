I would say both. I want to be able to master others tracks as well as my own.

If you’d like to get a job as an engineer I’d suggest study or finding a studio willing to take you on as an apprentice.

If you just want to be able to mix your own/friends tracks you can get a long way by just learning online.

When you get a track released it’ll most likely be sent to a mastering engineer anyway, so you really only need to be able to get your music to a level that will attract labels.
