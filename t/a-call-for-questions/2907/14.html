Auxy October 4, 2017, 8:02pm #29

Great initiative!

To clarify though, Auxy is designed to have a pretty maxed out mix with little headroom, since that’s how modern tracks usually sound. We’ve spent quite a lot of time with Grammy-winning mix engineer Niklas Flyckt, who currently mixes all of Galantis’ tracks to name one example, working out settings that will make tracks sound like finished tracks in the typical genres you would create with Auxy.

We appreciate that there are styles and occasions that call for more headroom, and if this is something people are looking for, I think a better solution would be to add e.g. a setting for the master effects instead of suggesting that people turn down the levels. I can guarantee you that there’s plenty of headroom in the mix bus, and what you refer to as clipping is mainly coming from the limiter in the final mix stage.
