akabillposters February 15, 2018, 7:36pm #5

In terms of this place, at least, I like to think…

…my tendency towards overt lead melodies and solos is somewhat distinct.

Above that, my fondness for combining jazzy vibes (often via Vintage Keys) with overtly synthetic compositions could possibly be something of a signature sound.

It’s possible that the range of genres I create in are also a little outside the norm (here). (The closest thing I have to a single ‘go-to’ genre is probably ‘electro-jazz’, though I do use that as a bit of a fallback/catch-all. I’m not aware of any other Auxians creating electro-jazz [not the same as electro-swing].)

Beyond that, others might be more able to point out any unifying style that runs through my stuff. I’m not able to see it clearly.
