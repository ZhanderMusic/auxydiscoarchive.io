Auxy Sound Wiki
Label Description
Pack OG Sounds
Waveform Sine and a distorted sine wave that functions on an ADSR envelope that controls a bitcrush distortion on the waveform. Has a slight chorus effect as well.
Details One of the weirdest sounds of the OG pack, Blob is hard to use at times, but when used effectively can create a very heavy sound. It’s unwieldy but can be used very effectively in certain circumstances.
Macro: Tone Enables the ADSR envelope that controls the distorted sine waveform. This modulates the shape of the wave. No tone does not modulate the distorted sine waveform, full tone cycles through the entirety of the waveform
Macro: Shape Adjusts the attack (A) of the ADSR envelope controlling the distorted sine waveform. This means that the cycle is slower rising up at full volume, creating a different effect
Styles useful Heavy sounds, dubstep
Featured Tracks SUFOS - Cloud
