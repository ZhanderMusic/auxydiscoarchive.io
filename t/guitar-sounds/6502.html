Hey, friend. I don’t know if you’re still out and about on this post, but I had the same problem. I discovered that you could use Mental from the Disturbing Leads pack and alter the shape, tone, and pass effects of it (Shape: ~30%, Tone 50%, Lowpass: ~75%, Highpass: 25%), all while throwing in whatever reverb and pitch-bending you want, and it gave me a really nice electric guitar-esque tone.
