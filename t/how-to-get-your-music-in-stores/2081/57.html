Produk October 12, 2017, 12:09am #42

I recently got some of my music onto Spotify, and I just wanted to let everyone know how I did it.

Spotify

Produk

Produk, an artist on Spotify

From a friend, I learned about a distributor called Amuse. They’re an app. They ship out your music all for free, and you get 100% of the pay. I was like, “Why not?” So I submitted Nova. It got approved, and it shipped out a while ago. This service is truly amazing. It’s incredibly simple to use, and it works great. The only issue that I ran into is that I was sharing an artist page with someone else for a while, but I got it cleared up real fast. Amuse’s customer service is amazing.
Hit up their website at amuse.io for more info.
They have an app on the App Store, that’s what you use to upload songs.
Hit up my Spotify if you didn’t see that big link up there.
