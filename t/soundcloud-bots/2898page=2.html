You can pretty much guarantee that when accounts are deleted, any plays and repost they did are purged from the site too. To keep them would only encourage more bots, as there’d be no lasting downside to ‘getting caught’.

There’s a good chance that accounts which receive a high number of reports are quickly assessed and then, unless found to be legit*, deleted in huge batches. (I always report suspect accounts, but then that’s easy to keep on top of when I’m getting fewer than 50 plays in total in a week

Things like having a large number of follows and followers, but no likes or reposts (of other users’ tracks) and no tracks of their own, will trigger an automated spam flag and the account will be checked and flushed.

El1011:

Soundcloud i guess cant detect the device used

Fwiw… Incognito/private browsing mode typically doesn’t hide details about the device, browser or OS you’re using. It’s more about whether the sites you visit have access to other stuff like history, 3rd-party cookies or not.
(‘Mobile’ will often appear in the data that your phone shares with each site it visits. The device itself will often be a general assumption based on OS - e.g. iOS, therefore iPhone, although you can probably get more accurate by testing things known about specific devices, such screen pixel dimensions compared to pixel density)

Check out https://www.whatsmybrowser.org/ while in Incognito and see what a site can see.

Some extensions allow you to ‘spoof’/fake your device, os and browser info.
