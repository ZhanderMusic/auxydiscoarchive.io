Hello!
I am hosting a contest!
The challenge is: create a song using one sound from each pack. (Sorry non-premiums). You choose one sound from each pack, and no more. You can use that sound as many times as you want but in the track there must be sounds from ALL the packs. You can use as many drums as you want but the challenge is judged off the sounds you choose and how well you use them. Winner gets a shoutout on my Soundcloud. There is no limit for the track length but try to aim for 1-3 minutes.

Any questions, just ask me. The contest will be over when everyone who want to participate is finished, unless it is taking way too long.

Good luck and have fun!
