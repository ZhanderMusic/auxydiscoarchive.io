Dadmother November 24, 2019, 5:37pm #73

You know, I’m not sure I’m able to answer this question and be concise. It’s not so much a “who”, but a “what”. Music in general serves as an inspiration to create it for me. One of the beautiful things about music is that it basically serves as its own source of power and is an endless font of inspiration. I couldn’t begin to narrow down who/what inspires me, but I think it deserves mentioning that I am ALWAYS incredibly inspired by the Auxy community. I mean, just scrolling through this forum is like taking a trip through Talent City. And it’s not like these are people that society has decided are on a different, “celebrity” level and don’t interact. We have the amazing luck that we get to interact with incredible artists each and every day anytime we log onto the Disco or Discord.

I guess to try and make a long story short: I am inspired by all kinds of music, movies, feelings, people, etc. However, over the last few years, the Auxy community has been a gigantic source of inspiration, and without it, I can guarantee I wouldn’t have created even a fraction of the music I have at this point. This community pushes me to be a better musician as well as a better, more supportive fan.
