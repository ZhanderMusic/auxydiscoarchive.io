anon64827621 November 24, 2017, 6:25pm #99

Times up. I listened to each and every track and will now announce the winner … everyone.
As some of you might notice now, I never said that there’d be one winner and encouraged everyone to see this more as a challenge reason is: Who am I to decide if your music is good or not? Of course I have my own taste but I don’t lie when I say that every single one of those remixes is beautiful and dear to me. The song cœur., which is French for heart, was written for a person who meant a lot to me. I wanted to express the feeling they gave me and thus I wrote a song representing my heart. Each and every single one of you interpreted this song one or the other way and put your own signature to it. Some of you made it loud and vibrant, you might have felt the adventurous part of the song. And others of you felt the sadness, the longing and created a mellow lullaby. No matter which aspect you chose every time I heard a new interpretation of my song, I truly felt happiness.

The beginning of this thread turned into a little discussion about “being acknowledged for my music” and even after that ended I still read comments of people saying “I’m going to give up.” “I’m not good enough.” I read those things a lot on the forum. Debates about biases, people questioning if their music isn’t good enough, that turning into people going on hiatus or giving up completely. Today I want you all to feel appreciated and loved for what you make, for the craziness you don’t like to share, for the melodies that we hear in our head and then try to turn into something we can share, for what moves us - music.

Create music you enjoy to hear.
