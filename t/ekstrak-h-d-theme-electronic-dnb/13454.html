stereofield January 3, 2019, 1:31pm #9

Cool effect at the beginning. Only issue with that is there’s a plucky sound with reverb right as the note ends & it ends up being louder than the note itself Great beat, probably some of the only good drum & bass I’ve seen in Auxy. When the chords come in, it sounds a little too soft – I’d suggest changing to Sweet Spring or Sweet Saw without any attack so it doesn’t feel “wimpy” if you know what I mean. Some of the piano chords at 2:24 muddy up the mix with the bass, try to fix that up. Overall, maybe add just a bit of variation to keep listeners interested. Doesn’t have to be much, considering drum & bass usually is pretty repetitive – however, in its current state, the level of repetition gets fairly boring.

Other than that, great track! One of the best I’ve heard from you. Keep it up
