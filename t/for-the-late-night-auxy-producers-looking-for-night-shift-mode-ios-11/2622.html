For the late night Auxy producers looking for Night Shift mode (iOS 11)

RazerFireRiot September 20, 2017, 8:33pm #1

With iOS 11 finally out, some of us producers were pretty confused about navigating our own devices.
I’ll show you how to enable Night Shift with control center without having to go to settings, so you can’t comfortably make your Auxy track late at night!

(Note): Because you can now customize your control center, my screen will look slightly different.

Step one: Open up your control center

FB25D7F9-37FE-4957-B9B8-3736CD756609.jpg750×1334 119 KB

Step two: Tap and hold the sun (brightness adjuster)
You should get this:

05280477-E3BE-41DF-A788-6C0B020039B2.jpg750×1334 50 KB

Step three: Tap Night Shift.

BAE41C65-6023-48AA-9D26-521F4DE1EDB8.jpeg750×1334 178 KB

Step four: Continue making your amazing track on Auxy!

Repeat these steps to deactivate Night Shift mode manually.
