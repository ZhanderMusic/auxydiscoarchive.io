TheRealJFalc October 4, 2018, 3:32pm #29

While yes, I do agree that there are plenty of electronic sounds in Auxy to begin with, remember that Auxy is an electronic-focused application. While I do agree there would be more help in having a stronger variety of instruments, some suggestions from the developers suggest that premade instrument loops would be a much better route for expanding the sound rather than forcing samples into a playable format, which I partially agree with (and disagree with at the same time due to personal preference. But I completely understand the logic and support it. I only have a personal preference for customization over authenticity).

This is the first pack since RAC Esssentials to really focus more on analogue-esque sound, and it was a highly requested pack for a long time. It’s also versatile. With the introduction of twenty-second imports, you can effectively import real instrument loops into your songs and achieve the desired effect. You literally could record it from GarageBand on iOS if you wanted to, it would be that simple.

While there is value in customization, i’d much rather wait for the perfection it would take to make more real instruments optimal, rather than take a quick solution that doesn’t sound as good.
