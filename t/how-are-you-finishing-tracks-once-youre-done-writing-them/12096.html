akabillposters September 9, 2018, 8:53am #10

‘Release-ready’ is a rare state for me.

I never upload a finished piece directly from Auxy to SC, only WIPs, primarily for my own review purposes.

I’d like to be better at mixing and mastering myself as my tracks are often muddy, but at my current level, I tend to do more harm than good.

So for now, I lean on auto-mastering services like Landr or CloudBounce.

I’ll sometimes use Landr’s free 192 kbps MP3 mastered output as an ‘in-between’ review copy, between the Auxy output and a (paid-for) CloudBounce output.

When I’m ready for a decent (auto-)master, I’ve recently been impressed by CloudBounce for both quality and price. I’m thinking of buying an unlimited annual package, next time they run an offer.

(BandLab’s auto-mastering is too hit n miss for regular, reliable use.)
