I export tracks into Auria where I use the fab filter EQ to get a sense of whether the track is balanced or not. I try to identify eq problems or excessive peaks here, then go back into Auxy and try to fix them in the originals. Having done that, I will go into Auria one more time for minor eq cleanup and compression/limiting. Sometimes even that is not necessary.
