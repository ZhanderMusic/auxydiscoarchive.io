Produk:

Upload a lot

share your music

I’d strongly disagree with the first part – and strongly agree with the second part.

Speaking as someone without a broad audience, plays tend to peak briefly after a release or a new mention, but then quickly taper off again.

This creates the urge to release frequently, but this isn’t a good plan, as it will simply devalue each previous track. It pushes you towards a ‘quantity over quality’ process – and tracks created in the mode are less likely to gain individual traction – and break out of the Auxy community, which is what we should all be aiming for.

Find a way to build an audience that goes beyond the Auxy community, as this should help stabilise listen rates and give each track a life of its own. (Auxy community listens are quickly exhausted and there’s little real value in Reposts from within the community as they’re all kinda aiming at each other.)

And, of course, put the time and effort required into making tracks that have greater potential and staying power.
