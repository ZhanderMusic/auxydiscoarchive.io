Auxy March 20, 2018, 8:59pm #64

There’s no digital clipping in Auxy, i.e. the processed signal’s output stays < 0 dB at the final stage. Otherwise I would be embarrassed. The limiter is distorting the signal in a way that for some sounds sounds a bit like clipping, but this is on purpose. You could even call it a compromise…

We could potentially add a feature (!!) for turning off the master effects and let you guys deal with “mastering” in whatever way you like. My hunch is that this would only complicate things for most of you though and I’m not sure you would get any better results in the end. You can already bounce each instrument as a separate file, without the master effects, and mix the levels in e.g. a DAW before you send the track off to a mastering engineer.
