Special thanks to our Janitors @MisterMaster and @iammane for keeping the place nice and tidy, and for @Auxy and @Fredrik for continuing to push the boundaries of what’s possible with the app they’ve created.

That’s all for now. 'Tis been a pleasure hosting this year’s Auxy Forum Awards–here’s to a continued great new year and I look forward to seeing all of you develop and continue to create more tracks in the coming year.
