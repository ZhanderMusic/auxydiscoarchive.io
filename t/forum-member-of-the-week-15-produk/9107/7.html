anon73773654 April 8, 2018, 11:52pm #16

Here’s some feedback on Distant Future (the album) and a few tracks after. Gonna do it here instead of spam commenting on SC. xD

Distant Future: Love that finger click in the intro. I think some more reverb on it would be cool though. Sweeeet drop, wubs are awesome as always. Great lead… is that Coin? Anyways, awesome track.

Don’t: Opening with another arp, and piano. This one’s got it’s own originality though. Great snare roll that comes in later. I personally think you could’ve used another lead instead of Hour. It’s a great lead, but I think it sounds a little whiny. Nothing personal, imo Hour always sounds whiny. (also sorry man, 666th play)

Magnetism: Nice little intro melody. Charge snare is great. Sounds a little empty at 0:32, think you need to stack the layers a little more there. Love the chords too, but I don’t think the wubs are necessary in this one. Other than that, I love this one. (trippy drop btw!)

Vision: Nice percussion! I think you overuse the arps as well as Hour in many of your songs, and it might be fun to expand your horizons a little? Power chords btw! Quite similar to Magnetism imo.

Memories: Probably one of your best songs to date. The emotional piano’s on point, honestly nothing I could tell you to improve on. Plucky wubs are amazing… the melodic percussion that comes in before the second drop adds a nice touch. 1,200th play!

Rest: My personal favorite. Anyone else love it when a-team/FB artists try a new genre? It’s usually one of their best tbh. This is a prime example. The percussion is perfect, and the chords are great. Gives a nice groovy feel, honestly. Love that June arp thing, I actually tried something just like that in a recent WIP.

Some of your other tracks: Burden is awesome, the melody is beautiful, and the chords are so powerful. Meteor was also pretty great-- nice use of the guitar!

… as you can see I had a lot of time on my hands today lol. great job, keep up the awesomeness!
