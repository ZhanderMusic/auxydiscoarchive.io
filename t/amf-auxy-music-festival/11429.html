AMF (Auxy Music Festival)

Random
eohr July 28, 2018, 8:15pm #1

Ever since I joined Auxy about ten months ago, it seems the community has grown immensely. I want to thank everyone here for being such a great group of musicians with great ideas and the devs for making the best mobile DAW and one of the best DAWs of all time.

BUT now, to take the community even further and connect us even more, I would like to propose that, as a community, we have an an Auxy Music Festival.

I was thinking it would be awesome if this now-huge community had some kind of get-together to share our music with the community in person. My ideas are still pretty basic at this time, but what I came up with was a group of Auxy users taking turns at DJing simple, short sets (of their music and other music) and talking about music.

This is pretty far fetched as of now, but I do think it would be pretty sick if we could coordinate something in-person like @DHO mentioned about a real-life Auxy tournament or collab:

[CONTEST] Auxy Summer Tournament 2K18!

Would anybody ever be interested in attending an in person Auxy Tournament? Not something I’m planning to organize for at least a few years, just wondering.

Thanks for reading, everyone!
eohr
