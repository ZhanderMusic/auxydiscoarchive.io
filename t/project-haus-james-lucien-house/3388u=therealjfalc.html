TheRealJFalc October 25, 2017, 3:15pm #7

Not necessarily creepy, actually xD

The differences between harmonic and natural minor are much different in older music that is not EDM and electronic-based. The reason why harmonic minor exists is that it was a tendency to go sharp on the 7th tone in a scale due to the strength of the leading tone to tonic (root, 1). This strength was so powerful that harmonic (because it produces the harmonic strength in the leading tone) minor developed. Most classical and contemporary music is in harmonic minor because of the strength in resolution of the dominant (V) chord when it is a major triad compared to a minor triad in natural minor (v). There’s always exceptions of course, but this is why it developed.

In traditional EDM music, resolutions do not occur the same way as non-electronic and older musics, so the leading tone creates a sort of creepy effect when it is not used like it was designed. Not to say that this is wrong, far from it. It’s an alternate application. Its a feature, not a mere glitch.

Sorry for going all music theory dense here, but I wanted to explain why it does what it does xD
