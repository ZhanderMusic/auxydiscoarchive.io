Found an old version of the app on iPad. Live recording is something I equally miss, as well as resent. Bad, because you have to scramble to press your loops and I remember having these 9 minute epics. I mean, I aimed for 3 minutes and that didn’t happen at all.

But it was also good, because you’re having a load of fun. I spend way longer on tracks in the new app these days, cutting and refining. It keels me.

So this was good to relax with.
