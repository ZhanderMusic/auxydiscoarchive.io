I’d say it largely boils down to what each of us hopes to achieve with our music.

If the ambition is to produce tracks that sound good next to the stuff you hear in the charts or the big YouTube promo channels, and that have real potential to get traction, then chances are, spending 20 hours total working time - in Auxy - isn’t going to get you there.
(Unless you’re exceptionally lucky and/or talented.)

Conversely, if the ambition is simply to produce something that you and some Auxy users like and enjoy, then sure, bang them out as fast as you want.
