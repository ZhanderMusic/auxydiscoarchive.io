OIL March 25, 2018, 6:29pm #38

Add a new instrument, or melody, or drum track. Even if it seems like a non-sequitur, it’s something that could poke your brain into thinking of new stuff. Hell, even adding an automation clip from another song you made to an instrument could change your entire perception of the song.

There was one track I made where I wasn’t sure how to continue, so I did the automation clip thing on that and ended up with something that I thought was really cool.

Hope that helped, yo.
