Update for those not in the Discord:

Round two of voting has concluded and the tracks will be reposted throughout the week. Below are some important notes that happened during the past week:

  1. We have a finalized system for voting and reposting as well as a schedule. Voting concludes each Sunday and tracks are reposted throughout the week at 2-day intervals starting that Monday.

  2. The Auxy Collective Discord now has a 24/7 bot to automate the leaderboard process.

  3. As part of our mission to broaden and expand the listening audience for those in the community, specifically The Auxy Collective, all tracks that are reposted will also be reposted to the labels “The Music Spotlight” and “EDM Central”. We are going to continue discussing with more labels agreements as far as repairing and distributing the pre vetted tracks from the collective, so the audience only grows from here.

  4. Last but definitely not least, there’s been so much amazing music suggested that I hadn’t even heard before. Really an amazing thing to see how much talent there is.

As usual, the majority of the updates do happen on the Discord, so if you’re interested you should join it to be kept up to date. However, I will post the most important updates to this thread. The tracks for this week will be reposted to our official SoundCloud account at the top of this post.
