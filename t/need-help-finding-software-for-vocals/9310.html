iammane August 22, 2018, 1:37pm #14

I have no experience with it, but aside from Autotune mobile and Voloco there’s also Voice Jam Studio. I have the Voice Rack: FX product, it’s ok.

Both of those are by TC Helicon, pretty well known in the desktop space.

To be honest, any kind of serious vocal editing (pitch/timing correction etc) is super seriously lacking. You’re probably going to want to bring it into desktop land if you need to do heavy editing.

Voloco is cool as an effect, I wouldn’t use it to treat vocals if you want to preserve the performance. Voice Rack FX is the same (to be honest, I’ve used it as an AUv3 on other stuff like basslines and guitars lol…).

That’s about all I know about these kinds of apps
