TXA February 14, 2018, 11:45pm #31

I have a trick for this.

Let’s say you want 64th hi-hats, for example.

To do this in auxy:

First you choose your hihat sample and load into a drum set with at least 2 free slots. Load the same sample into the other free spot. When you get to the spot you want the hi hats in, place 32nd notes on both of the hihat lanes. Then go into one of the hihat sample modification section at the beginning of the drum loop and turn the offset up fully. This should result in 64th hihats, with a few exceptions of course. You might need to play around with the amount of offset depending on the kind of sample you use.

Hope you enjoy your 64th notes!

EDIT: I read the posts above and though they aren’t really 64th notes you still get the same sound.
