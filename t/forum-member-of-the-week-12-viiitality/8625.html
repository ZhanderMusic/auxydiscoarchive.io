Featured Auxy Artist #12: @viiitality

The first three names randomly selected for this week were returned to the pool, as we want to focus on people who have been supporting the featured forum members. On the fourth spin of the wheel, we got a winner…

Viiitality is a Texas-based Pilipino student and musician. According to an earlier post he was one of the top-20 breaststrokers for his age group in the US last year (and top in his state). He plays the tuba & ukulele and is learning guitar and piano. He enjoys Deadmau5’s music. Meanwhile his own music has been reposted by Auxy and shortlisted for Beat The Clock. He’s a regular on the forum and every time I see his name, I think of this. Show him some

Week starting: 19 Mar, 2018.

  • Please listen to at least one of their tracks (or more)
  • Provide at least one Soundcloud comment / feedback about their music during their week.
  • Comment in this thread once you’ve provided any feedback.
  • If you want people to comment on your week, remember to comment for others!
