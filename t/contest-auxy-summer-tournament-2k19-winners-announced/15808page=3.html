DHO June 15, 2019, 5:56am #57

Automation is still taken into account, however. It is just not judged as part of the composition (in this case we are looking at harmonic content). The reason the judging is broken into categories to avoid unmethodical, biased, or thoughtless scoring. Sometimes it is difficult for one to be objective and uninfluenced by personal taste without taking something apart, using their acuity to discern the different ways the song is pulled together and score that. For example, a song could be great in every way besides composition. To judge the final result it would likely get a much lower score than taking into account all categories, besides the poor harmonic content will negatively influence the listening experience even if the participant excelled in every other way.
