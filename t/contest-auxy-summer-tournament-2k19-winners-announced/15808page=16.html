What I wanted to showcase with this track is how I can layer many instruments and drum samples (I’m using a custom kit with 27 short samples in this project!) to create a fuller sound. I also imported single pitched samples for acoustic bass and brass, instruments Auxy usually doesn’t have.

The track is infused with a tropical feeling representing the start of summer, achieved through fusing genres like bossa nova, swing, and jazz.

paroxyzm June 30, 2019, 4:35am #308

https://soundcloud.com/paroxyzm-music-alt/auxy-summer-tournament-2k19
My focus was on the melody, but more specifically making a melody on top of somewhat uncommon chords.
The melody is my favourite part of a track, and if the melody isn’t quite right it can ruin the whole thing, so I’ve always tried to make it the best it can be. Melodies can be simple to make if the chords are quite simple but as they get more complex it can become more difficult, so I tried to test my abilities of making a melody by having chords that aren’t too common. Hope you like it! (Good luck @brandnaqua, I really enjoyed your track!)
