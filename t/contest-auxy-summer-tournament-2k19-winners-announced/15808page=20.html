Hi, all, great job with the first round! If you want a track evaluation, reply here if you’d like it posted here, or send us (the judges) a private message if you’d prefer a private one.

One thing I wanted to clarify about submissions: Entries can be up to an hour late (losing one point for every ten minutes). After the hour it is disqualified. We also cannot offer extensions for the sake of keeping things fair and on schedule.

The Challenge

So, one of the commonalities I noticed across the last round was repetition. Of course, repetition is important, but when it’s the same thing again and again with no variations it gets to be a little bit much. So, for this round, I want you all to make a one minute solo! Bonus points if you make a funk bass solo.

The track can have more than one instrument, but the main focus needs to be on a solo with minimal unvaried repetition. It can be a melodic solo, a drum solo, a bass solo, even a chord solo (so long as the chords are heavily emphasized and varied. This will be a chance to really hone in on one aspect an try to create something special. Let me know if you have any questions. Remember, the solo can be in any style.

The submissions are due by Wednesday, July 10th, 5:00 PM Pacific Time. Post a SoundCloud link here. We will not accept private messages/private links.
