NickElle February 14, 2018, 5:44pm #5

It depends.

I used to make Hip-Hop (like actual Hip-Hop, not Lo-Fi), so I know a bit.

As far as key, it depends on what kind of rap you’re going for (what cadence you’re on).
If you’re on some “Pop trash” (mumble cadence, sing-songy), yes, key matters.
Also, if you’re using auto tune on your vox, make sure the key is set.
But, if you’re going for more of a hood thing (which may or may not be possible depending on your culture), then key isn’t a big deal. You won’t be changing your pitch at all for that.
