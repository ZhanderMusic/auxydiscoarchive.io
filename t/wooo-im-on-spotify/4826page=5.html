Tip for everyone getting their stuff up on Spotify…

Be sure to get yourself verified and grab your Spotify for Artists access (and the app).

This allows you to…

  • Add a Spotify profile avatar
  • Add a profile header image
  • Add artist playlists (your favourite tunes, for listeners to follow)*
  • Add a bio
  • Have direct access to all your Spotify stats
     

* It’s my understanding that Spotify’s algorithm partially identifies where new music belongs by what other artists appear in the same playlists.
So, it might be worth creating a playlist with your tracks and other similar artists, primarily for listeners to follow, but also as it might help Spotify start to identify similar artists.

 
Also, bring your SoundCloud up to spec too…

  • Make sure you add your Spotify, iTunes, Bandcamp, etc… links to your SoundCloud profile.
  • Start adding the Spotify, iTunes, etc… links to your track descriptions, if the track also appears on Spotify, iTunes, etc…
