akabillposters September 16, 2018, 8:38am #143

A few points…

(SAMPLE)

Are you posting clips to Spotify?
Not sure of the value of that. “Sample” suggests a short teaser/taster/excerpt for the full track that’s coming later. (Not the same thing as the full instrumental version.)

Really not sure Spotify’s the right place to do that.
(I could be wrong. I’ve not seen it used that way before and it instinctively feels like a ‘not good’ idea, to me.)

Alternatively, if it’s the full length track and ‘…(SAMPLE)’ is just a creative/stylistic element of the track title, then that’s ok, of course.

 
If this release is genuinely intended to be the instrumentals from an upcoming release, then there are track naming conventions you could/should use - e.g. …

‘Album/release title (Instrumentals)’
‘Track title (Instrumental)’

 

VM / VJM

You had three letters to type and you honestly managed to get it wrong?

Afaik, you can’t change it while it’s up. You’ll likely need to delete the tracks/artist and reupload again with correct artist info.

Which is actually good, as it gives you the opportunity to review and revise the track titling.
