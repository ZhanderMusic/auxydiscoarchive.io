Auxy Day Mode Tip! Random
So, as we all know, Auxy has a dark theme. I like the look of the interface. However, if anyone wants a “day mode” theme (light colored), then just do this! Go to settings, click “General”, go to “Accessibility”, click “Display Accommodations”, and check “Invert Colors”. If you want a fast shortcut, while on the “Accessibility” screen, go to the bottom and click “Accessibility Shortcut”, and select “Invert Colors”. Now, whenever you want to use your day mode theme, triple click the home button…
