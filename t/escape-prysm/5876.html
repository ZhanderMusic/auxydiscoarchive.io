First off. It isn’t all about the plays, don’t worry as much about those. I didn’t like the buildup much, I personally prefer softer buildups and this one sounded harsh. That opinion could be different to others though. The drop was nice but it needed more chord variety a bit. The same two or three play throughout the song and it sounds nice but maybe don’t overuse it. The volume is up a lot on the builds and it takes away from the drums. Try using automations to create tension and build it up . The melodies in the drop was nice and I liked the transition with the start and stop to the second build. Overall I would say work on intentional instrument choices and mix the song a bit more, maybe throw in some automations to spice things up. Good song overall. Thanks for sharing
