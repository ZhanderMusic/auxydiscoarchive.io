Time to Beat the Clock!

This week’s host is @Otzsil who will present the challenge below and pick his favorites on Monday.

If you’re new to Beat the Clock, here’s how it works:

  • Download the project template below.
  • Be creative and make some musics based on the rules presented by the host.
  • Upload your track to SoundCloud and post a link to it in this thread.
  • The host will listen to all submissions, so there’s no advantage in posting early.
  • On Monday, the host will pick his/her favorites, so it’s a good idea to post your submission on Sunday.

Let’s do this!
