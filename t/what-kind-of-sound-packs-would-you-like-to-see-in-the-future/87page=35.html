HYDRANS August 14, 2019, 5:07pm #724

I think that this might backfire because most of the sounds in future/experimental music are made to be unique and different, but if they were added to Auxy (a preset-based synth), the sounds would get overused. For example, when Tonsil (a very distinctive sound) came out, it got used in lots of tracks, but now it’s barely used at all because it instantly makes your track sound like a typical Auxy track.

I think it’s best to stick with simple sound packs because it’s easier to be creative and make your own sound instead of fitting the “Auxy sound.”
