akabillposters January 21, 2018, 10:40am #483

But, as Xiejra correctly implies, when maxed out, you’re not getting a 100% wet-only signal, which is how proper wet/dry controls work.

A wet signal is not the ‘dry signal with the effect on it’.
The dry/source signal and the effect-only signal are typically two signals.

A wet/dry mixer has more in common with panning, as I understand it, a mix balance between two signals.

With Auxy, you’re simply bringing up the effect-only signal on top of the dry signal, not instead of the dry signal.
The dry source signal is always being played underneath.
