Eric_Classic August 24, 2017, 9:39am #271

Hand percussion. Bongos and Congas. Acoustic Drum Snare. Longer shake. Acoustic Bass Drum. Tabla. Natural Hi-Hat. Natural Clap. That is really all I would want. The current sound packs are quite expansive depending on the artists’ imagination and mixing techniques. For example…Silk, Runner, Bow, and others can be easily mixed to sound like a stringed instrument. There are others I mixed to sound like a flute, guitar, and other dynamic tones. With the right layering, panning, EQ’ing, and etc., any sound can be customized. The sounds I listed can bring more depth and an added dimension to an already exceptional sound library. For the record, I do own all packs.
