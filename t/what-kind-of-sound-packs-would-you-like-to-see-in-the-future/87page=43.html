Wunder_Wulfe May 3, 2021, 9:03am #881

I would love to see some interesting and unique instruments get some love in the Auxy sound packs. There are quite a few missing sounds that would help with many different genres of music. Some that I had in mind were the following:

  • Accordians and Concertinas.
  • Music box instrument.
  • High quality distorted guitars, perfect for heavy metal and rock.
  • Smoother and groovier slap, pick, and finger bass for funky dance music.
  • Various wind instruments such as ocarinas, flutes, oboes, clarinets, and more.
  • Various string instruments such as sitars, 12 string guitars, mandolins, banjos, ukeleles, etc.
  • Various 80’s and 70’s style synths to make funky grooves with. Good examples are the acid bass, some light synth strings, and tacky electronic keys.
  • Some matching electronic drum kits with sounds such as record scratches, orchestral hits, and others.
  • Trap style horn staccato, heavy bass, and leads.
  • A brushed drum kit for jazzy beats.
  • Hard heavy distorted kick drum kit for hardbass and drumstep and the like.
  • Heavy wobble synths and bass, perfect for dubstep drops and melodies. There are a few already in Auxy within the Panic Attack pack, but it would be great to have a couple more with different flavors.
  • Soft electric pianos and strings, think mii music or elevator music.
  • Ambient synths and pads, good for synthwave tracks and for filling chill music with ambient noise and background
