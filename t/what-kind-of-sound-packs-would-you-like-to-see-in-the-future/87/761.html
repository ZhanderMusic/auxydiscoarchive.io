EVERANGE March 19, 2020, 7:42pm #766

That is definitely a noteworthy comment. The more I produce on auxy (which I have been doing since it came out) and I begin to stray out into the more advanced and complex work of music, I feel that auxy has limitations. We cannot create our own sounds, only modify those that already exist and only to a moderate extent. My experience with other music production software is that there are simply so many options and things to do and tweak that I feel overwhelmed. However, I am beginning to feel limited by auxy. I know it might be a difficult step but I may have to leave auxy and use other software unless, as @blakkaz mentioned, we get more tools, maybe a synthesizer, and the ability to better modify or even create our own sound. After all, auxy is one of the few programs that has a subscription fee (most programs have a one time fee and maybe another one time fee for upgrading should on wish to do so). Therefore, as a long time client of auxy, I would hope that it would be able to make some changes. In the end, everyone is paying money for auxy and more updates and changes wouldn’t hurt.

Don’t get me wrong, there are a lot of great things about auxy, it’s just that it could be even greater.
