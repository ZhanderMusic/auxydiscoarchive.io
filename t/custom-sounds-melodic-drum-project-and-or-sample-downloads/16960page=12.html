Unless/until Auxy has a dedicated synthesiser built in, where you can build your own sounds from basic waveforms, you’re basically using ‘other people’s packs’.

iammane July 8, 2020, 4:16pm #258

Geometric Traces v11400×1400 240 KB

A collection of glitchy / IDM inspired sounds including drums, ambience loops and guitars. 35 common chord wash fingerings, all six strings sampled from open string to 5th fret (by string and note name), 12 ambience loops driven by Quanta on iOS. Drums a mix of Fractal Bits, some Drambo slicing and Ruismaker Noir. The full pack is available on my Bandcamp page, but because I’d never consider not at least giving a healthy taste out to my favorite community, I’ve selected some from each category and built a demo. Please feel free to use any of the included sounds / loops whatever in your own stuff. Absolutely no credit is necessary.

https://app.auxy.co/projects/chGvYvEoqgzRV5QVtARP_A==

This is my first time doing this. Might be my last. We’ll see!
