https://app.auxy.co/projects/YCOSo1oz-KW-R6COgmlwsQ==

Model:Cycles Toolbox.

I spent a few hours sampling this awesome groovebox. Unfortunately, a lot of the magic is lost when you’re recording it dry. I think what makes this piece SO awesome is the parameter locks, the Elektron sequencer and the on board reverb and delay are SO good. When you sample stuff though, you usually want to do it dry and since I’m not sampling whole loops, all that magic is basically lost. That said, since I DID all the work sampling it, I figured what the heck, samples are samples, it’s still worth sharing. Maybe someone can use them.

10 melodic instruments, mostly leaning toward basses, 50 drum samples.

These were all recorded straight over USB into AUM and the only post processing was normalizing them in AudioShare, so they might run a little hot. I didn’t want to add TOO much to the base sounds but obviously, dial in your filtering and effects to taste.
