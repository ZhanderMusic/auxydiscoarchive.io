tictacto September 27, 2019, 8:12pm #20

Well… here it is. My own little bundle of sounds for all to use. Enjoy!

https://app.auxy.co/projects/n32-jclAjbrsqJvM9v81LA==

Slice: A heavy Mental-ish lead, without the vocal tinge that you might hear. Good for heavy FB and House songs.

Crescent: A grand pad, this sound will add some depth to your song. It’s like a vegan choir! Good for FB.

Showtime: A plucky bass. I tried to make a slap bass for those funky basslines, but this was the closest I got. Good for Dance, Swing and Future Funk.

Palace: Nitty, gritty, and primed for beating a fastfood CEO Kudos if you get the reference. Good for heavy music in general.

DRUMKITS

Grind: Distorted DnB kit, great for providing a really edgy sound, or use it without distortion for a clean, neat kit.

Barren: A trap kit I’m using in my upcoming song. Provides a light atmosphere while giving a song a certain urgency to it. We don’t talk about those percussion.

Stage: A nice kit. What more is there to say? It has that pizazz, with ALL the jazz, this kit just OOZES laid-back energy.

Ritual: This kit mixes natural sound with electronic. with drum hits, clicks and vocal cuts, it has the power to make you feel like a jungle explorer.
