I decided to put in some drums this time. All of the sounds are free for use, but you’ll have to credit me if your gonna use the loops

Sounds:

MELODIC INSTRUMENTS:

The return of the legend - a loud boom bass, sounds better individually IMO
Confusion - a deep, distorted pad, with a burning feel to it
exetremity - A very intense “lead”.
EMP - a loud bass made out of a piano
Tickling spark - an arpeggio
Overdrive guitar - May sound on “experimental” and “sad” songs.
Burning laser - a pad which is slightly unclean, yet has the clean feeling
Blender phaser - Some sort of weird mix of an “organ” and a “lead”.
Heartbeat - a soft synthwave style bass
whut just haped? - PLS DONT USE
Sparkler - A cool, sparkling sound

DRUMS:

Rust - An electronic kit, best for DnB, with a kick that has a lot of sustain. Decrease the lowpass, and increase the distortion to make it sound more fit for the “jungle” genre.

Hype - an electronic kit, but can be used for non electronic music too. I think it’s meant for some style of odd music. (I dunno how to describe this one sorry) Remove the distortion, and don’t use “Snare 4”, and occasionally use “Snare 5” to get a cleaner version of the kit

Hyper charger - A kit, generally for epic intense bass music (similar to ones like the second part of the drop in my track, The Final Hour, but with some volume automations and stuff.) It doesn’t have to be used for those only though. It can be used for any electronic genre, maybe not around the trance area…

808 Messy - an acoustic, and very distorted version of the regular 808 kit, may work with messy future bass, trap, and other genres that use 808s. If you take the distortion off, it won’t change the distortion throughout the kit. I made this kit using the most distorted samples from the “808 from mars” samples.
