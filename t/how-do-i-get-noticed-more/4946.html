submithub.com is a good way to directly share your music with promotion sites, radios, and more.

Build up social media around your music so that you’re more visible online.

Be very active in the music community (not just here on the Auxy forum.) Talk to other artists, get your name out there, make connections, don’t hesitate to ask questions, and give feedback.

Just a few general tips from me ️ Feel free to ask more questions as well.

xavior December 10, 2017, 9:56am #4

I don’t think it’s “begging” if you’re just sharing music and asking someone / people to take a listen. Submithub is set up in a way that you pretty much advertise your track and you can send it directly to sites (where it ends up in a pool of other submissions) and they can choose your song to promote or they tell you why they didn’t pick your song. It’s very helpful. The success rate is iffy, but that’s to be expected in such a competitive art.
