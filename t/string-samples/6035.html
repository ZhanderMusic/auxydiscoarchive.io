Cheddr January 10, 2018, 7:39pm #14

Layers help with the chorusing. @BALLISTIK is right, it’s important. Modulating volume, tone, or eq randomly and individually helps it sound more natural too. I like the reverb up when doing this as well.

I like to put bow, runner, and story together to sound like an orchestra.

Be sure to use low octaves for bow.
Story to me sounds like brass instruments. There are others for brass, but I can’t remember which.
June for high woodwinds like flutes and clarinets.
Others that could be mixed in are otter, strain, jelly, silk.

A lot of my songs have strings. Runner is my go to to usually for that.
