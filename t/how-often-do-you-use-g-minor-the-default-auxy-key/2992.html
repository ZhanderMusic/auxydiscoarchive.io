TBH, I don’t actually know.

I ignore the key setting, and always switch to Chromatic at the start of each new project.

From there, I’ll often already have something in my head, e.g. a melody, that I need to quickly get down, and flow out from there. But, I literally have no idea which key or chord/scale I’m working in, although I know it varies from project to project. I never pay attention to the notes shown on the piano roll.

I just feel my way through it, ‘playing’ by ear. Never felt the need to be more self-aware about the music I’m making – at least, in terms of being more thoughtful about keys, scales, etc….
