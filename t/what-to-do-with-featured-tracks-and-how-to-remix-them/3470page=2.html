Auxy November 12, 2017, 2:07pm #34

Remember that the projects in the feed are released under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 License, which means that what you can do and not do with a featured track is covered by the license. I recommend that you read the full license and make sure you understand it before sharing tracks using it.

The same license could be applied to artworks, but remember that as long as you don’t explicitly know which license an image has, you should assume that is “All Rights Reserved”, which means you cannot use it for your own work.
