blakkaz July 15, 2018, 11:18pm #133
Karlos:

The mono thing is because most Phones or small portable speakers (although may not really be the case in todays world) play music out loud in Mono.

A few more reasons :

  1. Mono compatibility will result in better reproduction on vinyl.

  2. Mono compatibility will result in better reproduction when converting to lossy formats.

  3. Mono compatibility will result in better reproduction in oddly shaped venues. (The more common the left and right channels are, the less loss there will be in awkward spots.)

  4. Many clubs/festivals will actually be wired up in mono.

  5. FM radio broadcasts are sum/difference signals as opposed to left/right. If you have a weak signal the sum (mono) is used, as it’s the stronger signal of the two.
