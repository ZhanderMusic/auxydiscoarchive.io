akabillposters March 6, 2018, 4:58pm #111

Just a quick note to all…

Invest in a decent pair of studio monitor/reference headphones*.

I’ve been revisiting some of my older tracks, created before I bought my Sennheiser HD 380 Pro.
I’m hearing things I’d not heard before, even on tracks of my own that I’ve listened to many, MANY times and I’m very proud of.

 
So, if you’re at all serious about your music, invest in a pair, because, if you’re not listening on a decent set of studio monitor/reference headphones*, you’re not really hearing everything your own music is doing.

(* Of course, this goes for studio monitor speakers, but then you’re getting into ‘acoustic treatment of rooms’ territory…)

 
/captainobvious
