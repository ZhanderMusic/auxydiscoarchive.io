Contest & Challenges
Osharu April 9, 2018, 3:04pm #1

New challenge from Osharu Perez! Make a song with only one instrument from each respective category! So, that means only one instrument from “Lead”, one from “Bass”, and only ONE drum kit! You can layer the sounds so you can have two of the same lead, but not two different leads. You can fine tune your drum kit, but you can’t have more than one drum kit. Layering drum kits is NOT allowed! You can release this track, and post the link here, or you can make the song, and then post the project file here. Can’t wait to hear what you guys come up with! Only reply if you are entering this contest please! A minimum of three contenders is needed to start the contest.

Summary

One “Bass”, “Lead”, and “Drum Kit” is to be used to create a song. Layering of “Leads” is allowed, as is for the “Bass”, but not the “Drum Kits”. Only reply if you are entering the contest please! Minimum of three contestants to start the contest!

Entries Deadline: May 4th.
Winner Announcement: May 7th.
