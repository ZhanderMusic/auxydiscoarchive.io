Contest & Challenges

About 2 years ago I released my first (and as of right now, only) album on SoundCloud! It was pretty amateur, since I was still not really that great at making music, but it was still a pretty big achievement.

I decided since it’s been 2 years I should do something to commemorate it, so I’m doing a remix contest! Each competitor will remix one (or more) track from the album, and there will be an individual winner for each track. The winners for each track will be part of a remix album/playlist I’m going to make, and will also get followed and reposted by me (sorry I don’t have a better prize :/)
