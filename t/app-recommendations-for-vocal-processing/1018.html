iammane July 22, 2017, 2:19pm #5

Take is good, but you can’t get any audio out of it unless you also have Reason (drop from Allihoopa to Reason).

There is autotune on iOS, not sure how recently updated. Not free though, I’ve used it a bunch in the past, it’s ok it gets the job done.

GarageBand on iOS did recently add pitch correction for audio tracks but it’s not very full featured. It’s better than nothing though and iOS GB has been progressing very nicely over the years. You’d probably want to just export tracks from Auxy and use something like Audioshare to get the Auxy tracks into GB as audio files then record vox on top.
