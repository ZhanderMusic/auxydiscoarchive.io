HYDRANS July 10, 2019, 9:25pm #26

In the previous versions of Auxy, Tone and Shape were parameters that changed for each instrument in the app. Most of the time, Tone was some form of distortion or phaser, so the instruments that don’t have tone anymore have the same effect in the instrument, but under a different name (distortion, etc.)

Some instruments have a more unique effect for the tone knob, though, and from what I’ve seen, these instruments still have the tone parameter under general settings. (It might be labeled under a different name still. For example, Bitty’s “Tone” setting is now Vibrato)
