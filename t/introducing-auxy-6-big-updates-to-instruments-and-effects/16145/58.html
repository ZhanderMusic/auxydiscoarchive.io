Nindota July 11, 2019, 2:32am #60

Good update and all, but I’m noticing a fairly notable issue regarding one of the changes.

In previous version of Auxy the effects applied to an individual instrument would remain the same when changing said instrument. What I’ve noticed with the latest update is that when changing instruments, it will now completely reset any of the effects you applied to the instrument before changing it.
If people are trying to create multiple instruments with similar effects to one another, before they could simply copy and paste each the instrument with the desired effects applied, and simply change this instrument to another to carry the effects over. With this new update, if people wish to do the same they now have to manually copy each of the effects applied one at a time, potentially causing the amount of time it takes to grow exponentially.

I do not know if this was something intentional or not, but I can and already am seeing it as a fairly notable issue that should likely be resolved.

In the case that this was intentional, however, I believe it would be far better to give people the option to have their effects reset or not when changing an instrument, as I can understand why some may prefer it to do so.
