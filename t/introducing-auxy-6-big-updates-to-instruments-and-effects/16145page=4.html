iammane July 11, 2019, 9:05am #77

Not a bug, the text on the bottom of the effect window explains:

B34E1B95-F9B1-470B-BCB0-1F37FB235D9B.jpeg828×826 156 KB

Each instrument channel has an independent send effect section. So you can have one drum channel with whatever settings you like, and then another drum channel with different settings. Unique fx settings per sample would be astronomically costly for CPU (and would have to be calculated on the fly?)… it would be like having a unique fx send for every single sample in a kit… ie drum 1 has a unique distortion, reverb and chorus, drum 2 has a unique distortion, reverb and chorus and so on
