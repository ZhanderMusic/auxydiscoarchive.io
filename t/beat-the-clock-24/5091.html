Merry 24th of BTC everyone

Since it’s the second last weekend and BTC before Christmas I need you to indeed jingle the bells and make a track with two formal specs:

a) structure it: I need you to build a max 1:30 song where you get things up, escalate and slow them down again. Thus please incorporate a clear

  • intro
  • mid part / chorus
  • and outro

b) wrap it: make it frosty by prominently using one or more of the provided ‘tiny’ instrument patterns. You may not alter notes for the sake of fairness, since not everyone has the melodic percussion sound pack yet.

https://app.auxy.co/projects/9cvRVSHEr9U2zD0eARVTsw==

Otherwise you’re free to do whatever you want (use the key and tempo you like). To submit your BTC please upload the final song to soundcloud and share the SC link here (not the project link please).

That’s about it. I will chose and announce the winners and put ‘em in a shortlist on Monday.

Good luck and most importantly - have fun!
