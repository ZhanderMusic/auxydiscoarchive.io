Yes, but the first impression still matters. A cluttered profile can immediately steer someone away. I usually do go to ‘tracks’, however my opinion on reposting still stands. If people want your work, they’re gonna go to the tracks tab and ignore the reposts. Which sorta defeats the purpose of reposting: showing off a track and exposing new people to it.
