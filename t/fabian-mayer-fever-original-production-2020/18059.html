Hey Fabian

Since you’re asking for feedback, I’ll give you some:)

This track has 3 parts, divided by the two breakdowns/drops. If you skip between these three sections, they are very similar.

There should be a build up of intensity. Else the listener/dancer/clubber has already got the payoff from the beginning.

Consider stuff like automating the reverb, so the melodic and harmonic sounds (not the percussion / drums) very slowly get more and more reverb, throughout the track.

Or add layers that play the same melodies, but with different sounds, so the main lead/melody gets more and more intense.

I highly recommend the book The Addiction Formula (also available as an AMAZING audiobook on Audible) – it goes into exactly how to build up the energy and tension, throughout your tracks.
