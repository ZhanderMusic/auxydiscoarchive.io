BobTheGUYYYYY June 14, 2019, 12:58pm #40

If your budget reaches $300 or so at some point, I would SOOOO recommend you buy the Sony WH-1000XM3’s. They’re wireless, have a super long battery life, let you make your own custom bass and treble mix, amazing sound cancellation (based on the air pressure, if you’re moving or not, etc.), and have a bunch of other fun stuff like making it sound like you’re listening in a theater or at a club, or making the music sound like it’s behind you, or allowing people’s voices to come through but not other ambient noise of your headphones. Or all of those features at once. This makes them great for producing or listening, since you can adjust the mix accordingly.

Every tutorial or top 10 or review on YouTube gives these insanely positive reviews, and when I gave them a try myself I was not disappointed.

Just something to think about. Saving your cash up for these for a while could be a great investment.
