foryou June 1, 2019, 10:29pm #9

if they’re not auxy users, then export wouldn’t work the same way as it does in auxy. you should get the app “documents by readdle”, it’s a smart way to save audio files and samples directly to your device. after you have it downloaded, i recommend having them send you the finished song as a .wav file. you should be able to open the file in the documents app and save it there. then go to soundcloud.com and click the upload option. from there, you should get 3 options for where to open the file from. click “browse”, and your saved song should show up in the documents app folder. then you could upload from there. it sounds like a lot of work, but it’s actually really easy to do and it will save a lot of time.
