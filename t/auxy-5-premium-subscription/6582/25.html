From the Subscription FAQ:

What happens to my sound packs if I cancel my subscription?
Any individually purchased sound packs will always be available, regardless of whether you have an active subscription or not.

What happens to my projects if I cancel my subscription?
Your projects will always be available in iCloud, regardless of whether you are an active subscriber or not. You will always be able to export your projects and create new renders. However, if you want to edit any loops using premium sounds or imported samples, you have to have an active subscription.
