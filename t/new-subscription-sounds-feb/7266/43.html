DH0:

some other vintage keys

Indeed. A Vintage Keys Vol. 2 would be welcome, with more classic electric pianos and organs.

Proper Rhodes (Mark One*) with variable tone, drive and tremolo, Wurlitzer, Farfisa, Hammond, Vox Continental, Mellotron (multiple voicings)…

Problem is, any one of those could take up a whole pack of their own to even try to reflect the possibilities of the source instrument, not to mention the shaping controls required to get the best out of them – e.g. tone, drive, tremolo speed, tremolo depth, …

Would be great if they could find a way to have a few major vintage pieces, but increase the shaping controls to allow flexibility within each of the samples.

(Imo, some shaping controls should be added at a universal level – tremolo (speed and depth) and vibrato (speed and depth) would make useful additions across the board.) Imo, they should be available universally (per instrument), like LPF / HPF, Volume, Delay, etc…

I don’t think one instrument (with multiple soundings) for a sound pack is going to meet the expectations of most Auxy subscribers – especially if that instrument is a vintage piece.

If the tremolo / vibrato controls mentioned above where introduced universally**, that would free up Tone and Shape for custom shaping, specific to each instrument in a pack.

 
All that said, I suspect a Vintage Keys Vol. 2 is unlikely given how relatively little I seem to hear the existing Vintage Keys pack being used. I think maybe you and I are the only ones who would appreciate a Vol. 2.

 
* I have a pretty sweet Rhodes Mark One external VST (for use with Ableton Live), but it would be great to have something like this available within Auxy – my go-to idea creation and early development tool.

** + ADSR
