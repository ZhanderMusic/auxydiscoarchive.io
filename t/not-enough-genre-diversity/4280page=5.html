It’s not just about standing out in the community – being ‘Auxy famous’.

From the Auxy makers POV, it’s partly about having access to the audience beyond this community. Auxy SC channel, as a hub for both music making and mobile music making generally, and Auxy music specifically, has attracted a following of over 2600 on SC to-date. That’s around 10 X as many as the most popular Auxy user, and quite possibly more than every Auxy user combined (given that Auxy users probably have a lot of the same followers).

Now, 2600 is still small in the scale of things, but it’s potentially a good springboard to larger audiences.

I’ve started getting signs of interest and appreciation from beyond this community since the repost. My numbers across the board, while still tiny compared to some, have seen a significant jump.
If I was serious about music, I could take advantage of that bump to enageg that new audience and build on it.

So, it’s understandable that users would like access to that type of promotion – and trust that they have a fair chance of getting it.

(It’s perhaps significant – and telling — that the track that was reposted was the most overtly EDM thing I’d ever produced, rather than any of the arguably better, more complete, but still ‘not typically Auxy’ tracks I’d made.
