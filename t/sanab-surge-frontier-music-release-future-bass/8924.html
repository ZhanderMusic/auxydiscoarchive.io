akabillposters June 27, 2018, 8:43am #2

This just rolled up after listening to another Auxian’s track…

Surprised there’s been no engagement here.

Quite a simple track. The piano chord progression and the way you resolve the progression actually makes the bar count feel a little odd.

i.e…
Bar 5 feels like an extra bar added to the first progression/run-through – which sounds quite satisfying.
Bar 6 sounds like an inversion of the chord from Bar 1, creating the impression that the progression has started a second run-through.
However, the progression ends just two bars later, which makes the drop feel likes it’s coming in a bar early, creating the feel (to me*) that the bars are actually 5 + 3 (rather than 4 + 4)

Generally, the progression feels ambiguous.

I’m assuming the track won’t be updated, but maybe consider adding in a greater sense of build towards drops/choruses, especially when the structure isn’t so standard.

 
Maybe it’s just locked in my head that way now – ‘Yanny/Laurel’-style – and it’s hard to hear if differently.

 
Btw, feels like it’s really suitable for vocals.
I started mumbling vocal lines from the very first bar.

At 2’43", it’s quite short, which makes me think there’s space for an additional bridge, where everything is stripped back to the same simplicity of the first few bars, before coming back to the drop/chorus/chords.

 
Not sure I’d call it Future Bass. The chorus/drop is definitely FB, but the main thrust of the track is a house vibe.
Future House?

I don’t know enough about FB or Future House to say for sure, but my instincts say that FB isn’t the right primary genre.
