To prevent this from becoming a self promo dump thread, why don’t people explain their choices more.

I’ll explain a bit more about my previous choices.

Toughest: I chose two non-Auxy songs for this category. The first one, motion, is a self released track that I posted a few months ago. The track is definitely a top choice because the drop is pretty unexpected and hits hard. The other choice, variable, is my first non-Auxy song that had an official release. It was released with Tesseract Records earlier in the year. I chose this one because the punchy drums along with the gritty bass and lead patterns make the drop very hard hitting.

Chillest: I chose my track hologram, which I released with Ridgeline back when I was using Auxy as my primary DAW. I still hadn’t found my niche yet and this track reflects some experimentation with the chill genre. I would have picked purple magic for this one but I personally like hologram more.

Weirdest: If you haven’t heard my music, I make some pretty weird and experimental stuff. Most of the stuff on my main SoundCloud page after February is really weird since I became more interested in the experimental bass genre at that time.

Catchiest: I chose Insomnia, my first label release with Auxy (and in general). The song was released with Frontier last August. You may notice that the track is very different from my current stuff. At that time, I was making more melodic EDM stuff, but I still used some experimentation in the track. The song is so catchy because I spent a long time perfecting the main melody until it was perfect.

Most underrated: My track monsoon season was released about a month ago as the debut single for an album I’m working on. The track is currently sitting just below 300 plays, which is one of the lowest tracks on my page. I was pretty proud of this track so I’m hoping the track will get more exposure once the full album is released.
