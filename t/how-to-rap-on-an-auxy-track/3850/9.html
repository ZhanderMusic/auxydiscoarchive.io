akabillposters November 22, 2017, 3:50pm #22

Or Ableton Live, of course, for pro.

Logic, Ableton Live, etc… are powerful, but relatively complex pieces of software, that can be intimidating.
The Ableton Live UI definitely stalled my interest in diving deeper for a while.

Over the coming years, I think we’ll start to see simpler UIs enter the DAW market, at the ‘pro-enough’ end of things.

Auxy are certain other mobile music apps are part of that movement. Just compare Auxy to something like iMPC, which is more of a legacy-style UI.

Yes, there’s a power differential at the moment, but I definitely think we’ll see ‘lite-but-pro’ DAWs, certainly as the notion of the mobile, professional musician gets proper traction.
