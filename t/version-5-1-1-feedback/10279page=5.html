Nah, it goes on to say “solely and never for any other purpose than for the operation of the App (or future software, applications and services provided by Auxy that are similar to those represented by the App) and as necessary to allow you to share your Music with other users of the App.”
I don’t personally feel like that’s a big deal. If I were to make a very successful track I’d be thrilled for them to demo my production.
Auxy would still have to be cautious about sounds an artist has used in their composition that the artist has rights to that Auxy does not, so while they could demo your production for educational purposes, if you have non-Auxy sounds that you have license to or that are your own IP they’re not likely to risk it for anything else. You’re not going to see Auxy advertise, publish, or publicly brag about a user’s work. Not without the artist’s permission.
