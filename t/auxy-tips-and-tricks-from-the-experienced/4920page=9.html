NotMiles April 19, 2020, 7:38am #184

A trick I’ve been using since the pianos and pitch automation came out: take whatever loop you have and raise it an octave in the note editor. Then drop it down to its original octave with a pitch automation (nothing fancy, literally just a straight line). Boom, now the piano has a slightly washed out tone. I actually do this with the majority of instruments to bring out a darker tone. (Doesn’t work well with all of the instruments however.) I’ve literally stopped using Silk without doing this to it.
