It’s not a big risk at all. But in the mind of the developers, their inclusion isn’t pushing subscriptions. And apparently they’re “lower quality” (that’s one of the justifications given previously, let me find the post)

(From Auxy 5.1.0 Thread)
Also note that we have replaced the original demo sounds with a smaller set of “free” sounds. The main reason is that we want to increase the quality of the sounds that new users get to test. We also want to make it clear that Auxy is a premium app and that we are committed to building an awesome experience for paying users. Thanks everyone for supporting!

However, this changed in the official thread that backtracked the demo sound removal in April, to only include the part about Auxy being a premium app. Not to accuse a bait-and-switch PR-friendly excuse here, but the missing explanation can tell quite a lot about true intentions.
