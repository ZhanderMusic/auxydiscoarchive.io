My cousin does music really well and he told me something so simple long ago that stuck. Major sounds (mostly) happy, Minor sounds sad, Harmonic minor may sound scary.

I thought of it like

Major Smile
Minor Smile

and never forgot it.

Start there. Don’t be afraid to start over and learn the basics again. I am learning french and have apps that are meant for kids on my iPad. I’m not too good to learn the basics. The language apps sometimes have full sentences when I can admit to myself that I wanna know shapes, colors, and the alphabet like toddlers need to learn. I need to learn the same. I would look up on google the definition of music and sound. Forget the definitions to melody and arpeggio… what does sound mean? You can’t expect to be Skrillex if you aren’t willing to go super basic even if it seems silly. We all have to learn to sit before we crawl, walk, jog, then run. Be curious to look for details. Be willing to know the basics to everything and why things are the way they are. That will help so much!
