What two sounds combine good to create one lead?

Production Talk
primary868 October 28, 2017, 6:23pm #1

I’ve ran into a bit of a creative block with most of my Auxy projects, and I find that the problem is that I run out of good sounds to use. A workaround I’ve discovered for this is to use two sounds with the same loops to create one, different sound. The problem is that I’m not quite finding what I’m looking for, which is a lead that would fit well for a trance track (I am trying to create trance music since it is one of my favorite genres).

TLDR: Has anybody else found any good combinations of sounds to create a solid (but not bombastic) lead that would fit well for trance music?
