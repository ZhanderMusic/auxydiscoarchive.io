akabillposters July 21, 2018, 10:25am #16
TheRealJFalc:

do not use anything outside of the original Auxy sample pack for drums . This is because everything else is licensed from BlackOctopus, and I don’t believe that this sort of thing would be permissible or ideal

To add a little more to @iammane…

Legally, Auxy users aren’t required to adhere to Black Octopus’ original T&Cs – only Auxy’s.
It’s Auxy’s legal obligation to ensure that rights they give/pass on to Auxy users don’t infringe on any rights agreed between Auxy and BlackOctopus.

(Usage terms agreed with Auxy might differ from those BlackOctopus offer directly to end-users.)

So, if Auxy T&Cs don’t distinguish between Auxy-created and 3rd-party licensed sounds, you can do it using any sound available in the app.

 
Generally speaking, reselling sounds from sound packs is prohibited if you’re simply trying to resell the generic, unaltered sounds – rather than selling music compositions created using them.

What you’re suggesting is effectively the same as ‘selling beats’ composed using a sample pack. Totally fine.
