UPDATE

I’ve experimented a lot, and someone helped me out too. But, there are ways to make Neuro basses.
Since a few weeks ago my dnb style completely changed because Hexx found out a way to make Reese/Neuro basses, by using sweet spring, pitch all the way down, and bitcrusher, also some chorus.
I like to layer it with sweet saw and mix it the exact way as I do with sweet spring.
Needy can be a very good second Neuro bass, so for example the first 2 bars sweet spring and saw, and the third and fourth needy, with pitch to make it interesting.
I’ll try to find more neuro basses so more updates might be coming
