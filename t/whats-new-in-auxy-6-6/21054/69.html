What about: Waveform Instrument Creator

So what if you want a square wave and JUST A SQUARE WAVETM, you go to Drums and Samples, then select Waveform Instrument. Then, edit the waveform in this cool menu similar to the Pan automation display. After that, you can make that an instrument and stuff.

The customization options for the waveform instrument are really large. For starters, you can select from preset waves, or make your own and save it as a preset! After that, you can do some really epic stuff with it. Check Beepbox’s custom instrument options. That’s the options you’ll get in the waveform!

The waveform instrument could revolutionize videogame-style track making as we know it. There’s also no reason people like me (aka. demo users) should have this, so it’s another thing to pile onto the things you’ll get by getting the subscription.

Unfortunately, I can’t make good mock-ups, so that will come later on.

The waveform instrument, a must-have/QoL feature for video game track makers.
