Cheddr June 15, 2021, 10:23am #41

Can anyone clarify how the previous versions worked before master effects on/off was implemented?

Was it always on previously and now we can turn it off?

What is the use case for this?

iammane June 15, 2021, 12:45pm #42

YO! Correct, it was always on, except if you exported uncompressed wave (and maybe stems, I can’t guarantee there though).

The use case here is for folks who do post processing once the audio is out of Auxy, your mix could be substantially different in Auxy than what the app spit out, so it was hard to tell how to mix down in app knowing you’d put the audio somewhere else for a final touch. Now you can turn it off in app, bypassing the secret sauce, and know exactly what your output will sound like for work in another app / DAW / whatever.
