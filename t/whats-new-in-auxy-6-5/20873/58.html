Auxy April 24, 2021, 7:28pm #65

I checked this and Stress used to have a Tone macro that was assigned to distortion mix, as well as a shape macro assigned to release. These were both removed when we updated to new macros, and replaced with dist and the actual release control. So there were no shape and tone macros in previous versions, i.e. since after the old sounds got an update button.
