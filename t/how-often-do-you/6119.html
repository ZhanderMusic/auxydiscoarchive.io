akabillposters January 12, 2018, 8:27am #8

Invest a little time putting together a ‘system’ for your covers.
i.e. a consistent approach for visuals, typography, etc…, but with an element that varies.
Something good looking and representative of your music/style (‘brand’).

By having a system, you reduce the amount of new thinking required for each new cover, allowing you to put together new, high-quality cover art more quickly and easily.

I did that myself recently, moving my own generic, ‘it doesn’t matter’ cover artwork to something more graphic and useful. It’s simple, impactful, distinct - and easy to iterate on.
