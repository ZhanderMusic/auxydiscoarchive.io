akabillposters October 28, 2017, 10:44pm #26

Debatable.

Music without discernible regular rhythm would be considered to have no beats/pulses.
There are many types of non-rhythmic/arrhythmic music including ambient, drone, free jazz, free improv, avant-garde/experimental, noise, chanting, …

However, ‘0 BPM’ could also suggest that there are discernible beats/pulses in the composition, but that the playing tempo is 0, which would mean the music doesn’t actually begin… which could be described as ‘no song’.

…which actually raises some interesting art/conceptual possibilities.
