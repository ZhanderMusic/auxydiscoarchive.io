Well for one, in the music industry, you have to invest in things.

You have to invest money.
Whether it be gear (software, hardware), talent (singers, session players, etc), tools (sample packs, plugins, etc), or even Facebook ads, you’ve got to spend a bit.

And, you’ve got to invest time.
It’s not just learning how to “make beats,” or learning how to make a new genre, but learning the craft, how to get better, and finding your “sound.”
And going out to clubs and parties to listen to new/interesting stuff that will grow your musical palette.
Or, meeting more artists out there who know more than you so that you can learn from them.

Auxy can be a tool, for some, but can be a comfort zone for others.
