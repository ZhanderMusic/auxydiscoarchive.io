Db909 August 3, 2017, 2:56pm #2
  1. Find a bass sound you want to wub and program a pattern with lots of sustained notes.
  2. Automate the low pass filter up and down in time to create desired wub pattern

Optional: automate ducker and whatever tone controls the sound has for added spice.

Mix to taste.

I would but I don’t do a lot wubs in my music. I just happen to know the recipe. But you can think of it in a similar fashion to the ducker when you crank that up and hear the pumping. You’re simply creating that pump with the filter instead (and controlling the rhythm) which takes it from a pump to a wub, so to speak.
