Genre/genre labelling is about relevance.

Of course, genre is certainly secondary to the music itself*, but accurate and useful genre labelling is important for helping others find the music they want to hear.

That includes automated recommendation engines, such as those used by SC, Spotify, etc…
It allows them to recommend your stuff to the most welcoming and appreciative listeners.

 
* Unless you’re making music aimed at a certain genre.
