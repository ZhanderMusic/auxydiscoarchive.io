Auxy June 22, 2017, 11:08am #6

Great question!

The reason we setup the account is that we wanted a way to show people just how great the music is that’s coming out of the community. The selection is definitely biased since it’s done by me and based on my personal preference. It’s not just about picking tracks that I like though, but I’m trying to showcase the breadth and depth of what you’re able to make with the app.

The reason some names are more familiar than others is simply that they have a lot of experience and tend to make really good tracks. However, nothing makes me more excited than finding new producers who make great stuff and I’m doing my best to keep track of what people make.

Submitting tracks to ‘Beat the Clock’ is definitely the best way to get noticed in case we haven’t found you yet. In addition, if you find someone who you think is really talented that we haven’t noticed, let us know here in the forum!

I should also say that even though we think it’s fun to showcase people’s tracks, we want to focus mainly on building a great app and you’ll have to find additional ways of getting your music heard.
