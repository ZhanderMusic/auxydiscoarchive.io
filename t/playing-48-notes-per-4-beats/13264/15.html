Plhector December 17, 2018, 6:35am #21


First you have to put a 32nd note on the second space of the grid and put a 24th note next to it as shown.

Then you copy and paste both notes all the way at the bottom of the grid, but make sure to have the grid at 32 notes or else it won’t work.

image.jpeg640×508 24.5 KB

Next you copy and paste the notes above each other as shown.
image.jpeg640×640 29.7 KB

You’ll have to delete the 32nd notes for this next step.
image.png640×1136 19.2 KB

(This is the most annoying part.) you’ll have to copy and past above each note on a 24th note grid to get this to work. Keep pasting until you get 2 notes on one 24th note square.
image.png640×1136 17.9 KB

Delete the notes that don’t fit and you should end up with this. If you have any trouble, let me know and I’ll see if I can help. Have fun with this insane discovery! You’ll now be able to go up a whole octive in one beat. Crazy, right!? 12 notes per beat! You’re welcome everyone!

If you’re still having trouble get the 48th notes there here is a project link:
https://app.auxy.co/projects/9ScvCfNRX_55QuTuOHb3RA==
It’s The Word II by Shigeo Sekito! Enjoy my recreation of it on Auxy using 48th notes
