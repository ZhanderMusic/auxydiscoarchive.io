Zeph January 29, 2018, 6:07am #14

Think about what you want to accomplish with the melody, if you want an interesting uplifting melody, big gaps between pitches is key. If you want a trap kind of melody, keep the melody pitches as close as possible, with a variation every other bar. I usually start with one or three notes, then imagine what comes next. Usually the first note is always the key note.
