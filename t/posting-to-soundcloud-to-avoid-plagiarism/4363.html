Flawless logic.

 
Of course, posting on SC doesn’t really prevent plagiarism.
In some ways it’ll discourage it because you’ve published it publicly and it’s associated with your account and a date.
However, the more you share, the more people see it – and the more likely it becomes that someone will plagiarise it.

The good news is that plagiarism is still extremely rare in the scale of things.
Unless you’ve been targeted (repeatedly), it’s not something worth getting paranoid about.

That said, I’d typically recommend against sharing a project file unless you’re inviting others to work on it, adapt it, whatever. No point in making it easy for anyone who might consider ripping your stuff.
