TheRealJFalc January 12, 2019, 6:18pm #2

Are you talking about something akin to the original Auxy Creator Studio app (The green one)? It had something similar to this, but it only had three knobs for use. Development on that app was shut down and the app was (unfortunately) hidden from the app store, but it had some useful features that some (namely @blakkaz) still use today.

It would be interesting to see something like this in the future, but it would cause the app to be less streamlined, which is against the design philosophy of Auxy as stated by Lenberg. It’s unlikely such an addition will ever make its way into Auxy, at least in the near future.

All auxy would need to do would be to support some form of MIDI out in order to make that a reality, even if it wasn’t directly built for Auxy it could theoretically work through a standard MIDI format. True but it feels like major ailment having to work around that way from a work flow stand point especially for multiple complex drum patterns, when you factor Ableton export has already been rolled out in the application.

Separate sample stem exports cannot be that much harder to implement unless i’m missing something?

eg. lets say you have 4 drum variation patterns consisting of kick snare hihat,

Scenario 1:
3 sample banks x 4 patterns

  • You bounce between your pre-made drum sample banks your creative workflow/speed and the song vibe is going to suffer having to jump between drum sample banks to program the drums.

Scenario 2:
1 sample bank x 4 patterns.

  • You finish the drums and then duplicate the sample bank 2 times (for a total sum of 3 drum banks) and rename each bank as kick, snare and hi-hat.
  • You go into each sample bank and delete the duplicate drum samples for each pattern for a total of 12 touches.
  • You are now ready to export the song as individual drum stems.

Requested Scenario:
1 sample bank x 4 patterns.

  • You hit export song and the sample bank is exported by stem by stem for mixing/mastering (common practice)
