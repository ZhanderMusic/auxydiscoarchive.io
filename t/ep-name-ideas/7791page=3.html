Ep name ideas

Branding & Distribution
BALLISTIK February 20, 2018, 6:36pm #41

I’ve got some ideas, but I can’t tell you anything unless you provide specifics.

Here are some questions to maybe ask yourself:

  • What do all your tracks have in common?

  • What is special about your style or this EP in particular, especially when compared to your other music?

  • How does listening to you EP make you feel?

  • What is your ultimate goal in releasing this EP (and your music in general?)

  • What do the tracks on your EP bring to mind?

  • Are there any themes that characterize your EP?
