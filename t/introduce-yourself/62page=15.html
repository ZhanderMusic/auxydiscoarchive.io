Hi, I’m W7SEGUY and I actually joined this forum a long time ago but never posted anything. But now that there’s a new BTC challenge I would like to introduce myself.
I’m 38 years old and started making music when I downloaded an amazing and incredible app called Auxy! I can honestly say that it’s one of the best things that has happened to me, because I never thought that I would be capable of creating music.
I mean I always dreamed of being a songwriter or composer, because to me music is an essential part of life. I love music so much! But I just figured that creating music is a talent that I don’t possess and never even bothered to pick up an instrument to learn. Then back in October 2016 I was browsing the App Store for fun new apps and games when I saw Auxy in the featured section. I read the description and became interested, so I decided to try it out. Well, to me it became a life changing moment and it still is. I’ve made so much music with this app and I’m very pleased with the results so far. I learned a lot and consider myself more of a composer than producer. I still have to work more on my producing and mixing skills.
I’ll be releasing more music soon and I’m really excited about it! Till now I only put my beat the clock entries on soundcloud. Here’s #14:
