Zeph June 27, 2017, 11:16pm #59

Howdy folks, my name is Zeph and I am 18, and I began teaching myself music theory, mixing, and synthesis last year. I had no prior knowledge on the subject but I have managed to make something of myself. I started out teaching myself piano and getting accustomed to musical ideas and learning songs from tutorials and YouTube, then on Garageband for iOS, messing around with apple loops. With the positive feedback that I recieved from family and friends I continued my journey, transitioning into Garageband for Mac. From the videos and articles I read, I started getting serious and forming a solid foundation on which to build upon. From there, I bought Logic and a good pair of headphone studio monitors. But I had a problem, either buy a macbook so that I could use Logic on the go, or find something I could form musical phrases on, which is how I found Auxy :). Auxy really made it effortless to cancel out my teachers at highschool, and dedicate myself 100% to music production. I could make samples with the tools auxy provided and export them to garageband, tinker further, and then export that to logic when I got home. All in all, Auxy has provided me with a satisfying experience I just wish that Auxy continues updating its pseudo DAW so that I can remove the middle man and just make complete songs in Auxy easily, without getting headaches from the obstructive UI.
