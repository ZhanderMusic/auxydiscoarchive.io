Tarekith August 8, 2017, 4:01pm #46

My bit of advice is don’t feel you HAVE to keep piling on layers all the time. Often times a few key sounds playing can be more powerful that having a lot of things doubled-up. A lot of the sounds in Auxy are already quite full, and with the behind the scenes master buss processing, it can be easy to make things too muddy sounding when you get too much happening at once.
