Branding & Distribution
akabillposters June 29, 2018, 9:00am #23

If scouting through Discord servers, try to find ones that have other channels, such as YT, SC or even a popular Spotify playlist.

Alternatively, track down the community spots (incl. discord servers, etc…) for popular promo/repost channels and labels – and try to get on those.

My best advice for getting more recognition is getting in contact with other artists and forming partnerships. Between you and the other artists you support each other’s music so you’ll be getting plays from them and in many cases you’ll be reposting each other’s music, allowing for that other artists audience to check out your music as well. It’s always good to make connections with others😄
