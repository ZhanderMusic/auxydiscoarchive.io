caldera January 25, 2018, 8:27pm #505

I think the update is awesome, I had bought all the AP and I subscribed immediately, for me it’s a bargain.

BUT

I think the reason people have a problem with this subscription system is that it’s the same price for everyone even though Auxy has a different value for every individual. When I discovered Auxy I spent the whole night playing with it, I was completely hooked, and skipped work the following day. I’m self employed, meaning I lost 500Eur just to play with Auxy ! I showed it to a friend who bought an Ipad Pro just for Auxy! People pay 1000$ for the OP-1 even though Auxy is much better … So for people like us 5eur/month is more than a bargain, it’s actually way too cheap. On the other hand, I remember when I was 16 I wouldn’t have paid anything, all my software was pirated. A subscribtion would have been out of question. Yet it’s the young guys who have all the time and energy to create an awesome community and all the content that inspire us.

Also I liked the soundpacks very much as every time it was an opportunity to play with new sounds. With the all you can eat buffet of the subscription I fear that the new sounds will be lost in the crowd.

I think you should keep the soundpack & the subscription system available. Another possibility is to let people pay 5 bucks to use 10 sounds of their choice from the library / iCloud.

Also I think there’s a lot of money to be made with iconic ‘nostalgic’ instruments for old people like me. I would be willing to pay 50eur without thinking for a good 303, or a complete 808 drumkit. Ask your local music shop where they make money from, I guess it’s not from the young or the pro, but from middle aged guys who finally have the money to buy the musical instruments they wish they had when they were kids xD

Anyway It’s heartbreaking to hear that you’re barely sustainable with such an insanely great app. I hope the subscription system works for you, but as far I’m concerned, you’re leaving money on the table. Please rip me off !
