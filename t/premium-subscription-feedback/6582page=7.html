NotMiles January 24, 2018, 10:30pm #134

I bought the subscription, but I’d have to agree some of the complaints that I’ve seen so far. Having a monthly/yearly subscription alienates over half of the Auxy community that don’t make an income (ie, “Parental Unit, ples gib monies for de muziks”). I think it would be a better idea if there were two options for getting the new features:

  1. The current subscription model, but instead of allowing access as long as you pay, you would instead get all the new content as soon as it comes out (at a discount) [and possibly some exclusive content] and the stuff would stay on your phone even if you opt out.

  2. A way for non-subscribers to buy the sounds a pack at a time, like it was in the past. This could work if we had an “AuxyShop” where you can buy sound packs (this part could be tough to set up since there’s only two devs)

That way, those who can’t afford a subscription can still get new sounds to use and getting a subscription will still be a perk.
