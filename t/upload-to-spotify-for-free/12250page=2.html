akabillposters September 25, 2018, 6:25am #30
XLXK:

Tags do exist on Spotify, but they are automated

Not entirely. There are also crowdsourced tag and metadata contributions via Spotify’s Line In community.

Much of it is pulled in from sources like Gracenote or new associations built by making assumptions based on aggregated patterns of how users listen and save artists.

e.g. if enough users who listen to a lot of rap/hip-hop also listen to [insert new artist], then the algorithm and staff will likely initially categorise the new artist as rap/hip-hop too and potentially show them as a related artist to other hip-hop artists. It’ll fine tune the profile as more data comes in.
