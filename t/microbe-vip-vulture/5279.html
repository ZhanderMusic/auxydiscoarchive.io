Polar December 20, 2017, 6:15am #3

Keep in mind while reading my review that I am listening without headphones, and if you think this song is a lot better with headphones, or there is a certain part I criticized that sounds way better with headphones, let me know and I’ll listen again tomorrow. Also keep in mind that this is not intended to be hurtful or offend you, this is all constructive criticism mixed with some of my own personal opinions. If you don’t agree with something, than that’s fine, but maybe you should at least try a suggest I include.

Ooh, the intro sounds kinda like the Bojack Horseman intro, I like that. As much as I like it with less lowpass, I don’t really like it as much when it’s fully faded in. It’s really obnoxious and in your face kinda. Some might disagree. The melody of this part I just don’t get, and nothing in me wants to get up and move to this because they’re slightly confused how to move to this. Really odd transition into the piano, it just feels really out of place. It struck out of no where. It was especially surprising with not knowing when the first run stopped or began. I really like the chords, nice! The buildup scene has got a lot of cool sounds playing around, and it builds up nice and smoothly. The drop is kind of a let down, imo. The main tune doesn’t have much support, and doesn’t give off an “epic” or “big” vibe that the buildup anticipated. And the snare. Oh, the snare. As cool and funky as it sounds, it’s just so out of place. It doesn’t fit the song and even sounds a Little’s offbeat because of the weirdness, even though it’s not. 2nd part of the drop is still meh, the only change other than the hats (which isn’t the best hat choice imo) is the piano dropping down a scale, which makes it sound weaker. After the drop: once again, I like the chords. I wish they could be more implemented into the drop, though. One thing I’ve noticed is that the progression and the drop sound like they come from multiple songs. The 2nd buildup and drop are the same as the first, which sounds repetitive. You should switch it up. I like the quick and dramatic ending.

In conclusion: Good song, cool effects, good ideas, but poorly executed. I hope you can agree with my feedback and take it in to make your next track even better.
