iammane August 3, 2020, 10:52am #26

No sweat! Yeah the sound selection methodology in Auxy is a blessing and a curse. Sometimes it’s nice to just click around and not really know what you’re gonna find, and sometimes I have a very specific idea of what I’m looking for and WHALE or MENTAL or OOF don’t really tell you much about the sound.

Sennheiser Pro HD 380

sennheiser1204×1264 527 KB

EXTREMELY flat response, pretty cost efficient ($150) - these are not listening cans, for sure, they’re monitoring headphones. But this is really the kind of thing you want to make sure your mix is as even as possible. There’s lots of other great options in similar price ranges but anything with BASS BOOST or any other honky tonky crap you want to avoid for a serious mix session.

If I didn’t have to worry about noise levels I probably would have gotten open back cans. Maybe next time.
