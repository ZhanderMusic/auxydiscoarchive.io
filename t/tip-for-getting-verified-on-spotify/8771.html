Tip for getting verified on Spotify

Branding & Distribution
anon73773654 March 24, 2018, 4:39am #1

Hey y’all,

So, a lot of you in here are on Spotify, but a few of you aren’t verified. Meaning, you can’t change your profile photo or your bio.

The thing is, no matter how many links you post in the Spotify Artist app to get verified, the most important thing is posting your email on the sites you share.

I didn’t post my email on my SC before, and I still used the link to get verified. 2 months later and I still got no reply. Then, after talking with the Spotify artist help (realllly speedy replies!) they said it was absolutely necessary to share the email you used for your non-artist Spotify account on your sites.

I did this literally five minutes ago, and I got verified two minutes later, no kidding.

Just a little tip. You guys can share your helpful hints too xD
