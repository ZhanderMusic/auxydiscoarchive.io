Welcome to my remix of the Auxy demo for Ambient Rimshots! I threw a whole bunch of reverb and delay on the rimshots and added some heavy dubstep to it. I think it worked really well! Been dying to use these things in a track for so long but I’ve never found a good use for them in my new music approach, so I’m very glad that this project idea panned out as well as it did.
