akabillposters May 7, 2018, 3:42pm #12
DJ_La_Rocca:

Original name? It’s way too late to change after 3 years

Fwiw, it’s not about the length of time, but about the size of the audience.

No-one here has an audience so large that changing their name is particularly risky.

The SC Followers and YT Subscribers you have now will still be automatically following you if you change your SoundCloud artist name and YouTube channel name.

If the name you have now isn’t ‘right’, change it. It’s certainly better to change it now while we have tiny audiences, than later once the audience has significant numbers and new listeners start searching for you by name.

Far better that the other 99.999% of your future audience experience you with the name you think is best, rather than worrying what the 0.001% of that audience that you have now think.
