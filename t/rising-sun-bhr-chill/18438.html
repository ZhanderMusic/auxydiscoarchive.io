Nice work! It is very reminiscent of Tycho, which I do not say lightly! About to press play on it for the 3rd time today. There honestly isn’t much I have to say in terms of critique. Just FYI, it’s not a trance song. Trance is signified by a kick drum on every downbeat and an open hat on every upbeat. So trance has a signature sound that goes “boom cch boom cch boom cch”, and usually has a very heavy kick that you feel in your chest.
