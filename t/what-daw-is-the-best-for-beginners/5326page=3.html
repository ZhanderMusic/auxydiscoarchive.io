NickElle December 29, 2017, 9:19pm #56

But, here’s something that people keep coming back to:

They say Auxy music is “just as good as other music made on with a DAW.”

I don’t believe that the statement above is a good one because a DAW is NOT the GOAL.
The goal is making great music.

A great song can come from tons of places. It can come from anywhere*, like a dual-deck tape player (overdubbing loops tons of times), a sampler, a mixer and an audio recorder, a DAW, or most recently, the iOS app (Auxy, Medly, Groovebox, etc). So, there is no need for comparing “Auxy music” to “DAW music,” because there is NO SUCH THING. It’s just music. GREAT music, to be exact.

*But, different ways to make music have different limitations. For example, even though recording with tape is cool, it sucks because it takes FOREVER, and with a mixer, you need to have some experience as an audio engineer to get anywhere, because it’s not very easy to only be able to listen to sound and not see it.
So, just like those classic methods, Auxy has it’s limitations, too.
