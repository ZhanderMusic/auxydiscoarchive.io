Polar October 17, 2017, 11:01am #7

I used to decide how the song is going to be before I make it. As I started getting better, it was harder to come up with good ideas on the spot.

Now, whenever I need an idea, I take a nice warm shower and let my imagination flow. My most recent song was straight from the shower.

I don’t come up with the whole song in the shower though, just the drop. When I’m out of the shower I’ll record my voice making the drop, and then on the bus to school I’ll try to recreate it. And then I’ll form the rest of the song around it.
