What law is that?
Afaik, there’s no legal age limit, but there are legal requirements that sites catering to users (including those below 13 y/o) are required to include in their Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

Some sites choose not to jump through the extra hoops, so simply don’t allow those below 13 y/o. In those cases, it’s not a law preventing pre-teens from using the site, it’s the site owner choosing not to allow them.

At least, that’s how I’ve understood US’s COPPA.
(I’m assuming this forum is hosted in the US, hence US legal jurisdiction.)

It’s not illegal to break T&Cs, at least, not in the sense I think you have in mind. They fall under contract law, so it’s a Civil thing, not a Criminal thing.
