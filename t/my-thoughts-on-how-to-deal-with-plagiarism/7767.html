My thoughts on how to deal with plagiarism

Production Talk
crystldawnmusic February 19, 2018, 2:24am #1

It’s definitely an issue, no question. When I run into stolen tracks, I usually just send the person who posted them a PM and they take it down and apologize. It can even turn into a friendly conversation.

What I personally son’t think is necessary is chewing them out in public chats, threatening to attack their SoundCloud, etc. I’ve seen some people do this, and it really doesn’t help anything. If anything, it raises more attention to something that can be kept private in the first place! It also makes the person who said that look immature.

If people will steal music more than once, then it’s a bigger deal. At this point, I’d send them a ton of comments, maybe even report them if it was serious.

What no one should ever do, never, is hate on people. There are better ways to deal with it. Hating gets no one anywhere and creates mistrust and hard feelings. We don’t need that.

Just needed to put this out there.
~c
