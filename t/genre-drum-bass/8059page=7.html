@Twizard raggajungle.biz awesome mixes, sample packs, such a great site. The basses are usually very simple, everything is rooted in dub and reggae. @blakkaz put it best, bass lines are slow, drums are fast, and everything else is just floating around in between as you see fit.

Pick up some classic breaks it’s a big part of the reason why the tracks sound like they do (nothing in Auxy natively sounds like these breaks). Amen, think, funky drummer perhaps. There’s lots to choose from.
