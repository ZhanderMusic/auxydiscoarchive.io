After over a month of music-making, I am excited to release my newest album thingo.

VOXEL CITY ORCHESTRA

VCOCoverArt.png946×946 623 KB

The album is “set” in an imaginary futuristic city (whatever you can imagine).

Here’s a rundown of the songs (with links if you don’t want to listen to the whole album at once):
Voxel City - An ambient opener for the album
Acid Rain - A more cinematic piece
Foggy - A chill & ambient track
Vibrant Night in the City - A future bassy-ish song
Subway Tunnels - A dark closer with a drop inspired by IVO’s deep drops

Enjoy!

Disclaimer: I don’t actually speak Korean. It’s on the album art for the a e s t h e t i c.
