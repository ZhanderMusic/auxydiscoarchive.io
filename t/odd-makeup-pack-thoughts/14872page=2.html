Jax:

There’s going to be inadvertent overlap with any sound, because they all are based off of basic waveforms. Although you can get a lot of variety, not all of it will sound pleasant to the ear. Especially when trying to define slight differences in tone and timbre, they can sound similar. For me personally, even if a sound does sound similar it has a unique character that cannot be ignored.
