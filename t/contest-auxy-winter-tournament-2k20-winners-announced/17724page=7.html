Okay, here we go!

I’ve had a handful of favorite memories over the last decade, and it was difficult to choose. However, one memory in particular stood out to me the most, and that was the day that we got to bring our son home from his 2-week stay in the NICU. Our first car ride together as a family evoked all kinds of emotions. We were terrified because of we were brand new parents, we were relieved that our son was okay, and we were unbelievably excited because we FINALLY got to bring our baby boy home. Most of all, it felt like my life began all over again. No one tells you that becoming a parent has a way of biologically re-hardwiring your brain and fundamentally alters your sense of being. This track is meant to represent that very first drive home together as a family, and the beautiful sense of togetherness that permeated every aspect of that journey.

This is Eight Miles

The only sound used was Gentle Dreamer and a single sample of my voice counting in the track.
