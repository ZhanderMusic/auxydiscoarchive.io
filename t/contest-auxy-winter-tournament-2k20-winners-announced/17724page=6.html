Red Skies - https://soundcloud.com/xenocutter/red-skies-1

edit: forgot about the rule that says it’s supposed to be under a minute lol. cut it down a bit

This song tells my favorite memory, but not my happiest.

About 4 years ago, I was a massive insomniac often staying up for days at a time. As a result, I often got some interesting hallucinations during my awake hours. The thing I remember most about these hallucinations was that the sky was always a bright red in the middle of the night. At the end of those unhealthy sessions, I had a particularly unsettling hallucination, where I could feel and hear everything to an extreme— My heartbeat hurt my ears. That’s when I decided to put an end to my unhealthy habit due to how painful it was; so the first thing I did was close my eyes (indicated by the snap at the end). Happy to say I’m well rested now!

The struggle I had is indicated in the solo’s slow breaking and cutting, and the chords are suspenseful to show how my insomnia had been harming my lifestyle.

Five instruments were Pear, Swank, Pastel, a drumkit, and June.
