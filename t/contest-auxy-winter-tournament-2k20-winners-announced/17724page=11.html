- (hyphen, auxy tournament demo)

https://soundcloud.com/tc64music/hyphen

In October 2018, a friend and I won grand prize (new laptops! ) at a fairly large high school computer programming contest. It was at that moment when I realized how much potential I had - even outside of music production - and that I could take my life wherever I wanted to next, as long as I’m having fun. This song is about this realization, as well as the memories from the event itself - working with my friend on our project until the last minute and throwing together a presentation as fast as we could. We were nervous about what could happen next, but we still enjoyed every moment of it. It all paying off in the end was just a bonus!

As the memory is from the 2010s, the song is also made to be a snapshot of the decade. I fused genres that thrived during the decade like modern dubstep, glitch hop, and future funk - even though the 2020s now are upon us and new genres will inevitably take over, this track is one last look at what defined the 2010s before we take a huge step forward and leave it behind to embrace the future - while still keeping everything we’ve learned.
