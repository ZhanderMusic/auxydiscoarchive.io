Hey guys! So I’ve just finished a new track (see link below) I exclusively used triplets notes for every loop. The idea is that this gives the song a new time signature! Instead of counting a bar as 1,2,3,4 (4/4 time) you count this new layout as 1,2,3,4,5,6 (6/8 time). I wanted to challenge you guys to have a go doing the same! The only “rule” is that everything is snapped to either triplets or precision triplets. If you come up with something post it’s link here! I’d love to see what you guys think of it.
