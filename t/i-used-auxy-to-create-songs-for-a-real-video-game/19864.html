About a year ago, I got hooked up with this dev that was creating his own game called Roobound. He was a legit developer who had worked on games like Uncharted, and Dante’s Inferno.

Here’s a couple images from the game:

image1242×2208 267 KB
image1242×2208 628 KB

I started creating demos with Auxy, and he’d pick which ones he liked, and I’d recreate the demos in Cubase.

I’m super proud of these songs. I don’t think I’ve ever worked harder on anything in my life.

I ended up creating 4 full length songs before he pulled the game because he got a
job at Facebook.

Here are a couple of the songs. I’ll post the rest if yall want to hear the others.
