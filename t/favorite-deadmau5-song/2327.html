I love all of deadmau5’s music overall, but my favorite types of songs he does are:

Those Long Progression Ones: aka the ones that made him famous. Whenever I’m on a long plane trip or car ride, I pull up one of these songs. (ex. Polaris, Strobe, Jaded, Faxing Berlin, Brazil, I Remember)

Power Songs: aka the club bangers. Whenever I’m getting ready for my swim meet or any competition, I load these songs. (ex. Professional Griefers, Avaritia (ish), 2448, No Problem)

The Odd Ones Out: aka the weird ones, not the YouTuber. I just really like these. (ex. Glish, Whelk Then, Take Care of the Proper Paperwork, Careless, Long Walk Off a Short Pier)
