Could you elaborate on what happens when you try to upload? You could try resetting your connection in the Auxy settings, then connect again to SoundCloud. If you can connect properly, it’s usually a problem related to SoundCloud if you cannot upload. E.g. you haven’t confirmed your email or you don’t have enough space in your SoundCloud account.

Also, if you are running a beta version of Auxy alongside the App Store version, it usually messes up the connection flow, so then you need to remove the beta and connect again.
