MrSerpent January 17, 2018, 10:48pm #28

For my soft, flowy, ambient tunes I like to use a variety of bass sounds that sound very quiet.

RAC basses paw and purr are good for this but there are also some really good vanilla basses for this.

My current go-to basses that aren’t from RAC are Pepper and Crater, which, if tuned correctly, can provide the perfect soft sound I crave in my quiet songs.

In the past, I’ve also used Needy, Pasta, and Panda when I didn’t have as much soundpacks (which is what I used for Looking Back)

Interestingly enough it was Akyut who helped me understand how to program and use good bass melodies when he added the needy and pasta in the early stages of making Looking Back
