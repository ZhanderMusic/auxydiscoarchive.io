Here’s comparisons between the old and new stuff.

The old logo is on the left, updated one on the right.

logocompare@3x.png7500×3817 344 KB

old wordmark up top, new wordmark at the bottom.

wordmarkcompare@3x.png4203×2223 89.9 KB

Overall, the logo’s outline is slightly thicker than it used to be, and the beak is more of a curve into a point. I also went over the curves of the logo to make them flow better.

For the wordmark, I started from scratch. This time I focused on making the letterforms more circular (especially the o and the a), then did the rest of the letters to match. As for the negative space, no longer does the negative line go through the r. I tried it, and it didn’t work as well, due to the curve of the letterform. Plus, I felt that it would look a bit tacky to have too much of that line going through the wordmark.

One more thing: I’m in college now, and my major will be Graphic Design. What started out as a passion project is quickly becoming something I wanna work on for a long time, now.

Feedback is appreciated!
