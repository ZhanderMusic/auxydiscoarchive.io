Just a friendly reminder:

How to post your tracks Post Your Tracks
To ensure your Auxy tracks are visible and easy to browse, here’s some guidelines for posting your Auxy tracks. How to Post Your Auxy Tracks Post your completed track in the TRACKS category OR, click here One thread per track, EP or album. Post your work In progress tracks in the WIP category OR, click here One thread per track, EP or album. Threads should be named: [Track_Name] - [Artist] eg: Sonic Passion - StinkFace Tracks without a name can be called: “Untitled - By…

It helps to also put the track name in the title of the post and if possible tag with genre so you can get the appropriate listeners
