Trey:

How will the new graphics affect battery consumption? Is it the graphics that use a lot or is it the processing and stuff that we can’t see?

I suspect it’ll make no noticeable difference.
The vast majority of Auxy’s battery usage is down to live compiling and dynamic rendering of music as you work or play it through from the arrangement bar.

Playing pre-rendered tracks is a lot lighter on the battery as all the clever synth, sample and sound engine calculations have been flattened down to a single audio file.

It doesn’t come ‘out of your pay’ at all.
Only courts, govt agencies and creditors can take amounts directly from your pay.

It comes out of whatever bank account or credit/debit card you have hooked up to your Apple ID / iTunes account.

It’s up to the individual to make sure they’re depositing enough funds and keeping the account topped up enough to cover the outgoing subscriptions each time those payments are due.

If that means having to mow a lawn or two a week before the subs are due, then that’s what you do.
