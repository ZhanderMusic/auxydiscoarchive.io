akabillposters June 22, 2018, 8:27am #11
blakkaz:

I think stock FX (shadows, glows, etc…) are the clearest indicator a logo wasn’t designed by a professional.

As a design professional, I can say that it’s not that black and white.

Shadows, glows, etc… are not innately unprofessional or signs of bad design choices. It’s down to how they’re used. Sure, sometimes, less is more. But similarly, brash, unsubtle effects are sometimes appropriate for the desired aesthetic (e.g. Punk art).

Depends what you’re going for.

If the outcome doesn’t match the intent, then that’s poor design (or poor briefing from the client).
