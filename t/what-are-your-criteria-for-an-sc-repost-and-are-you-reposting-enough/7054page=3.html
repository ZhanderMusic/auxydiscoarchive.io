Arimyth January 31, 2018, 11:04pm #52

You continue to ignore the feedback given on this thread that you claim to seek out. I suggest that you re-read the comments left here so that you can better understand where people are coming from. I will summarize what I interpret to be people’s viewpoints on reposting atleast in the Auxy community:

  1. There is a difference between commenting that something is good and listening to it, and taking up a spot on their own personal profile with your track.
  2. Some people who enjoy your track don’t feel that it is their style of music that they produce, and therefore don’t repost it.
  3. Certain people don’t repost tracks period, whether it be to keep their soundcloud profile “cleaner”, or to highlight their own tracks more without cluttering it up with highlights from other people’s music.
  4. Reposting can be viewed as a type of free promotion for whoever is reposted. That’s not something to necessarily take super lightly. It’s why huge promotional SoundCloud accounts exist. Individual Auxy users can be viewed as a “miniature” version of that.
  5. Sometimes people like your song enough to give you a like or a comment, but not enough to repost it. That’s just a plain and simple fact.

Also note that growing your audience does not involve tons of reposts (while it can involve it, it’s not necessarily true.)
