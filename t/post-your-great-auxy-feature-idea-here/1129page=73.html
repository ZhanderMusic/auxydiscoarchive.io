TheRealJFalc November 14, 2018, 4:29pm #1494

Just some food for thought at potential things I’d really love to see introduced in Auxy in the future

  • A way to customize how much a specific sample triggers the ducker, like in other programs. If I want, say, a weaker duck from a softer kick between beats, I don’t want that kick to push down the volume of everything else too far. A possible way to implement this would be to have two different volumes it could be ducked to, determined by the “soft” (velocity) of the sample being ducked from. This would scale to the ducker value on the instrument’s macro.
  • A way to duck samples would be very nice, although the most optimal feature would really to be able to import samples into a melodic instrument which may not work due to reliability of iCloud access. Thusly, a compromise would be the ability to set the sample to duck with the trigger of another sample.
  • More options for exporting stems. This has been asked for before, yes, but I just want to reiterate that these features would greatly expand the reach of Auxy, as they would offer easier opportunities to be integrated into major artist releases, like Sketchy Bongos. I’ll be putting these ideas into a detailed list below. A note that these would be options for exporting, allowing users to choose what sort of export they want.
Export features
  • Remove delay or reverb from instruments pre-export.
  • Allow users to select which individual instruments are exported
  • Give option for users to export single stems per drum sample, instead of full drum kit loops.
  • Add options to apply Auxy’s mastering algorithm to the exported stems
  • Add the ability to rename/recolor/swap instrument to the instrument sort menu. This one was suggested by someone else, so I’m not taking credit for it, but it would definitely be a nice feature to have.

Hopefully these are seen as positive suggestions, as they are genuinely meant to help improve Auxy as a product.
