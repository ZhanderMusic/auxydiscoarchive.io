Shepdoge:

I would assume that they’re using 12 as the increments that echo throughout the app, based on there being 12 notes in an octave. Some UI elements, like pitch automation optimally fit to 12 (main) increments. (One octave up, one octave down. Hence, 24.)

The ‘simplicity’ mantra behind the UX/UI is likely why they chose to unify/standardise the number of increments.
