akabillposters:

It’s possible to import lossy formats, convert at import and then store only the WAV version, discarding the original format. (Yes, I understand the quality implications of starting with a lossy ‘master’ of a sound file. I just recognise and appreciate the benefits of a less strict policy on importable audio formats.

The problem is that thinking possible = easy to implement in software. So we prefer not to spend resources on implementing something that leads to more potential issues and rather educate people on the benefits of using wav import.
