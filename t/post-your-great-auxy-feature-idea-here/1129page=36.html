Hi guys,

I have two feature requests that should be easy enough to implement.

  1. Shake to Undo
  2. Octave Indicators

Can we add in the iOS functionality of shake to undo into Auxy? Sometimes, I find myself trying to copy/paste notes but accidentally end up deleting them. There’s no getting them back after that. Or sometimes I try to move notes but I put them in the wrong place and then I forget where they were to start. Or sometimes I’m working so quickly to come up with a part that when I liked something but wanted to try one more thing (and it turns out to not work as well as the last thing) I forget what I did last and there’s no going back. Shake to undo would be really helpful in these situations.

When creating or editing a part, I never know which octave I’m on. Auxy appears to have 5 octaves. For example, if I’m writing in the key of C, I would like to know if I’m on C0, C1, C2, C3, C4 or C5. Can we get some sort of indication of which octave we’re on? That would be very helpful.

You can hear them on Soundcloud at the links below.

Thanks again!
