akabillposters August 18, 2017, 4:06pm #1675

I understand what you’re saying @NYC, but the same thing could be said against offering ‘shape’, ‘tone’, LPF and HPF controls. Those don’t appear to have prevented users from getting stuck in quickly.
Every patch will already have ADSR characteristics embedded. It’s just about giving users access to adjust them.
(Of course, users who are intimidated by ADSR can simply go with whatever the patch’s defaults are.)

In my view, by opening up the existing and future libraries in this way, it would create innumerable new options similar to the effect that a dozen or two dozen brand new sound packs would have.
