Seconded.
Some aspects of the sounds are immutable, such as the fixed modulation on the sharp attack and release on Fuse. Any layering that uses it becomes recognisably ‘Auxy’.

The sound packs and defaults are generally good, but with more design and shaping control, tracks made in Auxy wouldn’t have to sound like ‘Auxy tracks’.

Whenever I hear Hour used in a track, I lose interest almost instantly. It’s symptomatic of why I listen to less and less ‘Auxy music’.

The tool has become too conspicuous in the music, imprinting its sound (limitations) on almost every track made using it.
