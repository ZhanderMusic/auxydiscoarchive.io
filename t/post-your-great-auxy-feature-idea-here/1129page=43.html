Arimyth May 23, 2018, 4:51am #863

Nice ideas. A couple things to add to your suggestions:

  1. There’s a hardware limit to the number of instruments that can be added, and this all depends on the specifications of the device. Maybe the current number hasn’t hit that limit though.
  2. It wouldn’t really be possible to switch between a note / automation editor as Auxy uses a different system than a mobile DAW such as Medly, where automation loops are completely separate and not at all tied to the note loops.
  3. The track-wide pitch is in line with some other suggestions for a track-wide mixing panel

akabillposters May 23, 2018, 8:33am #867

Maybe the solution is to avoid leaving your tracks with generic (randomly generated) titles too long?

As soon as my projects start to take on a certain direction, I’ll give them a more descriptive name.

e.g. !! [GN] dark synth-pop 8 - 116 bpm

([GN] is the artist code. I create under multiple names/genres. !! denotes how close to finished it is [!/!!/!!!]. Handily, it also sorts more developed pieces to the top of the list, when sorting alphanumerically.)

It might not be the final track name yet, but it’s much better for keeping a handle on multiple WIP projects.

It’s not as graceful as a folder and/or tag system, but it’s an easy habit to get into and it gets the job done.
