Reading this just now, and seeing how this has been suggested before. I just want to comment that automation on a master track would be nice. It could help with future bass and trap. Empty areas during a drop adds cool vibes. So far, the only way that that is possible is by going through your scene, duplicating every melody and note, and clearing the bar or area that you want to be empty. This wastes cpu space by adding more objects and makes the whole project bigger to look at needlessly. Adding on to this, it wouldn’t be a bad idea to add more effects, such as a phaser, slow time, speed time, and more reverbs. A cool way to solve the space issue when adding different iterations of the same effect, could be using the third dimension, depth. Using 3D touch on iPhones and using a long press on iPads could open a whole new area to the UI. I know that it would take a whole update to implement these suggestions, but I would love for you to keep these in mind.
