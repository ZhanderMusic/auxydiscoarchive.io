akabillposters September 6, 2018, 10:11am #1446

I think it would be much cooler to integrate some other synths into Auxy instead of sending MIDI to them.

Do you mean like Korg Gadget or Groovebox?
That could work.

One is presumably a much bigger ask than the other – unless you really think you can compete with dedicated, well-funded, corporate-backed synth apps from companies with the highest possible reputations, as well as dedicated innovative new synth app developers.

Relatively minor things (compared to entire user-customisable synth engines) like ADSR for the existing preset-based approach have seemed like big asks, so I wonder how well a dedicated, in-app synth UI might execute.

Auxy’s success is based on having one of the best sequencer UIs in the field.
I’d love to see a quality synth UI in there, but it would need to enter the game at quite a competitive level of quality to be more than a ‘my first synth’ feature.

Imo, if MIDI-Out and in-app Synth UI were both on the list, I’d put MIDI-Out higher on the priority list.
Better to give users access to quality options first (even if they’re 3rd-party), especially when those 3rd-party synth apps have carved a very clear, very desirable sound space for themselves. That’s something that will be take a lot of time to replicate.
