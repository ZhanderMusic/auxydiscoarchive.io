Fix panning

  1. Make hard panning possible

Hard panning doesn’t completely kill the signal in the opposite channel.

  1. Put panning after effects

Currently, reverb and delay come after panning. This causes panned sounds/instruments to be ‘re-centred’ during the reverb/delay because they’re both stereo/‘stereo-ising’ effects.
