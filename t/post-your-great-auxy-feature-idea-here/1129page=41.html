Hey all, not sure if these have been mentioned previously, but having had Auxy for two weeks now there are a few features that I’d love to see, namely:

  • A way to play songs back without the risk of accidentally changing anything - I often listen to and play with Auxy when out and about and the screen often gets tapped in my pocked when I’m playing a song back. Either a way to lock or make the app still play in the background when the app is not running would be great
  • In line with the above, it’d be cool to be able to lock certain loops/scenes once you are happy with them to prevent any accidental changes. Locking individual loops would be great as you could experiment with new loops in the scene without accidentally messing up any ‘permanent’ loops
  • Make the automation menu higher contrast, as it’s nigh on impossible to see the inactive grey options in broad daylight, even with the screen brightness turned right up (I’m referring to the page where you select the automation type, eg: Tone/Delay/Shape etc.)
  • Please please please, implement a way to loop the entire track, not just the current scene. I like to be able to just hear the whole song on repeat when writing
  • The ability to solo multiple tracks simultaneously - I often want to hear two loops in isolation but in order to do so I have to turn off all the other loops as you are only able to hear one loop in solo at a time

Other than that, keep up the good work!

nonetone May 21, 2018, 8:25am #836

Hello all!

I’ve using Auxy for a few years now, mainly in a passive casual way like when traveling or so. Never really paid much attention to new features or packs or so, just used the free content to sketch out quick ideas and play around etc. But now that the app is turning more premium (and I was forced to, or lose my content) I’ve happily subscribed and I’m excited to pay more attention to the new updates etc, and also would like to share a few thoughts for improvement that I have. And yes, there’s a quick summary and the bottom.

Organizing

  • The random name generation is super nice, but I would love if the names were even more random. + is fun for a while, but I have a long list of songs and it gets hard to remember which is which, when the list is full of Crazy Grizzly, Snarky Grizzly, Modest Grizzly, Grumpy Grizzly etc. Maybe some more different attributes could spice it up a little. Here are some suggestions: colors, spices, flavors, smells, feelings, shapes, kitchen utensils, professions etc.

  • My list of songs is pretty long, and I produce many different kind of genres under different pseudonyms. Would be amazing to be able to organize the tracks into folders. Maybe color coding?

  • Would love to see the bpm, scale (and maybe length) of a song without opening it. Some kind of meta information to keep track of what is what.

Production

  • Song templates. I often start by adjusting the tempo and key. I can of course make a track with what I want, and then always duplicate and load that track, but that doesn’t feel so premium…

  • Packs. It’s cool that I can easily delete a whole pack to free up space. But to download a sound I have to tap the download button for each individual preset/sample? When I’m traveling I often don’t have internet access, would love to be able to just download all the sounds available at once. Or at least each entire pack with a tap for each pack.

  • Drawing multiple notes quickly. Okay so here’s the problem. I wanna make those fancy trap hihat rolls, right. I wanna use 1/32 notes and make crazy beats that go TRRRRR-TTTTT-TR-TR-TR-TTTT, right. I have the big iPhone. I have skinny computer nerd fingers. I couldn’t physically have less meat in my hands and fingers without needing immediate medical assistance. STILL - I can’t tap all those nice 1/32 notes in the row without immediately erasing the previous one. I’m sure you know what I’m talking about. One step forward and two steps backward. I wanna be able to turn on the Pencil mode and just swipe my finger down for rapid note creation. And the same for deleting notes would be cool too.

  • Transposing. Pretty much the first things I do when I start a new project is do lay down either a simple bassline or chord progression. I start with one bar, and then while looping it I shift the scale up and down in the project settings, to find something I like. Then I extend my loop to 4 or 8 bars, and move around the notes to recreate it. This process is a bit tedious, especially with chords. Something I think could be cool is to instead of extending my 1 bar loop, duplicating the loop a bunch of times, transpose the loops up and down and then fuse them together to create the final 4/8 bar chord progression loop.

  • Effects. I know it sounds silly, but I just love to slap a trance gate on my chords. An Arpeggiator would also be insanely cool. I believe Portamento/Glide exists for some instruments (?), but its not globally available for all synths.

  • Drum Kits. This is the main reason I decided to subscribe instead of ditching the app completely. And honestly, this is one of the main selling points of the app in the marketplace in general. There are so few apps available where you can easily load your own drum samples and make beats, and afaik none of them are free. Here are a few sub-thoughts about the Drum Kits:

    • Sample names. Each custom imported sample is called “Cloud” in my tracks. Makes it really hard to understand when I’ve created a custom kit out of my personal sample library. Maybe just use the file name, or at least let me label it?

    • Save Drum Kit presets. Lets say I’ve uploaded a classic TR-808 kit to my icloud, imported each sample into the Drum Kit into a layout that I like. Now I have to do that again and again and again for each song I want to add those samples to? Please correct me if I’m wrong! Especially annoying when you are using the same samples often.

  • Simpler Sample player. OK maybe this is a bit too much for a mobile app, but it would be a totally cool feature. Load up a synth stab sample via iCloud or whatever, pick its root key and then play it like a synth. Crank up some delay and reverb and cut some lows out and virtually anything is possible.

Import/Export

  • Export stems with the option to bypass Mastering chain. Pretty simple really, why would you bounce all your stems separately if it’s not so that you can reassemble the track in another DAW, continue working and ultimately do the final mixdown and mastering? For me this is a no-brainer must have.
  • AXP Export - just wondering - which information does it contain? Could I create a converter tool to convert my AXP files to an Ableton project? They are only a few bytes big, and I get more or less everything when exporting stems+midi, except automation curves. But just thinking loud about the potential.
  • Is the AXP I get when exporting the same as the one saved in the Auxy folder in my iCloud?

Misc

  • I nearly always keep my iPhone in Low Power Mode, this returns a popup about performance throttling in Auxy. What is exactly happening there? I don’t notice any performance difference, could you elaborate on what this actually means?

  • I use Auxy on both an iPhone and iPad - the songs are stored in my icloud, so the song data synchronizes fine between the devices. However I have to re-download each separate sound on both devices. If my library along with my settings, presets and templates would sync through iCloud I would be a more efficient producer.

Sounds/Sound pack wishlist

  • A thick supersaw lead
  • A thick reese bass (basically detuned supersaw)
  • A squeaky acid 303
  • A robust and distorted 303
  • Human sloppy claps and foaley samples
  • DONK
  • Cheesy Vintage FM synth sounds, like Yamaha DX7 or a SEGA Mega Drive
  • Ambient, atmospherical stuff
  • Orchestral stabs and hits, both modern Future Bass style but also old school Rave style
  • For now there’s just the Sweeper. Something to make risers and anticipation effects would be cool.
  • A psytrance pack
  • An ethno pack with pan flutes and other woodwind instruments

TL;DR Quick Resume

  • More random in the name generator

  • Organize songs in folders, color codes

  • See BPM, length and meta info per song in overview

  • Song templates

  • Download entire pack at once

  • Drawing multiple notes at once

  • Transposing entire loops or sections, merging loops

  • More instrument effects, trance gate, glide?

  • Drum kit rename samples

  • Drum kit save presets

  • Simpler/Sample player instrument

  • Export audio stems with mastering chain bypassed

  • Cross device sync
