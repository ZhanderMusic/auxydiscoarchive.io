Auxy August 20, 2017, 7:04am #1695

I should mention that if you want to give feedback, we prefer if you tell us how you feel limited in your music creation or whether there’s a lot of friction using the app. That’s what we’re interested in improving. To give an example, comments like “I have a lot of projects and usually have to open a bunch of them before I find the one that I’m looking for” are usually more helpful than “you need to add folders for projects”.

One thing that I’ve learned from working with software development for a number of years is that users will rarely come up with a feature that the developers/designers haven’t already thought of. The challenge is rarely to come up with things that can be added. The challenge is to figure out what should be done next and how it should it be done to make the product better. We know that there are lots of things in Auxy that could be improved, but we’re doing our best to invest in the things that will bring the most value and make sure we do it in a way that will keep things smooth and creative. So that you can make more and better music.

So keep the feedback coming but remember that it’s not as easy as adding another button in the app.
