akabillposters March 14, 2018, 6:52am #496
Produk:

Delay on drums: I can see where it can be applied but I don’t think it’s vital.

Of course, we’re all likely to have subjective views on what’s ‘vital’, but objectively speaking…

Is it any more or less vital than, say, the ducker (which duplicates an existing effect - volume automation - for convenience)?
Any less vital than being able to tune the kick drum, to work in a preset major or minor scale or to know how long your track is without needing to render it first? Any less vital than sample imports? A feature so vital it wasn’t introduced until the fifth version of the app.

‘Vital’ is for V1. We’re supposedly four versions past ‘vital’ now, and yet…

Minimalism isn’t just about UI. It’s also about simplifying how thing’s work. By going out of their way to force users to use different methods to achieve the same effect on different instruments, well, I wouldn’t call that good design.
As the net impact is to actively and purposely discourage the use of a legitimate and popular effect, I wouldn’t call it good policy either.
