Fredrik August 11, 2018, 7:57am #57

Good feedback! We launched with perhaps the highest possible sampling quality (Nicklas Flyckt is one of the best) which is a bar we know we just can’t beat every month.

That being said, I think regarding this pack you were also expecting more of a jazzy solo pack. These instruments are more orchestral and most sounds are brass sections (not solo recordings). This makes them great for backgrounds, movie scores, ambient, chill etc. but not ideal for jazz.

What label a pack is released under is more of a contract thing and not really worth reading much into :). We still collaborate with sample providers and artists for the packs.

All: We’re in the process of planning the next couple of months, so I’ll be on the lookout for pack ideas!

(Please use the forum instead of PM’ing me! )
