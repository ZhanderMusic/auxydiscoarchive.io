Dadmother March 1, 2021, 7:43pm #24

Hokay, so:

I’ve been interested in and actively tinkering with music since I was VERY young. When I was 3 or 4 (1994-1995), my mom had gotten me little children’s keyboards and whatnot, and I used to play along to my favorite wrestling entrance songs (I was a huge WCW and WWF fan). For years afterwards, I tinkered with pianos when we went to church. On my 7th birthday (1998), my mom surprised me with a 1986 Casio CTK-50 keyboard, and from then on I’ve been truly hooked on music.

I’ve been making music since my middle school years (started around 2003), recording all kinds of absolute garbage in Audacity, began playing in bands in 2009, and even going so far as to record full tapes playing into an old dictaphone (unfortunately, those tapes have been lost to time). Started using Auxy in early 2016, and began releasing music as Dadmother in early 2017.
