Featured Auxy Artist #07: @TC64

Screen Shot 2018-02-08 at 8-Feb 9.09.29 PM.jpg806×255 17.1 KB

ToxicCaves64 (a.k.a T.C Music) produces Chiptunes, synth funk, and other sweet sounds with Auxy and Ableton. During October 2017 (“Auxtober”) he released a track each day. If you haven’t heard them, then you’re in for a treat - check it out. And if that’s not enough for you, look what he can make with only five Auxy instruments. TC64 got talent.

Week starting: 12 Feb, 2018.

  • Comment in this thread once you’ve provided any feedback.

Check out their tracks here: https://soundcloud.com/toxiccaves64/tracks
