Like it, even though it’s not a genre I typically enjoy listening to (at least, not since my clubbing days).

The main vibe is a funky tech-house, but that arpeggio is played straight.
Can’t help thinking that you try out a little syncopation to that arpeggio.

e.g. Push the opening G back to the 16th step of the bar (leaving the 1st step empty). This will create a funky little impulse that carries the music over into the following bar. Keeps it quite pure, just with a little lick.

Anyhoo, just thinking out loud.
