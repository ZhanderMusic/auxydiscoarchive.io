Artichoke July 13, 2017, 12:47am #258

That’s pretty good stuff! Hard to believe you’re just a noob, when I made my first songs they were absolute trash. However, I did notice some things you might want to consider moving forward—one thing I’ve always found immeasurably useful is lowering the hats and cymbals in the drum kits to the point that I can just barely hear them after getting more sounds laid down. The kick, too—try experimenting with different drum patterns as you go along to see which kicks are the least intrusive yet still thumpy to the particular style of music you’d work on.
