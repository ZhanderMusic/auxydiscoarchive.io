Safest way to archive an Auxy track?

Production Talk
corey September 24, 2018, 7:27pm #1

I finished a song. It includes Auxy samples and synths, but also custom samples loaded from my iCloud.

I don’t want to keep it on my iPad forever. What’s the best way to archive it so I could reload it in the future, without risk of missing samples or sounds?

iammane September 24, 2018, 7:29pm #2

I do this all the time. Well I keep it in iCloud but you don’t have to.

Make a subfolder off the Auxy/Samples folder with the song title or something that makes sense. Fix your drum tracks with the samples to point to this folder. Open up iCloud.com on a PC or you can even use the Files app on iOS to copy the folder with the samples and the Auxy project file somewhere else.

A little hoop jumpy, but at least you can do it all on your device
