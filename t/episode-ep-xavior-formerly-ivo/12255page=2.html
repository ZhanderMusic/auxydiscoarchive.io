xavior September 24, 2018, 5:39pm #24

Thank you so much for this awesome feedback. I’m so glad you enjoyed the music. It means a lot. I really wanted to prove how far I could take things with Auxy. I invested so much time and effort into every track and I just hope that shows.

And as for the cover art? Yes, that is an actual portrait that I took from a photo series that I was working on a few months ago. The gold is real (I doused my model is shiny gold acrylic paint, which was fun at first… until we had to clean her off) and the editing is kept as minimal as possible. I just desaturated the rest of the image, added some grain to make it more gritty and analogue-seeming, and mirrored the image to make it as symmetrical as possible. The shoot yielded amazing results and I just knew I had to use on the pieces for my next cover art.

Thank you again for the nice commentary! Really appreciate it.
