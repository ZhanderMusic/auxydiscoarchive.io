Today, I present to you the first ever…

Auxy Podcast!

Introducing Auxcasts,

a series of interviews, contests, news flashes, debates, and more! Come along and listen to artists such as Hexx, Mr. Serpent, HAYVN, Polar, and HELIXX, along with (hopefully) a couple guest stars, have a serious discussion about what snack food they like to eat while doing auxy.
Our first podcast has been brought to you by Hexx and Mr. Serpent, and goes through a basic rundown plus a short cross-interview QnA. Links to both the interview and the profile itself are posted below!

We’d really appreciate it if you could show some support by following Auxcasts and giving our podcast a like or repost.

And just so everyone knows, neither Hexx nor I know how to run one of these things, so you’'ll hear a lot of unprofessionalism throughout the entire podcast. I do apologize for the -50% quality of the audio and constant breaks and pauses due to Discord being a total bloombugger.

Anyways, that’s just about it! All aboard the hype train 'cause this is about to GET REAL!!
