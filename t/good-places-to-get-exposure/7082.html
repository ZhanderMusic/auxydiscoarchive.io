I’d possibly start by looking at where the stuff that’s getting exposure on the promo channels is coming from.

See which distributors, if any, have a connection to those channels. Track back some artists who have been shown (and who have a similar sound to you) to their own channels and see what’s being talked about there. See what networks and communities they’re involved with.

There’s likely more discussion about (e.g.) ‘how to get stuff on Majestic Casual’ around the web, and some established connections and pathways might be mentioned.

As I mentioned elsewhere, take the time to understand each channel’s playlist and submit each track to the most appropriate channel(s). Getting it wrong early could get you systematically ignored later.
