sn.ow.y August 17, 2018, 10:26pm #290

(I guess I’m kind of late to this thread…)

Ok so long story. In fourth grade, I started to really like snow leopards (the animal) and yes, I still love them. So, soon afterwards, I got minecraft and made my username Snowleopard01. Then, I was playing online with some friends and they got tired of typing the whole thing, so they just typed “snowy” instead. Then, I got some other games, like Clash of Clans, and started getting games on Steam and my Xbox. I used “snowy” or “Snowleopard” as some part of my username. For example, (I have three CoC accounts) my usernames are “SnowyWarrior01, SnowyWarrior02, and SnowyWarrior03”. The name stuck with me for almost 6 years now. So, when I made my SoundCloud account, I was thinking of a good way to make a logo, and I thought that if I split “snowy” up with periods, then I could make my logo the first two letters and the period. Hence, my logo just says “sn.”. I then just continued splitting it, so then it became “sn.ow.y”.

If you’re still reading this, I’d be amazed. Thanks for tuning in to this week’s episode of Horribly Long Backstories!
