Cool track.

The chords at the beginning don’t fit the bassline at all, so you might wanna change them. The snare is too bouncy and “happy” sounding for the feel of the song, maybe make it harder if you want it to sound better. It changes up a lot overall, but still ends up being pretty repetitive (same elements overused throughout the whole song). Cool concept, though. Let me know if you ever do anything with it
