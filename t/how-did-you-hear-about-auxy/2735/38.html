I searched for like an hour just looking through music making apps. Waaaay before I ever even knew anything about music production. I downloaded Auxy, I don’t remember why. And I didn’t like it because everything I put down sounded like trash. I would put several notes in random order at random pitches. I was retarded now that I look back . I would put kicks together in a line and snares on weird beats. I deleted the app because I had an iPad mini with 16gb and no space. And forgot about it. Once I got an iPad Air, I decided to look through the music apps because I was bored again. Downloaded GarageBand and watched a lot of videos trying to learn it. Succeeded, remade a bunch of popular songs with the help of youtube videos. Got Auxy after looking for something more streamlined. Now it was Auxy Studio though. Looked completely different but I immediately recognized it as the same devs after looking at the design choices. Stuck with it ever since. I’ve only been a member for half a year but now I prefer it over my studio, only because it is so mobile, and I have such a hard time transferring my ideas from Auxy to Logic. I don’t own many plugins so maybe thats why too.
