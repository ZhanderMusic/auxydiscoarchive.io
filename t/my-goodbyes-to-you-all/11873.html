My Goodbyes To You All

Random
Xiejra August 24, 2018, 7:53pm #1

So This has been a mixed year. I first found Auxy in July of 2017, I had always wantes to create music but could never afford the software.

I remember this was when I was in Canada with some friends when I found the app and have been writing tracks on it ever since.

As some of you may know I have had bad times throughout the year (as most teenagers do it seems) music has been the perfect escape for me and to that I thank the developers for creating this.

Now i’m not going to pull a DJ_La_Rocca and write the entire bible as a goodbye so i’m going to keep it short as to why: This past year has been the perfect start to my music career but now I must say goodbye to the app and it’s community. All of you are so talented and I really wish everyone the best in your future of music.

I would like to thank: @xavior @HELIXX @anon86704580 @Matik @DJ_La_Rocca @Produk @blackocean @MrSerpent @Phluze @MR_ANDERSON You have all shown so much support to me and I have really enjoyed working alongside all of you. I wish you the best

Now by no means does this mean that I am quitting music, as DJ La Rocca put it “Auxy was limiting” or something along those lines. That is why I have moved towards Ableton which I am very privileged to have, and I have already started writing tracks on it.

As my final goodbye I have released my “8 month in the making album”, Limitations And A Bad Case Of Perfectionism (Listen here)
https://soundcloud.com/xiejramusic/sets/gtil729x76vx

So then I guess that’s it from me. Thank you everyone and stay tuned!

~ Elliott Martin (AKA Xiejra)
