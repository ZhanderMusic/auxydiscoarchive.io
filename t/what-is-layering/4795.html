MisterMaster December 3, 2017, 10:28pm #2

Layering is just playing sounds at the same time.

A common approach is play the same thing on 2+ instruments at once. This is easy to do on Auxy, as you can simply duplicate an entire instrument (including notes / automation) and then change the instrument to something else.
