RazerFireRiot September 28, 2017, 11:31pm #9

My AP pack will not be focused on a certain type of sound, in fact, a variety of sounds and synths!
I’d would most likely call it “Ajacent Wonders”

Well… mainly effects

Effects:
“Skyline”: A sound effect based on the night time sounds from the birds eye view of New York, like that wind, traffic, police sirens, try imagining it to understand what I mean.

“Crowd”: An audience sound effect! How does that sound? Give the listener and fans the feeling like you’re performing at a concert in your own little music production room.

“Software”: Basically using all the sounds in iOS in one “drum kit”.

“Blast”: An airhorn, basically.

Drum kit:

“Bit”: An 8 bit based drum kit, bringing the retro to the future of music making.

Synths:
“Float”: That calming yet amazing synth that you would hear in a lot of Future Bass songs, for example, Virtual Riot - Stay for a While.

“Chips”: That square synth we all heard back in the day, ahh the golden age of games… Now imagine using it in your own Auxy track!

Bass:
“Ripple”: A bass that was meant to be with the innovative Automation, meant to be used for your wubby wubs you’ve imagined.
