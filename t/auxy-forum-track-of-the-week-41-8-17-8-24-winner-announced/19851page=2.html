Yup so the point of this rule was that people wouldn’t bring in entire melodic loops, like a four bar loop, or a two or four bar drum loop that they snagged from like loopmasters, or an app like blocs wave, etc. That’s why judges reserve the right to see the project file if there’s any doubt.

If you take a drum loop, cut it up into pieces and re-arrange it, that’s cool. I’m also a big proponent of taking melodic loops, cutting those up, and re-arranging the pieces. It’s a worthwhile exercise and usually sounds TOTALLY different
