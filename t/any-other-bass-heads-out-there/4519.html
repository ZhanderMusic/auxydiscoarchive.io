For my own ears, I use my Turtle Beach gaming headset. Not sure what type it is but it’s like $100, and I was able to get it at half off. FYI, if you ever need to know this (it’s pretty handy in my life), a quick way to repair most broken earbuds is to take pliers and squeeze the jack really hard. It’s sounds stupid but actually works sometimes. Only do it if it’s your last option, though. Not sure if it can Malen it worse.
