ben May 7, 2020, 9:10pm #6

I’d suggest looking at tutorials for other platforms or for the main computer DAWs for inspiration, as many of the techniques used in those can be applied to Auxy as well. I’d suggest going more towards the direction of song structuring tutorials, rather than tutorials focusing more on sound designing capabilities that wouldn’t necessarily be possible in Auxy yet.
Feel free to come to your fellow Auxy users for advice as well. I, for one, benefitted from this greatly back when I was first starting to delve into the realm of using Ableton. I’m sure your peers around you in this community as well as on the respective Auxy Discord servers would be willing to give you some more personalized advice (in my opinion, Auxy Collective is more active and engaging than the Disco at times, which could be of some help to you).
