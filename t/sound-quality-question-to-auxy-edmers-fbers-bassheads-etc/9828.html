akabillposters May 10, 2018, 4:17pm #14

I’m not sure that’s entirely true.
Auxy itself does quite a fair job of ‘mastering’.

It’s just that it’s all pointing to a single, ‘ideal’* dynamic profile.
(* according to the ideals of the dev team and their contributors)

Similarly, automated mastering is getting smarter all the time, and is, imo, already reaching ‘good enough’ levels of quality. (I’d say the days of human audio engineering are numbered, or at least, the numbers working in that industry will likely fall significantly over the next decade.)

Smart, AI-based automated mastering does indeed profile tracks individually and make adjustments accordingly, but they also tend to offer a range of profiles.

e.g. BandLab’s options are CD, Clarity or Bass Enhance, iirc.

Now, I’m not expecting the Auxy team to somehow create their own AI-based automated mastering into a £5/month app**, but perhaps a few other mastering profile presets would be possible.

A lot of this might be possible if we had per-instrument/kit compression, etc… controls.
(It comes back to that recurring request for a layer of ‘power’ tools for power-users.)

** Who knows, maybe it would be possible using API/hand-off to offer Auxy users the option to run their tracks through smarter, 3rd-party auto-mastering as part of the render/output process. Or maybe just offering a 3rd-party automated mastering site as an Export destination.
