Forum Themes.
Make your Auxy Disco forum look more colourful for a while, by changing your theme. We’ve added a couple of new themes to the list.

Choose a theme under:
USER PIC (Top right) > USER PREFERENCES > INTERFACE > THEME
If the new options don’t appear, refresh your page / restart the app.

Three new themes include:

1 - Forest:

Forest.jpg851×355 28.1 KB

2 - Fire Truck:

Fire Truck.jpg850×353 29.4 KB

3 - Neon Pinky:

Neon Pinky.jpg849×354 33.4 KB

We tested these successfully on phone browser, phone discourse app and on desktop, but let us know if you experience any problems. The theme tool doesn’t allow us to change every setting eg: there’s no built-in option to change the grey colours without scripting. Thanks to @iammane & @NotMiles for testing.

A small, but fun change. Check it out.
