We all know the giant megacollabs we love doing, and we all know that some folks work well together. With that in mind, I’ve got a strange idea I’ve wanted to try out for some time:

An Auxy Electro/House supergroup (think Swedish House Mafia but… not SHM) consisting of at least three people and at most five or six, with the main project being a potential album or a simple EP. This is different from the megacollabs as it is one large project with a small number of people compared to one track with a large number of people.

I’m currently taking applications, and the applicants must meet the following credentials:

-Must have Auxy 6.
-Must have an Auxy Pro subscription. (My apologies to those who don’t, this rule is just for a wider variety of sounds!)
-Must know how Electro and House music works and how to produce it, as well as possible subgenres such as Melbourne Bounce, Progressive House and Progressive Electro (or Progresso!)
-Must be willing to fully cooperate in this project. Goes without saying, you can’t just agree and then not do anything for it. (Even a freakin’ arp pluck would work, at least you’d be adding something.)

I would also like a demonstration of what you have done with Electro or House music. This way, I can evaluate if you’d actually fit in the project.

Deadline for applicants is 4/20 (nice), that’s the 20th of April, so that is one week from today. I will set up a Discord server in the meantime and invite the chosen members on that day.

Good luck to everyone, I’m hoping this turns out successfully!
