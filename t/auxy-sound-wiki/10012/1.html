Hello and welcome to the Auxy sound wiki, a quick easy-to-use reference guide for the sounds, their controls, and prominent tracks that utilize the sounds. This will (in theory) be open to anyone to edit (if this wiki thing works). Thanks to MisterMaster for giving me permission to create this for the improvement of the community. Please feel free to edit any details, especially adding in styles that the sounds best fit into, as well as tracks that make excellent use of a given sound.

This wiki will be divided into three sections, each with a collapsible interface that allows for easy reference of sounds. Unfortunately there is no easy/feasible way to indent through Discourse, so please bear the formatting quirks. It’s also going to be a bit of jargon too, so bear with any unfamiliar terminology or confusing descriptions.

Any unique behavior of macros such as delay and reverb will be noted, otherwise most instruments act very similar with these macros and functionality is (mostly) the same.

A NOTE ON ADDING TRACKS IN - Please do not add any tracks in that only have a tiny bit of the sound in question, or are just your own songs and you’re trying to promote yourself. The purpose of this is to show outstanding usage of a given instrument. An example of this would be the use of Beam found in Crimson by Blackocean, or the use of Bow found in Coup by Phluze. Priority should be also given to any track featured in the feed, to give more examples as to how the sounds were used. This is not for promotion. Be on an honor system when it comes to these things. If there’s any issue, it will be taken up with the moderators.

IN ORDER TO MAKE SURE THIS ISN’T JUST BEING USED FOR PROMOTION, DO NOT ADD YOUR OWN SONGS IN AS EXAMPLES. SOMEONE ELSE MUST RECOMMEND/EDIT THEM IN

Thank you for following the above rule.

IF ANYONE CHANGES ANYTHING ABOVE THE DOUBLE LINE OTHER THAN MODERATORS OR ME (TheRealJFalc) IT WILL BE REMOVED. I LIKE MY WORDING AS IT IS THANK YOU VERY MUCH

Beam
Label Description
Pack (AP-009) Destiny
Waveform Cross between a rounded triangle mixed with a cross-breed distorted sine and square wave. It also features a slight pitch bend on the attack of the note, making a sharp hit.
Details A very heavy bass from 7 Skies, this one can be awkward to use, but is great for very heavy sounding beats and drops.
Macro: Tone Adds clipping distortion to the waveform
Macro: Shape Increases the attack (A) of the waveform by a small factor. This seems to play a use in softening the attack of the note achieved by the pitch bend.
Styles useful Heavy baselines, such as trap and dubstep.
Featured Tracks Blackocean - Crimson
Bee
Label Description
Pack OG Sounds
Waveform Saw with attack, high resonance and clipping distortion.
Details One of the original sounds, bee is a solid saw-based bass that has some interesting features attached to shape and tone, specifically with how the resonance functions.
Macro: Tone Increases the resonance of the waveform, creating a vox-like effect
Macro: Shape Adjusts the Sustain (S) of the attack. No shape removes most of the attack-like effect from the instrument, while full gives the full impact of the attack and sustains it out.
Macro: Lowpass Controls the amount of resonance as well as the lowpass filter on the waveform. Having it at the lowest values removes most of the resonance from the instrument
Styles useful House, Trance

Blob
Label Description
Pack OG Sounds
Waveform Sine and a distorted sine wave that functions on an ADSR envelope that controls a bitcrush distortion on the waveform. Has a slight chorus effect as well.
Details One of the weirdest sounds of the OG pack, Blob is hard to use at times, but when used effectively can create a very heavy sound. It’s unwieldy but can be used very effectively in certain circumstances.
Macro: Tone Enables the ADSR envelope that controls the distorted sine waveform. This modulates the shape of the wave. No tone does not modulate the distorted sine waveform, full tone cycles through the entirety of the waveform
Macro: Shape Adjusts the attack (A) of the ADSR envelope controlling the distorted sine waveform. This means that the cycle is slower rising up at full volume, creating a different effect
Styles useful Heavy sounds, dubstep

Slope
Label Description
Pack (AP-003) 7 Skies Selection
Waveform Synthesized bass guitar sound.
Details One of the first guitar-like sounds added to Auxy, Slope can be used as a pluck instrument (with low shape) or a slight wobble bass (with high shape). This is because the filter is attached to an ADSR envelope controlled by the shape macro. Due to this versatility, it is a very ergonomic sound and fits well in many different genres.
Macro: Tone Adds a sine distortion to the waveform, giving it a wet sort of feel.
Macro: Shape Increases the decay and sustain (D&S) of the waveform as well as the filter’s decay and sustain.
Styles useful Heavy basslines, such as trap, or dubstep (with shape)
Featured Tracks aUstin Haga - Float, Mr. Serpent - Hypnotic Paralysis

Bow
Label Description
Pack OG Sounds
Waveform Saw wave with resonance and pitch bend controlled by a LFO
Details One of the OG sounds, Bow is a very useful sound when used in the right way. Without any shape, it can function as a pluck, and with full shape it becomes a strong string-like synth with a slight pitch vibrato. Some people struggle to use it, but when used effectively it creates a very solid sound that grabs the attention of the listener.
Macro: Tone Adds a phaser to the instrument
Macro: Shape Adjusts the Attack, decay and sustain of the waveform (ADS). No shape creates a pluck effect, while full shape adds full sustain to the instrument and creates a very strong lead sound.
Styles useful All-around sound when used properly
Featured Tracks Coup by Phluze, Kleuren by Conux

Label Description
Pack Game Waves
Waveform Altered unison saw wave with short release and bitcrush distortion
Details As one of the Game Waves sounds, Bitty is a strong saw wave that serves as one of the strongest saw-based leads. Its simple waveform allows it to be layered well with other sounds.
Macro: Tone Adds an LFO to the waveform that controls the pitch, creating a very strong vibrato-like effect
Macro: Shape Adds attack to the ADSR envelope controlling the waveform and lowers the power of the bitcrush distortion on the waveform.
Styles useful General usage, retro

Label Description
Pack OG Sounds
Waveform Sine x Pulse waveform with low sustain, an envelope controlling the filter as well as an LFO controlling the rate of the pulse wave.
Details Bumble is a weird lead that lends itself best to heavier types of sound. At its core, it is a sine wave and pulse wave driven sound, but its main functionality works with how it is ranged in the lower octaves, which sets it apart from the other leads. The filter is overdriven, creating a strong sound that resonates through the lower octaves, making it a great choice for heavy-styled songs.
Macro: Tone Adds a strong clip-based distortion to the waveform
Macro: Shape Adds release to the ADSR envelope controlling the waveform.
