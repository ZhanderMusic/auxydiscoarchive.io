crystldawnmusic July 31, 2018, 2:26pm #2

It’s fairly straightforward. Download amuse, then make sure you have your music (as .wavs) and your artwork (high-quality image, try 1700x1700) on something like Google Drive, Dropbox or iCloud drive. From there, make an account on the app (for free), select New Release and follow the prompts.

Hope this helped!

Maland2016 July 31, 2018, 6:53pm #4

So when you render the songs, tap export, but DON’T click Audio. Click the “More Options” button or whatever it’s called, I don’t remember, but it’s the last one of the four that show up.

When you hit that, press “Uncompressed WAV”. That will render the file as just that, an uncompressed wav. From there, you can save it to your Drive or Dropbox, etc, etc.

Hopefully this helps!
