Matik May 31, 2018, 2:08pm #14

I know stress is more commonly used as an 8-bit lead and arp, however I like to think of both gossip and stress being useful in terms of being either a riser or an 8-bit arp.

I guess it’s just about preference, but if I were to use both in a track, I’d probably make gossip the arp and stress the lead because

  1. Stress can sound slightly overdrived when placed together in a small area once the tempo is fast enough and
  2. Gossip sounds much cleaner in my opinion compared to Stress.

Again, it does depend on preference
I think I’ve heard anechoik too many times to believe that stress is perfect as a riser more than an 8-bit lead/arp
