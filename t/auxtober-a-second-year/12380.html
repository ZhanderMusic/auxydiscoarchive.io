RazerFireRiot September 30, 2018, 9:53pm #1

For you all (y’all ) Auxicians who where here since the birth of the Auxy Party aka Disco (or at least a month or two after the party got started), you might’ve remembered my idea of Auxtober (Auxy + October), where you’d create a track, preferably Spoopy themed, make it within a day and post it the same day and we’d all check out what you’ve made!

Auxtober 2017

I was thinking this year we have a specific challenge each week, like a certain instrument, chord, or whatever, or to spice things up, make it a different challenge every day .

Unfortunately, my good trusty iPhone broke and I had to switch to a weak 32-bit iPad, using FL Studio Mobile to make songs (zero progress has been made ), but I have the ancient version of Auxy (Blue logo, white A) and this could mean a nostalgic bunch of possibilities for making Auxtober tracks (I miss using Hour, Silk and Automation ). (Nothing wrong with the original pack and no automation, I can do it!)

But rest aside, what should we do this year to mark this unofficial open Auxy event?
