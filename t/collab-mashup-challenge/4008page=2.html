Just to clarify on this, for example, if you lose the drums from a loop because they won’t fit with your track, then that’s fine. And obviously the key will probably have to change to fit your own track. You can change the synths to fit, you might even need to make a small adjustment to the rhythm of a loop for it to work in your track. All of that’s fine.

What wouldn’t be fine is completely rewriting a loop so that the original author cannot identify it in your new song.
