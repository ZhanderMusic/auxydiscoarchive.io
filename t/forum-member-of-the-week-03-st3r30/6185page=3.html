I’ve listened to a few of your (@ST3R30) songs and I enjoyed them for the most part. So…

PROS

  • The chords in your songs are really unique (most of the time *cough * *cough * solstice *cough * *cough *) and really make the song better.
  • In my opinion, you’re transitions between buildups and drops really flow!
  • Speaking of drops, those are pretty good too!
  • Also your logos are awesome!

CONS

  • Your songs do get a bit repetitive.
  • In my opinion, you could definitely work on your percussion.
  • There’s some places where you differentiate one part from another by adding another sound, but in those places it feels like you didn’t add enough.

So overall, your songs are really aren’t really my style, but I would definitely listen to some of them again.
