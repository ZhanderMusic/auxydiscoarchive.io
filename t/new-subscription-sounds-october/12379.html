Comparing some of the sizes of the Destiny pack, it seems some of the sounds were direct ports via samples (due to size) while some were not, as they are significantly smaller. Seeming to indicate that it was not entirely a complex process.

Compare that to The Future pack, where some instruments are somewhere around the size of 15mb in total size (coughgrizzlycough), I’m theorizing it was a complex process as it could have taken more samples in order to reach that size.

That’s all just conjecture though. It’s likely the higher quality and more plentiful the samples are, the more evident it’s shown in the instrument (The Pianos vs The Horns/Strings). That also means it’s a longer process to fine-tune and adjust them.
