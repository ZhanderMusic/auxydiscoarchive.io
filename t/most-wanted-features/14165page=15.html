EVERANGE October 27, 2019, 4:24pm #294

An EQ eight is a feature that is found in more advanced music production softwares. It is basically like a high pass, low pass, and “middle pass” all in one. It lets you edit what part of the sound (low, middle, or high) you want to emphasize. For bass you would more bass, for lead you would add more middle and high, for singers you would add high, and for chords you would add mostly middle. In this way, you can have each sound the best it can without conflicting with others and highlight some sounds more than others of you want. It’s a very useful tool.
