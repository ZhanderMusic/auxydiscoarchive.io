The way I do it is

  1. Make a new drum track

  2. Insert a drum sample

  3. Make the drum sample trigger the ducker. (Make sure the ducker is set to “trigger by samples” and no other drum samples trigger the ducker)

  4. Silence it, either by turning down the volume for the drum sample or silencing the drum kit by automation.

  5. So what can I say except, your welcome! For the silenced sidechain you’ve got!
