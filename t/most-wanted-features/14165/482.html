ThoughtfulNoise October 23, 2020, 6:21pm #482

@Auxy has stated that there won’t be any major feature updates to Auxy, but I’m hoping that means no huge updates to the in-app creation functions and workflow.

I’m hoping this still leaves it open to workflow upgrades.

There are plenty of QoL tweaks that could/should(?) be included, but I think the following list could form the backbone of a reasonable, worthwhile and valuable ‘Pro’ enhancements roadmap while limiting serious heavy lifting and without requiring any ground-up rebuilds within the app.

It would be great if @Auxy could respond to this, and give some thoughts.

 
Workflow

  • Add support for selecting scene sequences/sections — to allow users to loop/review sequences/sections, rather than just single scenes.
    Loop function stays largely the same, but loops whichever scenes are selected, which can be 1 (as current), a sequence of scenes, all the way up to the entire track (If all scenes selected)
  • Increase 8-bar pattern limit for 32
  • Search support in Projects, Instruments, and Samples including iCloud
  • Stretch goal?: Support for shortening bars or support for setting step length of 1-bar patterns — or perhaps just quarter-bar, half-bar, three-quarter-bar, rather than full 12ths/16ths). This would offer a workaround for creating alt time signatures such as 5/4, 4/3, etc. by bolting full and part bars together. (1-bar scenes could also support setting step or beats length, or would be limited to length of shortest active pattern)

Mixing, mastering, export

  • Master volume/gain, maybe even with a meter? (yes, Auxy exports are sometimes a little hot and have clipping, or muddy clipping in the low end. Regardless, being able specify our preferred headroom would be a relatively easy ‘Pro’ feature to add). If added, please make this available directly in the composition UI, not the Export controls.
  • In-app auto-Mastering on/off toggle in Export (default to On). External mastering is becoming increasingly popular amongst experienced Auxy users.
  • Instrument-level Mute and Solo controls (which is also reflected when exporting tracks and stems).
    Would be more flexible/powerful than the current Solo approach, and would also make exporting single/selected stems simple (as muted Instruments would/should be ignored when exporting track/stems).
    Including ability to Solo multiple instruments together as in DAWs.
    Swipe from left edge in Patterns/Loops screen to reveal column with instrument Solo/Mute controls?
  • Stretch: Option to Export ‘Drum Kits’ as multiple stems

Sounds (the 2nd and 3rd ones could blow the app wide open)

  • Finish out the ADSR, or at least Decay. Automating decay is a very popular effect, in the targeted genres and beyond
  • Finer instrument tuning control w/ automation (for detuning). This could be a +/- Tuning control, like with samples, but ideally this would be more granular sub-semitone control in tuning automation — or maybe a ‘Free mode’ in Pitch automation with all snapping disabled.
  • Give users access to the synth tool used to create Auxy compatible sound packs. If there’s demand, you can fork it and pretty it up later (dedicated users will suffer through a bad synth UI for the upside)
  • Add support for importing sound packs (created using aforementioned synth tool)
    (This addresses the requests for an integrated synth UI directly in the app via a much more practical/achievable route. It could open up new additional revenue stream for Auxy, but also creators, if you set up an official marketplace through which user-created sound packs can be sold/downloaded.
    Additionally, the ability to create and use your own and 3rd-party sound packs could be a significant differentiator for Auxy amongst its direct competitors. And not least, this new import function could easily justify a new higher Subscription tier.)
