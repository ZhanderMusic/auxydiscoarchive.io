Auxy February 24, 2021, 10:10am #533

The reason we don’t expose decay and sustain is that they are already mapped to a macro control (shape) in a lot of sounds. But maybe we can sort that out. The goal of shape, though, is to just give people a control for changing the length of the sound, as opposed to having to understand how an ADSR envelope works.

And if we default sustain to near 0, it will be impossible, by definition, to have sounds with long sustain. Our decay time is capped at 5 secs.
