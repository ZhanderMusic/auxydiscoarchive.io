ThoughtfulNoise July 22, 2022, 7:38am #875

They’ll only be stuck without access to many of the more desirable sounds.

And, what if other free version users would rather have other specific sounds brought into the free version?

If the purpose was to allow everyone to create exactly the music they wanted for free, Auxy wouldn’t really have a functioning business. And make no mistake, it is a business.

Auxy will be trying to hit the sweet spot that there’s enough in the free version to engage with, and enough additional value promised in the subbed version to entice users to subscribe.

If $5/month is too much, then perhaps Auxy shouldn’t be a priority or concern for now. Or, if you’re serious enough about making music, find a (legal) way to afford the subs. (Keep in mind you can pay monthly, so you can opt in/out as you want. Obvs, there are savings if you go annual.)
