ThoughtfulNoise August 10, 2020, 5:34pm #455

Indeed. Gaz can sometimes come across as slightly daft and awkward in his videos, but I think he nails it when he talks about the next horizon being pan-directional music creation - i.e. starting on mobile, seamlessly shifting the project to desktop, seamlessly flipping it back to mobile/tablet, etc…

Whoever nails that will have a very compelling ecosystem to offer.

Currently, once you shift from mobile/tablet to desktop, the door tends to lock shut behind you.

 
Interesting that Lenberg was slightly out on his own with seeing mobile as the only space worth paying attention to. And, even then he was more focussed on mobile phone being the one platform that matters.

While it’s impressive what can be done on mobile phone, the screensize limitations force a compromise on workflow power and flexibility - at least for ‘all-in-one’, groovebox-type mobile phone apps.

Tablet is more likely to be the sweet spot for power + flexibility + mobility music ideation and production - though mobile phone + external controllers/surfaces could be a contender.

 
One point that was touched upon in the panel was that seamless project syncing across supported will be table stakes going fwd - if it isn’t already.
