A VERY Bad Case Of The Perfectionisms

Production Talk
Mr_Mooo February 19, 2018, 6:36am #1

Hi all.
I have a BAD case of the perfectionisms. At the moment in Auxy I have about 4 finished songs, for most people my standard GOOD ENOUGH TO RELEASE but not for me. I have been working on Tides, Febrero, Claustrophobia and No.1 (it has no name yet) for over a month at least. One of them for 3 months. I always think to myself “I SHOULD GET THESE PUBLISHED MR MOOO YOU IDIOT JUST PUBLISH THESE and you music is trash” but I never do. I guess I’m worried that when I upload it I’ll get 50 comments saying ‘oh this is so bad you could’ve improved this’ and ‘meh it’s ok it would be so much better if you improved this’.

That’s just the finished songs.

I have over 100 songs that I could make into full tracks and I just never do. I just can’t move forward on them until I perfect the beginning of the song. And for me that just never happens.

Please, I repeat, please help me. I haven’t uploaded a normal, non-remix or collab single (Enero doesn’t count because I had a time limit to upload that, end of January) since Invigorate, 2 months ago. This case of the perfectionisms could ruin my Soundcloud. I have had 32 plays in the last week, because I have so little new content.

I need help.

Just a word of advice (which is perhaps a little hypocritical seeing as though I release quite infrequently also)

Mr_Mooo:

I guess I’m worried that when I upload it I’ll get 50 comments saying ‘oh this is so bad you could’ve improved this’ and ‘meh it’s ok it would be so much better if you improved this’.

You gotta remember though: feedback, especially when it’s constructive, is so much more valuable than likes. Wanting to become popular without confidence in your product isn’t going to work. By putting yourself out there, you may cop nasty comments from time to time, but you will also gain a lot more knowledge than just keeping your music unreleased, which will skyrocket your confidence in later musical endeavours. Big names had to start somewhere; they probably released music under aliases such as ours in forums like Disco. ️
