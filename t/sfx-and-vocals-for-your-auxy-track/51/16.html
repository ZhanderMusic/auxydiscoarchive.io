We’re not supporting Audiobus because we don’t want to invest in the case where you’re running multiple apps at the same time to make music. BALLISTIK July 7, 2017, 6:15pm #26

To export into Logic Pro I usually, when exporting a project, tap the “export individual instruments” button, then share using airdrop. On my Mac I then open the file, and put it in Finder. After that I will go into logic pro, create as many audio tracks as I need, and then drag the audio files one by one from Finder into Logic Pro. An alternative that I have not tried might be using iCloud Drive to accomplish the transfer from the iOS device to computer, but when you’re uploading projects that have many tracks, that can be a lot of data to manage wirelessly.
