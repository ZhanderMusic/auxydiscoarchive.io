Featured Auxy Artist / Janitor #13: @xavior

IVO is a Germany-based artist who loves Moombahton and Minimal Techno. His track Civil Disobedience was apparently the first track ever featured within the Auxy App. IVO later took on Southborne in the popular battle-track Even Louder and hosted Beat the Clock #20 with the “Heavy Drop” challenge. More recently, he released his album Structure which took several months to produce. He uses his own photographs and artwork on covers and is a janitor (moderator) for this forum, while also doing his studies.

What’s IVO stand for?
Doesn’t stand for anything really

Describe your sound?
I’ve found three key elements in the majority of my tracks that make up my signature sound: A slow tempo, a prominent bass line, and piano.
