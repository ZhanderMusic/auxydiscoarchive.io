Introducing AUXTOBER

Contest & Challenges
RazerFireRiot October 1, 2017, 1:57pm #1

October is here!! And this morning I came up with a great idea!
I call it: AUXTOBER: The month for making a lot of music.
Basically, you make a long or short track or beat every day starting today till Halloween, making small track like in Beat The Clock, except you have the whole day everyday this month to work on that small track.
This isn’t a contest, and you don’t have to participate, but it’s a fun thing we should do, and we can even improve our skills too!

Be sure to post the project on here and SoundCloud, and make sure to make a playlist for your AUXTOBER tracks!

Let’s make some music!

Bonus: If you could make a spoopy tune, that would be even better because well, it’s October amirite?

Quick note: When you post it to SoundCloud, make sure to put the hashtag #Auxtober in your tags in the SoundCloud description so others can see your daily/weekly work!
