TheRealJFalc March 30, 2019, 8:03am #23

Alright, so after waking up to diahreah in the middle of the night I finally can respond to this. (Yay me)

Trap drumpack - we already have a huge 700-sample Transcendent Trap 2 soundpack with hundreds of claps, snares, kicks and whatnot. The problem with 808 kicks is that it’s highly unlikely that they will be pitched, or at the very least be given a key to the end user, as that unsimplifies things significantly. All regular kicks in the Leviathan 2 pack pitch to key, and many of them fly the sub-kick route. The problem with huge soundpacks and the sound library as is,
Is that we already have plenty of FX-like sounds in packs such as Beats from the Bass Ship 2 and Superfluidity, it’s just that they’re buried among 6000+ other sounds, making it extra difficult to find anything. Hopefully in the future, a better way to organize samples can be introduced.

Trap soundpack - aside from something like an oriental pack, I doubt we’d be seeing much here. A lot of sounds already work perfectly for Trap, as the Auxy Meta during Auxy 4 clearly demonstrated. We already have one horn pack, and although they didn’t work well for trap I doubt we’d be getting anything more without more time passing.
