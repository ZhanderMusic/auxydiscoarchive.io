akabillposters January 29, 2018, 12:57pm #29
dzvmvn:

Jazz, and every type of jazz has a difficult harmony. It’s very hard to do this with Auxy.

But, not impossible. And not all forms of jazz use complex harmonies.
Jazz is as much about flow, sensibility and rhythm as it is about harmony.

Don’t get me wrong, being a fan and regular listener of Bebop/Hard jazz (Coltrane, early Davis, Monk, Art Blakey, etc…) I love a good jazz chord sequence. Those harmonic progressions are characteristic to jazz, possibly unique to jazz, but they’re not the only thing that defines whether something falls inside ‘jazz’ or not.

So, there’s plenty to take from jazz without it having to be based on complex harmonic progressions.
