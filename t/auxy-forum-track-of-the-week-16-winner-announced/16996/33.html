Here’s my thoughts…

“Track of the Week” is designed to acknowledge the best track release of the week. So asking people to take a break so others can win feels wrong. We’re trying to encourage the best tracks possible. If one person is consistently producing the best tracks each week, I hope they keep entering and winning. And attract other good artists to compete. Kinda like evolution. The music gets better.

The alternative is that the best artist stops entering to be polite and we end up with lesser tracks winning. And that’s not the goal.
