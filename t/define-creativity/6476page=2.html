TheRealJFalc:

Creative music is music that pushes your boundaries personally. Such a thing is so subjective it’s kinda silly to try and describe it.

I guess this speaks to the difference between ‘creative’ and ‘original’.

Creativity can lead to something that’s only ‘new to you’ (subjectively new) or something closer to objectively new (i.e. original). ‘New to you’ is a great place to start (and often ends there, even for most creative people). ‘Objectively new’ is the common aspiration.

(I’m of the view that ‘original’ has more objective parameters. ‘Truly original’ is close to impossible. These days, when people say something is ‘original’, they’re effectively referring to it being ‘original enough’.
