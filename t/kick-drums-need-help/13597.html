TheRealJFalc January 11, 2019, 10:49pm #7

Tuning the kick is important.

For the record, all the OG kicks tune to the project’s preset key, along with all the kicks in Leviathan 2 (aside from the 808 kicks). Anything else you have to do manually, which is tricky as Auxy doesn’t natively display the key of samples. But as a rule of thumb, a good way to check is to just stick with one of the leviathan 2 kicks as they do tune. (The snares do the same as well)

An oldschool techno trick is to mix a short kick with a softer longer kick and swap out one or the other in parts of the track where you want to change the energy of the tune without losing the kick.

I do if ALL the time to keep the tracks dynamic without much effort /double and triple up your kick and move between them.
