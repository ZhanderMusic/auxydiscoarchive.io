Intro
Verse 1
Buildup
Drop 1
Bridge
Verse 2
Buildup
Drop 2
Outro

I (usually) create in this order: Make the drops, then the buildups, then the verses, then intro and outro last. But really, compose in whatever order you’re most comfortable.

If you have vocals, put them in the Verses/Intro/Outro

Good luck!

Try making a song 100% inspired from one you really like whether it be your own or not, and when you get to a certain point, drop that 100% inspiration and trail off.
