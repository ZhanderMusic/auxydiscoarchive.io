Another thing you have to think about when choosing a name:

Is the name original, but is it cool?

You don’t want to pick a name that you will be embarrassed about when you’re 30.

If you’re looking to be one of those “model producer/DJs,” then pick a name like “Calvin Harris,” “Nina Kraviz,” “Mark Knight,” “Michael Calfan” or “Nick Elle” ().
