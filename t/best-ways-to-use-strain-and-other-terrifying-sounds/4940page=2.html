tornait December 11, 2017, 6:33am #34

Don’t know if anybody else said this already, but Strain is good for a layered supersaw, especially with Otter and Jelly. Strain can also be used well with Gossip in leads. For Bumble, I sometimes use it for a contrabass, or to give a lead some low end. Castle is a nice plucky sound. Good for plucky chords along with Igloo and Climb, also good as a pseudo-organ in the lower registers. Finally… Secret. Secret basically has no more use by itself, unless you like corny, novel honky-tonk pianos… actually, try it layered with Grain or Chalk or even Tourist and Transfer (lowpass recommended for layering with the two in Vintage Keys). And Secret is also pretty okay as a weird pad sound, especially with lowpass.
