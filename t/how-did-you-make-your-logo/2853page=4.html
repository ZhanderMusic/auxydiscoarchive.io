This discussion got me thinking and so I just made myself one. It uses a picture I took of a steel ball floating in mercury and a picture of the mathematical symbol. I found the second picture on the Internet. While in gimp, I erased the extra text, scaled the image up, and erased the fancy L. Next, I layered this image on top of the mercury one, used various tools to make the L look less pixel-y, and messed with the top layer’s opacity until I got something I wanted.
