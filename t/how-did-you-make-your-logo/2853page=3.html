I changed mine up!
So, first, I went on Windows Paint (Yes, Windows PAINT), and drew what I called The N3xus Symbol. Next I drew a square around it. After that, I shoved it through a img to SVG converter, which vectorized the image to make it look smooth. I went on to my trusty BeFunky editor and applied a couple filters and done, the perfect new and original RazerFireRiot logo, made all from scratch!
