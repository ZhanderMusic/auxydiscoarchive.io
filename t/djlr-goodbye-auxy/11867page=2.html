TheRealJFalc August 24, 2018, 4:00pm #39

As someone who primarily uses FL Studio but still keeps chugging along using Auxy for ease of writing actual ideas, I’d strongly recommend trying to find a workflow that involves Auxy as well. For me, I find it easier to make ideas when I’m limited, not having to worry about tens of hundreds of features and quirks that FL Studio has as a DAW. After sketching out my idea, I use the export to midi option as well as exporting individual instruments, as some of Auxy’s sound design is the type of sound I prefer my music to have in most situations. I find this much easier than attempting to write from scratch in FL, as things tend to get messy fast.

As for VST plugins, all you really need is one as I’ve found, as simplicity helps projects flow easier for some people. I’d recommend Serum over any other VST, but any would do, as you can create anything with enough patience and creativity.

Definitely get the entire Valhala reverb family, not just Room. The plate reverb is especially juicy.

Good luck in your future endeavors.
