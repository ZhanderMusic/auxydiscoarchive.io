DJ_La_Rocca August 24, 2018, 5:05am #1

Hey guys, today I’d like to make a quick goodbye and thank you speech.

Dear Auxy, thank you for having me be apart of your app and a member of the community. Without Auxy, I would have never picked up music or even been interested in creating it. I had always been musical, but until I discovered Auxy (by accident in 2014 November (when it first launched) ), I never knew I had these capabilities of creating music or having virtuosic musical skills. It has opened up a H U G E pathway for me and I have learned the basics of structuring a song and putting together my ideas into making full tracks. However, I have always struggled to be able to implement my full ideas into my music due to the limitations and restrictions that Auxy does not offer.

From the day I used Auxy, I have never been taught anything and I have never wanted to learn anything externally or from anyone else. I started off with making random note placements and having no idea what so ever of music composition or production. I have picked up everything on my own and although it took longer for me to become better at music, it was worth it. Because teaching myself and not learning from anyone else has boosted my innovative ideas and allowed me to be unique from the rest.

Thank you for letting me start somewhere and building a small crowd to get me started off into the world of music.

Keep opening up pathways of upcoming musicians/producers. You certainly done a great job with me and I’m so thankful for everything Auxy has offered.

Unfortunately my journey with Auxy of almost 4 years has come to an end, as my dream of owning FL Studio has finally come, and now the possibilities and options I have with fl studio will take me to new places and hopefully I’ll be able to live the dream I’ve always dreamed since the beginning of my music creation journey. I dream to be famous and stand in front of 10,000 people playing my music to everyone who loves me. And I know I’ll get there because I believe in myself, and I am not a quitter. I know I can do this and I will prove to everyone that music is no fun and games to me. I am determined to fulfill my dreams and by succeeding and putting effort in I improve each time, and with the capabilities I have, in sure it’s possible.

Some quick advice before I go:

Live the dream you want to live, whether it’s music, writing books, helping animals whatever. If you put effort, time and practice into something, you will achieve things all the time. Maybe not straight away but everyone picks up things at different paces. For me, I’m a fast learner. I hope you all get to live your dream one day and I know you can do it! Never give up because the adventures and gateways life offers are amazing, and if you don’t put anything into it, you’ll be left in the dark. You gotta work your way up the ladder.

I’d also like to thank all my fans who have supported me from day 1. Without any of you, I wouldn’t have gotten as far as I have today. As I said, it took me longer than most of you to get recognised. I started in November 2014 and only got noticed by September of 2017. So thank you to all my fans who support me and my music.

Some auxians I’d like to thank:

@Eswyn @Xiejra @HELIXX @Matik . Thank you for being supportive of me and my music and creating friendships with me.

This has been a great journey, and I can’t wait to start a new one with FL Studio.

Thanks for having me Auxy.
