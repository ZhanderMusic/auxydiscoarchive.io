Featured Auxy Artist #18: @Andrew_Adams

I’m quietly pleased that Andrew Adams has been randomly selected this week, as he seems like a real nice guy, who’s always popping in to support others.

Andrew plays , some guitar & and he sings. He also excels at Auxy music competitions.

  • In the Auxy Forum Awards, Andrew was shortlisted for Producer of the Year (2017).

  • He won the 2nd Auxy Tournament to claim a and is currently co-hosting the 3rd Tournament.

  • Andrew short-listed for BTC 16. He short-listed for BTC 17. And he totally won BTC 23. (I stopped checking BTC’s at that point!!)

Andrew hosted Beat the Clock #22 - challenging us to put emotion into our music, based on two pictures (Freedom and Loneliness). Remember that?

He’s been reposted by Auxy and delivered collabs with peeps like Southborne, Voltt, Morgen, Horiz3n, intrepid, blackøcean and Just !n Cayz in recent months.

Enjoy a delicious taste of Andrew Adams this week.

Week starting: 30 Apr, 2018.

  • Please listen to at least one of their tracks (or more)
  • Provide at least one Soundcloud comment / feedback about their music during their week.
  • Comment in this thread once you’ve provided any feedback.
  • If you want to be featured, remember to comment for others!

Check out the tracks here: https://soundcloud.com/user-andrewadams/tracks

If you want to be in the weekly draw add your name.
