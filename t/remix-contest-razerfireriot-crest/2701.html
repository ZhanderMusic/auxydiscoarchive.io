Remix Contest: RazerFireRiot Crest

Contest & Challenges
RazerFireRiot September 23, 2017, 11:00pm #1

Crest is a song I released on SoundCloud in Late July this year. So I thought that as part of reaching past 50 views, I’d release an album full of all the remixes you guys make!

Here’s the rules though!

-Keep the scale if you’d like, but you can change it anyway you want! I also recommend keeping it at minor, and if you want, make it chromatic!

-The BPM range is between 100-140 BPM

-Don’t attempt to change the chords for Bo, Coin, Atom and Glow TOO much, because those are the core parts of the song!

-I don’t recommend swing!

-Be sure to try to make the song in formats like this:
RazerFireRiot - Crest (YourNameHere Remix) (something like that, because people might wanna listen to the original!)

-You can alter the drum beat as much as you want

-You can add as any instruments as you want (but not too much! Organization will be the best option if this get messy, it make production faster!)

-Try not to exceed 5 minutes length

-You can post the song yourself on SoundCloud, and I’ll put it in the Crest Remix album, or you can send me your project so I’ll render it, and post it to Auxy with your name on the Remix

-The date that this contest will close will be sometime around mid October, final date will be decided maybe next week.

-5 of the best remixes will be featured in the album.

Have fun, make those beats, slip on the headphones and remix away!
