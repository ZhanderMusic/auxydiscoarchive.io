Why are you reposting tracks with your SoundCloud artist profiles?

Branding & Distribution
Auxy April 9, 2019, 6:33am #1

Hey,

I use the stream view on SoundCloud to find new tracks that you guys post. Have noticed that some people repost a lot of tracks from better known artists. Not sure whether this makes sense or not? I’m thinking that you either build a profile as a curator or an artist. Not both. If people follow you to hear your tracks, they don’t necessarily want to hear a lot of other stuff that you happen to like. And thus you run the risk of scaring off some of your followers.

If you want to curate favorite tracks, it might be better to put these in playlists rather than reposting?

Just some food for thought…
