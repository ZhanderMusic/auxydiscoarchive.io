could i please make something clear?

there are two types of labels people are talking about here, and most aren’t specifying which:

small labels created by auxy members (but not auxy-only) and professional labels from outside of the community.

examples:
frontier, vvvvv, melo;
preconceived notions, future vibes, cosmic superior, lunch box, destinuna, phuture collective, night owl collective, delimited, ridgeline, etc.

second: yes, i agree, some labels started by people here don’t get a lot of promo compared to others. so what? frontier gets an average of about 500 which is definitely something to aim to for about half the people here. it means frontier can search for underrated and talented submissions that they can release to their page.

members of the community here for some reason seem to not be liking labels, and I’m not sure i understand why… i think it’s a misunderstanding.

they’re stepping stones to get accepted into bigger labels. trust me, doing that is a process; reverie was in the making for a month and a half and took another month to make edits to get released.
