Dear, Auxy community:

Today, I wish to share with you my biggest project to date… and it’s not another track. This time, I’m posting and promoting a charity campaign that I’ve been developing for a while now. In this post, I will share with you some backstory for this cause and why it is important to me personally, and then I will tell you how you can participate and show that the Auxy community is not just about making music, but also about making a difference.

SOME BACKGROUND:

A little more than a year ago, I met one of the greatest friends of my life. She has changed my life in ways I can’t even begin to explain, and as she has told me, I have changed hers. What you should know, however, is that she experienced a rather afflicted childhood (i.e. abuse of various kinds, a paralyzed mother, starvation, and countless, lengthy hospital stays for several health concerns), all of this while suffering from a lesser known form of bipolar disorder that causes extreme and, often times, long-lasting depression - most of the time without telltale signs or warning. Because of this, there were points in her life when she felt that the only option left was to end it.

Since I met her, things have turned around rather dramatically and she proudly tells her story of perseverance and determination to live on and make change in the world. The two of us are inseparable (so much so, in fact, that we have tattoos to prove it).

Now, I want to do something more. I know that so many other people did not feel they had the strength to keep fighting, given their severely unfortunate circumstances. Because of this, I want us to show people facing a similar dilemma that there is, indeed, a “brighter side.”

This song that I wrote and produced, The Lighter Side (feat. Tehillah Henry), was made for my friend. However, I feel it may have significance to many others alike.

THE MISSION:

Music moves us in some of the most unexpected ways. So, how can one song make such a difference for so many lives?

  1. Buy the song. The track is a mere $0.99 on iTunes. And every penny spent will go straight to the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention. You can find their website here, where you can find more information about their mission, events, and the impact they’ve made already.
  2. Share the campaign poster (above) and let others know about this campaign.
  3. Lastly, feel free to provide feedback! Perhaps there is another nonprofit organization with a similar cause that you feel could benefit from some attention here within our community? Don’t hesitate to grow this campaign and do more to help prevent suicide.

I give my sincere thanks to all those who have read this far and who wish to participate. I really hope this will help the AFSP and, ultimately, those who really need our help.

Let us show them “the lighter side!”

IVO
