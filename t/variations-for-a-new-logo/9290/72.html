  1. It was the seed of an idea, not a fully worked up, final visual. Zero refinement.
  2. Context would do a lot to establish it as a logo. E.g. if it was used consistently on cover art and profile pics, people would understand very quickly that it’s part of a visual identity. If it’s often seen with the words Osharu Perez, people will understand more readily that it’s an OP, though if the name is there, it only needs to be understood as a motif related to Osharu Perez. If some viewers never ‘see’ the OP, it’s not catastrophic.
