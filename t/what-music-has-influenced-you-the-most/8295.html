AzureOnyx October 27, 2020, 4:15am #19

Back when I was in 8th grade, around 2015ish, I was heavily influenced by trance producers from the Anjunabeats label, most notably Above & Beyond. This was around the time where I started to produce music instead of loops, and also the reason why I have a habit of producing music that’s 4+ minutes long, since trance music tended to be that long.

A&B’s album Group Therapy really helped me through this time since it was also the “darkest” time in all of my life to date. The theming of this album really struck with loss, darkness, melancholy, etc, which really set a theme of which I produced music at that time.

Other producers like Gareth Emery and Armin van Burren are also huge influential artists for me due to their earlier production.

Drum & Bass producers like Matrix & Futurebound, Netsky, Camo & Krooked, just to name a few also inspired me to make faster paced music. If you didn’t know, I only produce Trance and D&B to this day, and it was due to the genres I was into at the time, though, that kind of shifted through the years.

If we’re talking about the Auxy community, some Beat the Clock producers have really influenced me in a way where I can listen to their music freely. Some producers include @viiitality, Intepid, @simon2, just to name a few, though, these artists are no longer active within the Auxy community.
