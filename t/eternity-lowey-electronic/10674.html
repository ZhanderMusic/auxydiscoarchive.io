lowey June 21, 2018, 1:16pm #19
anon86704580:

I feel like I’m in Minecraft 2. Lol JK. But it does remind me of C418 a LOT. Bits and pieces at least. Man, its so atmospheric and chill

When I first started playing Minecraft there were moments where I thought “who the hell is this, their music sounds just like mine!” That was so long ago though, musically I’ve grown a lot since then and broadened my style.

Another artist that I feel I have something in common with is Blue States, particularly their earlier stuff. But the stuff I’ve been doing with Auxy is quite different to the stuff I was doing in the 2000s. I discovered Blue States entirely by accident and fell in love with their stuff, but found myself at times being slightly weirded out because I could hear ideas, styles and even phrases from my existing tracks in their work. Not suggesting they’d stolen anything from me — a complete unknown — just a weird coincidence

This has got me thinking, I might even dig some of my ancient songs out — I must have their MP3 files somewhere — and upload them. Most of them make me cringe, but you’ve got to know where you came from to know where you’re going (or some other such platitude).
