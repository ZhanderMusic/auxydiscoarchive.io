akabillposters May 31, 2018, 4:25am #17

You get a link in the description.

I don’t understand why you think that’s enough.
(It isn’t. Especially when you’ve got 46 followers and your original music has yet to get more than 100 plays.)

 
What you’re describing is a co-producer/co-production.

A ‘featuring’ credit or a direct co-credit are more appropriate than a mention/link in the SC description.
(…unless you’re going to pay them as well, in which case being listed as a co-producer in the track description would be fine.)

e.g.
Aviomusic - [Track Name] feat. [Artist 2/beatmaker]*
Aviomusic & [Artist 2/beatmaker] - [Track Name]

If it goes up on any monetised channels, streaming services, Apple Music, Google Play, etc… there should also be a revenue split.

* Although this one could potentially be confusing as ‘feat.’ normally refers to a guest vocalist.

 
Of course, if someone wants to do it for crumbs a mention in the SC description, that’s up to them.
But, it’s disrespectful to this community and all other music makers to make that the ‘offer’.
