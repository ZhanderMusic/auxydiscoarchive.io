It depends. If you want Logic, Ableton, Cubase, Reason, or any other professional daw, get a mac (and like other people are saying, get a big one with lots of space because they run out fast).

If you want Ableton, LMMS, FL Studio or Magix Music Making Software / MMMS (ew, I used to use MMMS, it sucks) then get a Windows.

If you want LMMS (and nothing else as far as I know) get a Linux. Except dont get a Linux, you really cant do anything on them except coding.

When getting a new computer, my motto is always
“Apple for music, Windows for Games, and Linux for coding”
