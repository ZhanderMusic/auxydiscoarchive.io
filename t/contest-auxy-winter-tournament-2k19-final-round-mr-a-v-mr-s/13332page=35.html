This is it. The end of the road. Huge congratulations to everybody who has been in the tournament for your commitment. And major congrats to Mr. A and Serp for making it so far.

But there can only be one winner.

The Challenge

This challenge comes from BTG, who proposed you each make an awesome track, but not something really finished. You have until March 24th by 5:00 PM Pacific Time to (semi-)complete it. Then, in a twist of fate, you swap project files and you have until March 31st at 5:00 PM Pacific Time to complete.

Only the demo sounds, original soundpack, and the first four APs can be used.

In the words of BTG: we will “judge based on how far in development the original projects were, how much each artist transformed the project (it has to still be recognizable tho), and, you know, just how awesome the finished product is.”

Something like that, at least.

No time limit this round. Hoorary! Go all out.

Final submissions are due by Sunday, March 31st, 5:00 PM Pacific Time. Post a SoundCloud link here. We will not accept private messages/private links. We also need to hear the original track your opponent had made you expanded upon.

The Competitors:
