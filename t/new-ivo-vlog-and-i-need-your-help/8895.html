Hello fellow Auxians!

As it has been recommended to me by quite a few of my producer friends and even Lenberg himself, I’ve finally come to the decision to start a new YouTube channel for exclusive new music, my production tips and processes, and other music-and-Auxy-related commentary!

For the very first ‘episode’, I want to answer even more questions from YOU! I want both producers and listeners alike to get to know me better as an artist and as just any other human being. (I’m shamelessly and famously a loon, so why not shine the spotlight on some more of that, shall we?)

I want YOU to ask ME anything and everything. It can be personal, it can be about my music, it can be about Auxy, or it can be a stupid question and I’ll probably reciprocate with a stupid answer. Your choice. (Just comment your question and what you want to be called down below.)

I’m very excited and nervous to do this as being some Auxy Internet personality was never really at the forefront of my music goals until now… so here goes nothing! I want to make this as fun, helpful, and original as possible.

Cheers and love

IVO
