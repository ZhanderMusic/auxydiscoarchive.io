So this almost keeled me around the holidays, but since I’m finally at home and have had a rest, I decided to get this out of the way.

I made a bunch of tracks experimenting with pitch, and the best ones were all cheerful sounding. So turns out you can get a couple of cool sounds playing with pitch, other than just a rise. I managed to even get a “car changing gears” sound when playing with deep bass instruments. But the best sound I got was out of Lake (RAC Essentials). If you take away the clang with low pass, and then play with the pitch, you can get a cheery whistle going.
