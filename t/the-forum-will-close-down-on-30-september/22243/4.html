The forum will close down on 30 September

Hey all,

It’s been a good run but we’ve decided to close down this forum at the end of the month, i.e. in two weeks from today. Forum usage has declined over time and the community has moved to Discord and other platforms, with only a handful of people still active here.

Please note that no forum posts will be available after the closing date, and make sure to check out the following post if you want to connect with other users in other places:

Auxy Discord Directory [for chatty chatty] Production Talk
There’s been a bunch of Discord Servers created for chatting over recent months, but it hasn’t always been easy to find the details and they don’t always stay active. If you have an active Discord server, click the EDIT button on this post and add it to the list. If you haven’t been here long enough to see the Edit button, then reply in this thread with the details, asking someone to add it to the list for you. > DISCORD DIRECTORY: The Auxy Collective Run by Arimyth, Hum4n01d, Ianardo, ToxicC…

Thanks for all your contributions and hope you enjoy music making more than ever!

/ Team Auxy
