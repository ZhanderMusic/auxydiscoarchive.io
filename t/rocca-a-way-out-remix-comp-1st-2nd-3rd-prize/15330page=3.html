NickElle May 9, 2019, 4:02am #45

Ah, okay.
Well, when engineers refer to “sub” they usually mean sub bass frequencies, not really the sub bass synth.
All basses have sub, so most basses should be mono (there are exceptions, like when your bass synth or guitar has high mid frequencies, then you can have those be stereo, but the low frequencies must still be in mono)

Also, I didn’t mean to criticize.
