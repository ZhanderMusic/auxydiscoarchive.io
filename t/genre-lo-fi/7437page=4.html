We did get a little off-topic…
As far as free options, I think Garageband is a great choice.

You could try any of:

  • The inbuilt bitcrusher.
  • The inbuilt distortion.
  • Timestretch a loop to double/half it’s tempo, bounce and timestretch back to the original tempo.
  • Roll off a little of the top end with a low-pass filter.
  • Over compress a sound with very fast attack and release times.
  • Push the faders into the red and bounce down.
