It’s true horror, and anyone who sees the Fast Spider, will be haunted from dusk to dawn. Your dreams will be stolen by nightmares. These nightmares, speak to people. They are enough to make people go insane.

There’s only 1 way to avoid Fast Spider, you have to talk to his cousin, Slow Snail. Only he knows how to stay away from the haunting Fast Spider. To talk to him, though, is a real, threatening adventure.

Before that, you need to call Gangster Giraffe and set an appointment. You can find his number posted on the bulletin in 160 Giraffe Avenue. Call him, and he will give you a secret destination to meet him. Make sure you’re not being followed. When you meet him, he will blindfold you and walk you about a mile away, and help you get into an underground hideout. He’ll unblind you and you guys will have a seemingly normal conversation. What he really is doing, is taking in all of your answers and evaluating whether you’re worthy or not for the next stage.

If you are worthy, he will tuck you into a boat, and float you down a very slim river, through a very thin cave. This is NOT for the claustrophobic.

10 minutes in, you will pass out from who-knows-what. And you will wake up, strategically, right when you arrive at your destination 40 minutes later. You will be in the basement of someone’s home. You will walk up stairs and yell “skoopidooblidoo.” Slow Snail will then appear out of thin air, in front of your very eyes.

He will be glad to see you, and knows what you are there for. He shows you his secret to not being haunted by Fast Spider. All it is is chocolate chip ice cream. Just eat it and you’re good, homie.
