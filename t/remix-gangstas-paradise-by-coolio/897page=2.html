Hey, @LaLa. Some interesting stuff going on.
Must say though, maybe it’s my old ears, but I’m struggling to hear the sonic connection to Gangsta’s Paradise.

I hear hints of references, like the use of string stabs on the 4/4, but given that you’ve changed the chords, I feel you’ve lost the connection to the track you’re supposedly remixing.
I’m not hearing any recognisable motifs that ties it back to Gangsta’s Paradise.

I also think the Bow instrument might benefit from being brought down in the mix a little, or maybe just take the edge of and soften it off a little by dropping the LowPass down a touch.
Using it as you have, it cuts through the mix a little too much, imo.

I think there’s some really good stuff happening underneath, but it currently feels like it hasn’t really fully come together in its current form.

But, as said, maybe just my old ears.
