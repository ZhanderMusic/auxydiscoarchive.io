Lots of what I see here (and elsewhere in the forum where logos are discussed) are not logos. Not logotype, not logo symbols, wordmarks, none that constitute a logo.

A logo needs to work in all kinds of scenarios. Small size. Large size. In color. In black & white. And grayscale.

I highly recommend going on Amazon, and ordering a good book or two on logo design.

One of my favorites is a slim but solid one called Logo Design Love by David Airey.

A clear visual identity is worth having done, or learning to do yourself.
