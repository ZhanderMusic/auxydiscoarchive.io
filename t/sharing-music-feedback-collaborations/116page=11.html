If you wanna make perfect chords you can use the music theory look at this two images

image.jpeg985×747 130 KB

image.jpeg1024×920 123 KB

In the fisrt one you have every chord for every key ( on the left) for example if we take B, you can use C# Minor chord, E Major chord … If you don’t know how to write these chords you can look at the second picture. Sometimes you may notice that some chords aren’t in the chart, but you have to know that:
C# is also Db
Eb is also D#
F# is also Gb and
Bb is also A#
(Maj7,min7,sus1 and sus2 should not matter to you yet)

This method will take you more time, but you can’t go wrong with it! (I forget to say that you have to use the chromatic mode)
Hope to have helped you
