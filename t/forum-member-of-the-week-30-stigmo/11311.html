Featured Auxy Artist #30: @Stigmo

Stigmo is from the Netherlands , where he sometimes drives his mothers purple Fiat Panda . He played for a professional football team when he was 13/14 and he likes beer . Hopefully not while driving his mothers purple Fiat Panda.

He’s also into DJ’ing and performs live DJ sets.

“I usually play for 50-100 people, but I’ve done sets in a club for 500-600 people as well”

“Generally speaking I like house and trap, but I like anything good really”

Stigmo always encourages other forum members and provides feedback for the Auxy featured artists, so let’s show him some support. You can listen just over . . . . . . . . . . . HERE!

Week starting: 23 July, 2018.

  • Please listen to at least one of his tracks (or more)
  • Provide at least one Soundcloud comment / feedback about his music during their week.
  • Comment in this thread once you’ve provided any feedback.
  • If you want to be featured, remember to comment for others!

Check out the tracks here: https://soundcloud.com/stigmomusic/tracks

If you want to be in the weekly draw add your name.
