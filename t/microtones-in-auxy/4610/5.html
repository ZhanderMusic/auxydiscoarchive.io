wagneric November 29, 2017, 8:24pm #2

Pitch automation snaps to half-tones, unfortunately, but I find that if you do, for example, a half-step over a long duration (8 bars), and experiment a bit, you can come close to what you want. It’s pretty fun to try to write a melody while the pitch is sliding.
