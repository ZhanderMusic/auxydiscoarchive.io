Posting to the disco and to discord promotional channels is a small part of it.

  1. Do repost trades
  2. Build a legitimate and engaged follower base
  3. Find music groups on Facebook or Reddit to share to
  4. Submit your song to repost networks on SoundCloud
  5. Don’t stress about it more than doing those 4 things and enjoy music without getting caught up in listening stats
