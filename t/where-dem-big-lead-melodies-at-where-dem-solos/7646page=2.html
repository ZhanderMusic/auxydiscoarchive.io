akabillposters February 17, 2018, 1:28pm #40
Shepdoge:

I really like funky basslines

But, with swing at 10, imo, you’ve gone straight pass ‘funky’ and entered ‘jaunty and fun’ territory, losing any ‘cool factor’.

If you’re rolling with high levels of syncopation, then it’s also better to layer it with elements that are less syncopated. If it’s all highly swung (and all instruments are perfectly in time with each other), it just sounds inauthentic.

And keep in mind that there’s more to ‘funky’ than syncopation. Simply loading up on syncopation doesn’t automatically make something funky. It just makes it bouncy.
