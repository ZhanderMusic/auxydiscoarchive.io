It’s not possible as of yet to have multiple syncopations within Auxy

There’s a fair amount of wiggle room if you use a modest swing setting, and then mix in 1/12, 1/16, 1/24 and 1/32 quantisations. Add the Offset to that (for drums and samples, including tonal samples) and you’re not stuck to one swing feel.

swing.png800×204 16 KB

(I wouldn’t recommend using more than two or three timings at once, unless you’re going for that sloppy, drunken, broken beat style.)
