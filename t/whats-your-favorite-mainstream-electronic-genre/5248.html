House isn’t "EDM."
Electro House, Future House, Progressive House are EDM, but you can’t lump ALL of House into EDM.
Take Deep House and Tech House. You wouldn’t play those genres on a MainStage at an EDM Festival, because they aren’t about “the drop,” but you would play them in a dark club because they are about the groove.
