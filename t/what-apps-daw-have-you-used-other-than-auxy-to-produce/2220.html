akabillposters September 10, 2017, 8:22am #6

Ableton Live + a smattering of hardware, though I’ve not stepped outside of Auxy for a few weeks.
It’s strength for me is sketching, getting ideas down quickly and getting them reasonably well fleshed out.

(I still consider everything I’ve produced in Auxy as unfinished/WIP – a decent, representative ‘demo’.
I’m personally not comfortable calling something made in Auxy ‘release ready’. Stuff just seems to lack certain dimensions, to my ears.

I do ‘plan’ to bring projects/stems/midi into Live at some point, to explore layers and edits that can’t be achieved in Auxy. Adding nuance to the ‘performance’.

But then, I’m also happy/keen to have someone (producer, engineer) give specific pieces a proper listen and either help rebuild it outside of Auxy, or to at least help with the mix-down and mastering, to make the tracks ‘finished’ and ‘release-ready’, imo.)
