I’ve done some research on graphic design. There’s a facet of it known as “gestalt psychology”. Simply put, gestalt psychology is how some logos ( especially ones that use lots of negative space, and ones that use multiple shapes to create one collective shape ) are visually pleasing because our mind fills in the gaps; that’s what I’m trying to achieve with these logos.

And for the record; blue jays aren’t really that wholesome, they’re corvids— related to crows and ravens— and they’re more bullies than most people think.
