akabillposters January 10, 2018, 11:57am #9
blakkaz:

Here’s hoping the next big update will include midi in and velocity

I suspect velocity would be some time after adding additional note volume levels, and I’m not confident that’s coming any time soon.

‘Midi in’, whether live or imported, I imagine, would be even further down the roadmap.
Live could potentially use live quantisation, but imported could require a significant UI rethink. External midi generators/recorders typically have a much higher resolution than the Auxy app, so they’d have to address the quantisation issue that translates midi data of a much higher resolution down to 32 (maybe 64?).

Plus, I think there’s a bit of a conflict with the walled garden approach that the app’s following. I think Lenberg’s on record as saying he’s not interested in exploring that direction for the app for the foreseeable future (even though it was possible in the original version, iirc).
