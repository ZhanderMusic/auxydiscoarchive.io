It’s been a very long time coming, but I’ve finally gotten myself to a point where I feel confident enough in releasing something onto streaming services. Introducing Density, a rework playlist of a few songs into a higher quality format and fine-tuned for perfection. Compacted into a seventeen-minute listening experience, this tight release is quite dense and diverse, ranging from cinematic-inspired strings to breakbeat-influenced DnB x Future Bass Tri-hybridization. It’s a huge step forward for me, and I’m proud to present this all to you.

Thank you for taking the time to look at this, and I hope you all are as excited as I am for this moment.
