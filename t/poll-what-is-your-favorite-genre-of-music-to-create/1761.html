Andrew_Adams August 28, 2017, 5:08pm #4

Lots of transitions, and shorter scenes. Songs full of 8-bar scenes can kind of drag. Also, just making everything more dramatic in general can help (aka lots of builds, drops, and a combination of low volume and high volume). Also try to make the song addictive (in other words, always have the song going somewhere so the person can’t stop listening until the end.) Intrepid and BSJ specifically do this really well in my opinion.
