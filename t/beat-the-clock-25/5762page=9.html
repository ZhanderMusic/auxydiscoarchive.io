The longer and better version
My story again for those who didn’t see it: This tune is Heart and Soul by Hoagy Carmichael. This was a big part of my musical life when I was a child. I played piano, but I was always too scared to perform. My brother also loved to play piano, and he did his grade one piano and performed regularly. My piano teacher then taught me the basic left hand person of Heart and Soul. I practised it every night (I was only about 7 so it took me awhile to memorise) and then I looked up the other half of the duet. I told my brother I wanted to do a duet with him at the upcoming concert, which was a big step for me as I wasn’t as experienced with crowds as some of the other kids at the concert would be. So anyway, I showed my brother the sheet music and he practised with me every day. On the day of the concert I said I couldn’t do it but my brother convinced me and we did it without mistake in front of the whole school and some parents and teachers too. It was that performance which made me comfortable with performing in front of a crowd and it was that song that changed my life.
