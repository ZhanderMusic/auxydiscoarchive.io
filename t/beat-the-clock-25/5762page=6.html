Here’s mine.
https://soundcloud.com/caed-mitchell/auxy-o-beat-the-clock-25

️ WARNING ️ Depressing stuff below.

I got the feeling for this song when I was 5 years old in the hospital with my grandma as her kidney had failed and she was getting a transplant. Every thing went fine and was fine for about 2 weeks. But the. we had gotten a call from our grandpa that we need to go to the hospital as soon as we can. When we got there,(we being family) we had found out that she Is in the hospital for her new kidney rejecting her body and now causing total organ failure. And I remember standing at her bed side as she had about 32 hours to live. The next day at school she had passed away. And I remember the feeling of dread very clearly and was able to put that in to this song. And it’s why I named it oceans apart because I will see her again but I will have to take a long journey to get there.

True story.
