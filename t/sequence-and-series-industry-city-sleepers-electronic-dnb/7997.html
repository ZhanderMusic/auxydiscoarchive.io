Sequence and Series

Wrapping this up, a few months in the making. More an EP I guess not really a proper album. You’ve all heard these songs already, but I figured I’d try to give it a proper artwork treatment.

If anyone wants to remix I’m more than happy to provide the samples and axp files. I’ve also set all the songs to downloadable just because. Mostly because I wanted to be able to listen to them offline, but if anyone else wants to grab them too, it’s cool

You’ve probably already heard INITaux as well, I gave that album a proper cover (sort of proper) but nothing’s changed there. That’s all pre update Auxy 4 stuff (again, more than happy to share those project files for remixing and other abuse).

WHAT NEXT? I don’t know. This stuff has been fun but there’s other sonic areas I want to explore. I’m open to collabs if anyone can deal with how slow I can be. I figure I want to take a bit of time to collect my thoughts on the ICS stuff before starting anything there, but of course, I still want to work with music so if anyone feels like some broken beat, drum and bass, glitchy, clicky IDM type stuff, I am game.

Thanks for following along if you’re still here.
-Matt

Re-introducing “Sequence and Series” - also now on Spotify!

Spotify

Sequence and Series

Sequence and Series, an album by industry city sleepers on Spotify

I wasn’t entirely happy with the mix, and with the help and guidance of @akabillposters I settled on a nice pair of monitoring phones and spent a weekend tweaking and actively listening to all the tracks. Made my adjustments, piped everything through Grand Finale and replaced all the audio files.

It’s exciting to have this stuff on the iTunes store and Spotify, was always a personal goal of mine and it makes me happy to see it come to fruition

Artwork has been done again as well. I’m not super thrilled with it, admittedly, next time around with the knowledge I’ve gained around here I’m going to give a real proper treatment. I was anxious to get these re-mixed versions back up and into Amuse.

Anyway, enjoy! Stay tuned for what comes next, still working out where I want to head but kicking some ideas around and have a new iPad on the way so I’m sure that’ll be a game changer (been using an iPhone SE this whole time, yikes! My thumbs hurt!)
